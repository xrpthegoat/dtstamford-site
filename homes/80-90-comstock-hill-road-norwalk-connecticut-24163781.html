About this home

A rare opportunity to acquire Silvermine Farm, a fully integrated, professional-grade equestrian facility spanning two contiguous parcels at 80 &amp; 90 Comstock Hill. Set on 7.06 level acres in a highly desirable Fairfield County location, the property supports a high-caliber training, boarding, or private equestrian operation. The facility includes one indoor ring (75&apos; x 150&apos;) and two outdoor rings (200&apos; x 300&apos; and 150&apos; x 100&apos;) with premium GGT footing, a ~35-stall main barn complemented by an additional 4 stalls at 90 Comstock Hill, multiple tack rooms, a heated wash stall, and well-designed turnout paddocks. A hay barn, office space, staff apartments and two homes further support daily operations. The property supports a range of uses, including training, boarding, and organized riding programs, with the flexibility to expand into private boarding, lesson programs, or a more tailored owner-driven approach. Its layout, infrastructure, and scale allow for the opportunity to shape the next chapter. Please contact listing agents for Equine Fact Sheet
