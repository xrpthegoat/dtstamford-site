About this home

Designed by custom home builder Vita Design, this exciting newly constructed modern residence is a refined architectural statement that balances scale and light, capturing direct 5 Mile River views and a connection to its natural surroundings. Finished across four levels, the home features dramatic 10- and 16-foot ceilings and a main level wrapped in floor-to-ceiling European-style windows filling the interior with natural light, creating seamless indoor-outdoor living. All living spaces are designed for flexibility and function. The kitchen showcases an oversized center island, Leicht mahogany cabinetry, Miele appliances, huge pantry and spacious dining area overlooking the river. The primary suite offers a private retreat with balcony, water views, lux bath and generous closets. All other bedrooms feature en-suite baths with high-end, individual finishes. Lower-level and third-floor bedrooms provide privacy and adaptable use. Outdoor living centers around the covered wrap around patio and heated pool with retractable cover, enhancing the home&apos;s resort-like feel. Additional amenities include home automation, mudroom, laundry room, and a fully finished lower level with large recreation / home gym space. An oversized three-car garage completes the home. Minutes to world class Darien, Rowayton, Long Island Sound, and easy access to New York City &amp; New Haven by train or car. Coastal CT at its very best!
