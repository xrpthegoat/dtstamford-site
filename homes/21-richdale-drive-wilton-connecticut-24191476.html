About this home

Enjoy the privacy and space of single-family living in this well-maintained 3-bedroom, 2-bath rental approximately 1800 sq ft and just minutes from Wilton High School, town center, shopping, restaurants, and commuter routes. The home features hardwood floors in the bedrooms and family room, with durable laminate flooring throughout the remaining living spaces. The updated kitchen offers a center island and walk-in pantry, while the flexible living/dining room includes a wood-burning fireplace. Additional features include a private home office, a spacious mudroom with laundry hookups, abundant storage and closet space, and a deck overlooking expansive shared open green space. Ample driveway parking completes the offering.
