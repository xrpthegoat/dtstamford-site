About this home

Step inside to this spacious and well maintained 3BR, 3.5BA Townhome. On the main level, you will find a magnificent open concept living, Hardwood floors throughout, kitchen with stainless steel appliances, custom upper cabinets and wood paneling, and family room with fireplace and sliders that lead to a private and serene patio that is perfect for grilling with a unique rock feature and grassy area. The second floor boasts a primary bedroom with tray ceiling and an all marble en-suite bathroom, and large walk-in custom closet. There are two additional generously sized bedrooms also with custom closets and Jack &amp; Jill bathroom. The partially finished basement/lower level (297 square feet) can be used as an office or playroom and includes a full bathroom. The basement also has a large crawl space (463 square feet) with a concrete floor. Low monthly HOA fees ($385 per month) include insurance, landscaping and snow removal. The Condo is located in the West Rocks area close to I- 95 &amp; Merritt, schools, Calf Pasture Beach, restaurants, shops and The SoNo Collection Mall.
