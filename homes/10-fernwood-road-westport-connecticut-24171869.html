About this home

In-Town Living at its Finest! Tucked away at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac, this private 5-bedroom residence offers the perfect blend of space, comfort, and convenience on 1.5+ of beautifully landscaped acres. A sun-filled, custom eat-in kitchen serves as the heart of the home, featuring stainless steel appliances, a center island, and abundant natural light. The flexible floor plan is enhanced by hardwood floors throughout, allowing for effortless everyday living and entertaining. Step outside to a spacious deck overlooking lush lawns and meticulously maintained perennial gardens-professionally cared for by the landlord-creating a serene outdoor retreat. The home also features a generous in-law suite complete with its own fireplace, kitchen, and a multi purpose room ideal for extended living or guest accommodations. A walk-out lower level provides the perfect space for a game room or recreation area, while the finished third floor offers endless possibilities for a home office, playroom, or creative studio. Additional highlights include a newly paved driveway. A rare opportunity for privacy, space, and in-town convenience-this is not to be missed.
