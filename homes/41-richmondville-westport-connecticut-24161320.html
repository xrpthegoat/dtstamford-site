About this home

A new opportunity to own in The Mill, now completely sold out, this is the first resale opportunity within this highly sought-after community. This thoughtfully designed residence features floor-to-ceiling windows, beautiful European Oak floors, and a sleek linear gas fireplace anchoring the living space. The open-concept kitchen includes an oversized island, custom Poliform cabinetry, and high-end appliances-ideal for everyday living and entertaining. A smart split-bedroom layout offers privacy, with bedrooms on opposite sides of the home. The primary suite features two walk-in closets and a bath with dual vanities, heated floors and a rain shower. Enhanced beyond its original offering, this home now includes custom electronic shades, designer wall coverings, and added frosted glass for privacy. Upstairs, a nearly 600 sq ft private rooftop terrace extends your living space outdoors. Additional highlights include in-unit laundry, parking, and storage. Residents enjoy a landscaped courtyard with a 45&apos; lap pool and hot tub, grilling area, fitness center, Great Room, cafe, rooftop terrace, and concierge service six days a week.
