About this home

Rarely does an opportunity arise to own a home in the coastal enclave of Shorehaven, and 10 Shorehaven Road is a true masterpiece. This Nantucket-inspired estate blends timeless elegance with relaxed coastal charm, offering unmatched luxury and tranquility. Set along the serene waterfront, the lush, landscaped grounds feature a sparkling pool, spa, and private dock-perfect for boating adventures or peaceful moments by the tide. Inside, a grand curved staircase greets you, leading to beautifully appointed spaces. Formal living and dining rooms offer sophistication, while the gourmet chef&apos;s kitchen and adjoining family room, with a coffered ceiling, fireplace, and French doors, invite effortless indoor-outdoor living. Five ensuite bedrooms, including the second-floor primary suite provide private retreats. The primary features two spa-like baths and a balcony with breathtaking water views. A custom office with its own balcony and a richly paneled study complete the home&apos;s refined interiors. Expansive windows flood the home with natural light, while the veranda, spanning the length of the house, is perfect for entertaining or quiet relaxation. Minutes from Shorehaven Golf Club and local amenities, this property offers the pinnacle of coastal living.
