About this home

Charming well-maintained 1930 Colonial with 2-car garage on a walkable, tree-lined street in Norwalk. This 3BR/2 Full Bath home offers timeless character on three sun-filled floors. The main level features hardwood floors, an updated kitchen with gas stove, dining room, living room, family room, and a full bath. A spacious over-sized primary bedroom offers room to potentially create your dream ensuite bath &amp; walk-in closet. Enjoy year-round comfort on the enclosed front porch, plus a large rear deck overlooking a spacious yard w/garden. Full height basement ready to be finished for additional living space, home gym, office, or media room. A walk-up attic provides ample extra storage. Convenient to schools, transit, parks and shopping! Perfect for first-time buyers or investors ready to add value in a prime location!
