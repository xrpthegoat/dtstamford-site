Living in Springdale

Springdale has a small, village-style feel: single-family colonials and townhouses along quiet residential streets, anchored by the walkable Hope Street commercial strip of shops, bakeries, and restaurants. Having its own Metro-North station gives it a distinct, self-contained character within the city.

Springdale real estate & home prices

Housing is mostly single-family homes with some townhouses and condos, with typical values around the mid-$600Ks (estimated) depending on size and condition. Getting around & commuting

The Springdale station sits on the Metro-North New Canaan branch, connecting to the main line at Stamford for express service toward Grand Central. The Merritt Parkway is also close for drivers.
