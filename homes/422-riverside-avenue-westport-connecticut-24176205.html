About this home

A rare opportunity in the heart of Westport&apos;s coveted Saugatuck section. Set on 1.10 dry acres w/ beautiful views overlooking the Saugatuck River, this property known historically as Twin Hickory&apos;s offers a unique blend of history, charm, &amp; future potential. Rich in character &amp; dating back more than a century, the property traces its roots to the Franklin M. Raymond estate. Orig constructed as a barn in the late 1800s &amp; thoughtfully converted into a residence in the 1930s, the home &amp; accompanying barn remain a fascinating piece of Westport&apos;s architectural history. The existing residence offers approximately 3,345 sq ft of living space w/ 5 bdrms, 3.5 ba. Filled w/ warmth &amp; character, the home presents endless possibilities for restoration, renovation, or reimagining. Set back from the road &amp; accessed from both Riverside Ave &amp; Cherry Ln, the property enjoys an exceptional setting just minutes from downtown Westport, the train station, award winning schools, parks, restaurants, shopping, &amp; major highways. Builders &amp; developers take notice. With half acre zoning, no wetlands, dual street frontage, beautiful river views, &amp; a prime location, this property offers exceptional redevelopment potential &amp; a rare opportunity to create something truly special. Whether your vision is to preserve a piece of Westport history, renovate an architecturally significant home, or build a custom dream residence, opportunities of this caliber &amp; flexibility are increasingly rare.
