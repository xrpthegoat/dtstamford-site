About this home

Oversized first-floor one-bedroom residence offering nearly 950 square feet of living space in one of Stamford&apos;s most desirable communities. One of the largest one-bedroom floor plans in the complex, this freshly painted, move-in-ready home features 9+ foot ceilings, hardwood floors, and classic architectural details throughout. Enter through a charming English-style courtyard with ivy-covered brick exteriors into a welcoming foyer. The spacious layout includes a large living room, separate dining area, and an updated kitchen with a gas range and ample counter space, perfect for everyday living and entertaining. Heat and hot water are included in the rent. Additional amenities include card-operated laundry in the lower level, convenient 20-minute parking directly in front of the building for loading and unloading, plus ample resident and guest parking in the rear. Garage parking is available for $50/month when available (currently waitlisted). Ideally located just minutes from downtown Stamford, shopping, restaurants, entertainment, Metro-North, and major highways. Tenant pays electricity, gas, cable/internet, and renters insurance. Pets considered with a one time $150 registration plus $25/month pet rent.
