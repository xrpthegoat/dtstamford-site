About this home

Unmatched value in the heart of downtown Greenwich. This sun-filled 3-bedroom, 2.5-bath townhouse offers a rare blend of space, privacy, and outdoor living-right in town. Enjoy a stair-free private entry, an expansive private patio, and the ability to BBQ grill-an almost unheard-of luxury in this location. Inside, a bright, open layout features pristine hardwood floors, a cozy wood-burning fireplace, and a spacious dining area ideal for entertaining or a stylish work-from-home setup. Brand-new windows (November 2025) flood the home with natural light while enhancing comfort and energy efficiency. Additional highlights include abundant closet space, dual entry doors, and an in-unit washer/dryer-making this home truly move-in ready. Set within the beautifully maintained, 13-acre park-like grounds of Putnam Park, just moments from Greenwich Avenue, Metro-North, Whole Foods, top dining, and shopping. Extra storage, plentiful free off-street parking, optional garage parking through the HOA, and property taxes included in the HOA (partially tax-deductible) add exceptional value. A rare opportunity to own a spacious downtown townhouse with private outdoor living at an exceptional price point.
