About this home

Completely renovated, turnkey Cape-style beach home in coveted East Norwalk waterfront neighborhood. Steps from Long Island Sound with kayak-direct water access from your front porch. This move-in ready 3-bed, 2-bath home has been extensively updated top to bottom. The gut-renovated kitchen features all-new lux-appliances, warming drawer, ice maker with water filtration, new Wolf gas range, custom bar area, walk-in pantry, and new hardwood flooring. The sun-filled living room centers on a fully restored wood-burning fireplace with new brick apron, custom overlay, and two custom built-in shelving units with cabinetry. Hardwood floors throughout have been professionally sanded and refinished. The private home office is ideal for remote work. Upstairs, all three bedrooms feature new lighting, custom closet systems, fresh paint, and updated electrical. The fully gutted upstairs bath is all-new: repositioned plumbing, expanded tub/shower, raised showerhead, new toilet, tub, sink, tile, shower door, and fixtures. Downstairs bath also updated. Clean, sealed basement with new washer/dryer, wine fridge, extra refrigerator, and new staircase. Central air, detached garage, two entertainment decks, fenced yard. Walk to beaches, restaurants, and marinas. Rare turnkey find in Fairfield County.
