About this home

Experience the future of coastal living in this brand-new smart home located just steps from the sands of Compo Beach. This is the only just-completed residence on the Compo Beach side of Westport delivered as a fully integrated, self-monitoring home: rooftop solar power w/battery storage, intelligent load management, leak detection w/automatic water shutoff, energy-recovery ventilation, radiant heated floors, singular app-controlled lighting, shades, locks + HVAC. The house can be monitored + adjusted from anywhere in the world and stays powered on stored solar when the grid goes down. Designed by The O&apos;Dell Group across three vertical levels + a panoramic rooftop deck w/gas fireplace, the home opens to Long Island Sound through full-height glazing &amp; connects ground-floor living to a private loggia, heated gunite spa, + landscaped courtyard. Inside, the open kitchen pairs Subzero refrigeration + an integrated appliance package w/a generous island + pantry; the primary suite includes a private balcony + spa-style bath; a fully accessible shower is finished in stone, and a flexible main-level room w/full bath lives as an additional bedroom, home office, or guest suite. Iconic Compo Beach is just a short drive to the Metro-North station and about an hour to Grand Central/Midtown Manhattan. The Westport Project is in a category of its&apos; own; a home engineered to take care of itself, and you, whether you&apos;re upstairs, in the city, or on the other side of the world.
