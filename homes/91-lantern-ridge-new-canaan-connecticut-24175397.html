About this home

Exceptional contemporary home featuring an open, modern floor plan and 4,550 square feet of finished living space. Situated on a private, gated, and fully fenced two-acre level lot, this residence is located in a highly sought-after, quiet cul-de-sac. The interior boasts an impressive two-story foyer leading to a well-appointed layout. The main level includes a formal dining room, a living room with vaulted ceiling and a fireplace. The updated eat-in kitchen features stainless appliances and adjoins the family room. The home offers four bedrooms, including a spacious primary suite, a large playroom, and multiple dedicated office spaces. The fully finished lower level provides significant versatility, featuring: - A potential fifth bedroom and a study with built-in cabinetry - A full bathroom with a steam shower - A wine cellar, gym, and additional playroom space The property is environmentally conscious and well-maintained, featuring a newer roof with Tesla solar panels, a Tesla charging station, and a backup generator. The park-like grounds and expansive stone patio offer an ideal environment for indoor and outdoor entertaining. The home is conveniently located just steps from reservoir views and a short drive to the village center and the Metro North Train to NYC.
