About this home

Move right into this beautifully updated and meticulously maintained ranch offering the ease of one-level living on a professionally landscaped .29-acre property. Filled with natural sunlight, and designed for comfortable everyday living, this move-in-ready home is ideal for first-time buyers, downsizers, anyone seeking a wonderful alternative to condominium living or someone thinking about a great location for expansion. The inviting floor plan features a spacious living room with elegant moldings, a cozy fireplace, and oversized windows that fill the home with warmth and light. The updated kitchen and baths blend style and functionality, while the very comfortable primary suite offers a peaceful retreat with a large updated ensuite bath. The second bedroom is spacious. A charming three-season porch provides flexible living space and is perfect as a sunroom, home office, fitness area, playroom, or quiet place to relax. The kitchen offers bonus space with potential for future customization, plus direct access to the garage for everyday convenience. The expansive lower level provides abundant storage and excellent potential for additional finished living space. Outside, enjoy beautifully manicured grounds in a desirable location close to shopping, restaurants, parks, downtown Stamford, and public transportation. Combining comfort, convenience, and future possibilities, this special home is a rare opportunity to enjoy easy living in a highly desirable location.
