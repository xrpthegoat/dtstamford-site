About this home

Impeccably maintained and tastefully renovated, this townhouse delivers effortless living with stylish modern touches throughout. The remodeled kitchen features white shaker cabinetry extended to the ceiling for maximum storage, a convenient breakfast bar, stainless steel appliances, recessed lighting, and updated hardware throughout, creating a cohesive and modern finish. The spacious primary bedroom offers a beautifully renovated en suite bath complete with heated floors, updated fixtures, and a striking matte black dual shower system with rain head. Hardwood floors add warmth and continuity throughout the home, while every update has been thoughtfully completed to enhance comfort, practicality, and everyday living. Additional highlights include a cozy sun deck, perfect for enjoying your morning coffee, reading a book, or simply relaxing outdoors. With its blend of modern updates, inviting living spaces, and low-maintenance lifestyle, this beautifully renovated townhouse is truly ready to be enjoyed for years to come.
