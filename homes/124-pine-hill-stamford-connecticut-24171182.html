About this home

Well-maintained and thoughtfully updated 2-family in Stamford&apos;s highly sought-after Glenbrook neighborhood. This side-by-side property features two spacious 3BR/1.5BA units, each offering a functional and modern layout ideal for both owner-occupants and investors alike. Inside, both units showcase a bright living room that flows seamlessly into an open-concept dining area and kitchen, complete with white shaker cabinetry, stainless steel appliances, and granite countertops. Hardwood floors run throughout, complemented by central air and efficient gas heating. Each unit offers three generously sized bedrooms with ample closet space, creating comfortable living across all levels. The exterior highlights a clean, well-kept facade with separate entrances for each unit, a large level front lawn, and an expansive backyard-perfect for outdoor enjoyment, entertaining, or potential future enhancements. Ample off-street parking is available with a wide driveway and additional rear parking area, a rare convenience in this location. Ideally situated within walking distance to the Glenbrook Metro-North train station and just minutes to downtown Stamford, Stamford Hospital, major highways, shopping, and dining. A turnkey opportunity in a prime location with strong rental and long-term upside potential.
