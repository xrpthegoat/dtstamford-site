About this home

Please submit highest and best offer by Tuesday, 6/9 at 5pm! Welcome to the ultimate in carefree, city-chic living! Located in the sought-after Greenbriar complex, this sunny, immaculate 2-bed, 2.5-bath townhouse delivers the perfect balance of style, convenience, and tranquility. Tucked quietly away from the street at the back of the community, this light-filled home features an open floor plan with brand-new hardwood floors throughout, a cozy wood-burning fireplace, and a versatile sunroom/office overlooking a peaceful courtyard-ideal for remote work. Enjoy turnkey living just steps from vibrant Downtown Stamford shopping, top-tier dining, parks, and public transit!
