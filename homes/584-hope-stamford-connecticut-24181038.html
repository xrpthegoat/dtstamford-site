About this home

Welcome to Oxford Court, a beautifully maintained 10-unit community offering privacy, convenience, and a single-family home feel. Ideally located near shopping, restaurants, schools, public transportation, and two Metro-North stations within walking distance. This turn key END unit welcomes you with an inviting two-story foyer with hardwood floors flowing into a spacious living room with fireplace. The dining room and kitchen both provide easy access to deck thru 2 sets of sliders. Perfect for seamless outdoor entertaining. Updated on trend kitchen features quartz countertops, stainless steel appliances and custom breakfast bar. Upstairs, the primary suite enjoys double closets and a spa like full bath with double sinks, soaking tub, oversized shower and heated floors. A spacious second bedroom with spacious closet and a second full bath complete the level, which features hardwood flooring throughout. The walk-out lower level includes a family room with dramatic double-height windows and a walk out third bedroom and full bath. Ideal for guests, a home office, or additional living space. Special and rare feature is a TWO CAR garage. Conveniently located for commuters and offering flexible living options and attractive association dues, this townhouse is one to see. A Springdale gem.
