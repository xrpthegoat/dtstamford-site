About this home

Welcome to the heart of Norwalk in the sought after SoNo area. Second-floor, one-bedroom apartment. This well-maintained apartment features beautiful hardwood floors, central air conditioning, with gas heat and cooking. Additional amenities include a washer and dryer in the basement, a designated storage area, and convenient access to everything SoNo has to offer. Enjoy walking to local restaurants, shops, Veterans Park, and The Maritime Aquarium. The apartment is also located less than one mile from The SoNo Collection and just a quarter mile from the South Norwalk train station. Commuters will appreciate the easy access to I-95, Route 7, and the Merritt Parkway.
