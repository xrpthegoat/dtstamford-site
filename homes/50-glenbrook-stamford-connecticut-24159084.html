About this home

Spacious and beautifully updated 3-bedroom, 3-bath residence in a desirable doorman building, offering stunning city views from the 7th floor. This open-concept home features gleaming hardwood floors throughout and two private balconies facing south/east and west-perfect for enjoying both sunrise and sunset. The renovated eat-in kitchen boasts granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, and space for a cozy bistro table and chairs. The generous primary suite includes an enormous walk-in closet and a beautifully updated bath. The second bedroom features its own private bath, while the third bedroom offers excellent flexibility as a guest room, office, or den. Abundant closet space throughout makes this home both functional and versatile. Enjoy the convenience of reserved garage parking, a private storage unit, and a fully equipped gym in the building. Ideally located just minutes from downtown Stamford, beaches, Metro-North, and I-95, this home offers exceptional comfort and convenience for commuters and those looking to enjoy everything Stamford has to offer.
