About this home

Welcome home to this beautifully updated gem in one of Norwalk&apos;s most convenient locations! Bright, inviting spaces and thoughtful updates make this home as comfortable as it is stylish. The white kitchen features granite countertops, abundant cabinetry, gas cooking, and a casual dining area that opens to the welcoming living room with a cozy fireplace. Hardwood floors and oversized windows fill the home with natural light, creating a fresh, modern feel throughout. A spacious formal dining room is perfect for entertaining family and friends. Upstairs, the primary suite offers vaulted ceilings, a walk-in closet, and a private bath with double granite vanity and walk-in shower. Two additional bedrooms share a full hall bath. Want more space still? The finished lower level adds approximately 900 square feet of flexible living space-ideal for a family room, office, gym, or playroom-and includes abundant storage plus your own private sauna. Outside, enjoy a spacious deck overlooking the level, fully fenced backyard, perfect for entertaining, pets, or play. Additional features include central air, an attached one-car garage, and an EV Charger. Just minutes from the Merritt Parkway, shopping, restaurants, and parks, this move-in-ready home offers the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and lifestyle. Make this house your new home!
