About this home

PLEASE SUBMIT OFFERS BY MON 6/29 @ 5 PM. A rare opportunity to enjoy the ease and comfort of Single-Level living in this beautifully maintained First-Floor, End-Unit 3 bedroom 2 bathroom condo in Eastwood Terrace. Designed with both entertaining and privacy in mind, this thoughtfully laid-out 1,500-sq ft home with central a/c features a spacious open-concept Living and Dining area connected to an Eat-in Kitchen. The Primary Suite and 2nd Bedroom are positioned on opposite ends, providing full privacy. The 3rd Bedroom located off the living room offers flexibility as a home office, den, or guest room, as it comes without a closet. Spacious Primary Suite has a walk-in and 3 additional closets and a large Bathroom with a separate vanity area. Convenient in-unit laundry is located in the 2nd full Bathroom. Bright and spacious living and dining areas are filled with natural light from sliding glass doors leading to a large private deck. Accessible from both the living room and 3rd bedroom, the outdoor space is surrounded by mature landscaping and enjoys a peaceful end-of-building location, creating a private setting for relaxing or entertaining. The unit has 1 assigned parking space very close to the private entrance and a large private storage area conveniently located downstairs. This well-maintained complex is situated in the quiet part of highly desirable Upper Springdale, close to shopping, parks, schools, golf courses. 1.2 miles to Metro-North, 2 miles to Merritt Pkwy.
