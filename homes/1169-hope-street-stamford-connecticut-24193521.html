About this home

Discover the perfect blend of comfort, style and convenience in this beautifully updated townhouse nestled in Stamford&apos;s vibrant Springdale neighborhood. The open main level welcomes you with a spacious, sun-filled living room that flows seamlessly into the dining area. Sliding glass doors lead to a private balcony, perfect for enjoying your morning coffee or relaxing at the end of the day. The newly designed kitchen (2024) is sure to impress with gorgeous Matterhorn Quartzite countertops, stainless steel appliances and abundant cabinetry offering additional storage. A convenient half bath completes this level. Upstairs, the bright and spacious primary bedroom features a walk-in closet and private en-suite bath. A generously sized second bedroom with double closets and a full hall bath provides flexibility for family, guests or a home office. The fully finished lower level offers endless possibilities, featuring a large open room, separate laundry area, additional storage plus direct access to the one-car attached garage. Additional highlights include crown molding, recessed lighting and hardwood floors throughout along with a brand-new air-conditioning system just installed (July 2026). Just minutes from local shops, highly rated restaurants and the Springdale train station. Do Not Miss, Welcome Home!
