About this home

Welcome to elevated waterfront living in the heart of Historic South Norwalk. The Maritime, designed by award-winning architect Bruce Beinfield, sits directly on the Norwalk River w/ waterfront access from the public docks and winter waterviews from the floor-to-ceiling windows. This 2 bedroom, 2 bathroom residence with a private balcony is bathed in natural light, w/ gleaming, hardwood floors running throughout the open concept living space. The kitchen is the heart of the home: w/ solid refinished wood European-designed cabinets, new high heat laminate backsplash, granite countertops, a 10 ft island w/ seating, new stylish lighting above, &amp; updated stainless steel appliances; including new microwave and new refrigerator w/ ice and water dispenser, &amp; refurbished dishwasher. Both bedrooms offer walk-in California closet inserts. A built out foyer closet allows for ample storage &amp; pantry space. All major systems have been serviced &amp; maintenanced; including a new dryer, giving this home more upgrades than most in the building. Condo comes w/ two parking spaces: one in the underground garage, one designated outdoor spot in a private lot. Building amenities include a fully equipped fitness center, a resident lounge w/ fireplace, pool table &amp; TV, &amp; a conference/meeting room. The private balcony invites you for morning coffee or evening sunsets over the water. Truly a must see!
