About this home

Nestled in one of Darien&apos;s most cherished neighborhoods, this charming Cape Cod home offers the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, &amp; community. The main level features a spacious living and dining area, ideal for both everyday living &amp; entertaining, anchored by a cozy wood-burning fireplace. Upstairs, two additional bedrooms &amp; renovated full bath provide flexibility for family, guests, or home office space.
