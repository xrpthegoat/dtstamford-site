About this home

Experience luxury living in the center of vibrant downtown Stamford. This beautifully updated one-bedroom condo features rich engineered wood flooring throughout and a stylish kitchen with stainless steel appliances, white cabinetry, black granite countertops, a new gas stove, and a convenient breakfast bar. The inviting living area offers flexible space for entertaining, along with a perfect nook for a home office. Sliding glass doors lead to a private terrace, ideal for relaxing and enjoying city views. The spacious primary bedroom also provides terrace access and features three closets for ample storage. The full bathroom offers dual access from both the bedroom and main living area for added convenience. A dedicated laundry closet includes an in-unit washer and dryer. Residents enjoy an exceptional array of luxury amenities, including a penthouse-level fitness center, resident lounge, and a glass-enclosed heated indoor pool for year-round enjoyment. The third-floor outdoor patio provides space for grilling, relaxing, or spending time with your pet. Additional highlights include a rooftop common area, full-time concierge service, one assigned underground parking space, and convenient guest parking in the adjacent public garage. Enjoy the ultimate downtown lifestyle with premier shopping, dining, entertainment, and theater just outside your front door.
