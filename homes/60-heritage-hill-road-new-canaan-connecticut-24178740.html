About this home

Located in one of the community&apos;s most desirable settings, this elevated end-unit one-bedroom condo enjoys sweeping views and exceptional natural light from three exposures. Freshly painted and thoughtfully updated, the home features an upgraded kitchen, spacious and well-defined living areas, and abundant storage, including a large walk-in closet. The flexible floor plan allows for the addition of a separate sleeping area or Murphy bed within the main living space, offering versatility to fit your lifestyle. Additional conveniences include basement storage and on-site laundry. Recent improvements include new windows and a new air conditioning unit. Heat and hot water are included in the common charges for added value and ease of ownership. Ideally situated near the assigned parking space and separately deeded garage available for purchase with the unit (not included in listing price), residents also enjoy access to two community pools. A perfect combination of comfort, convenience, and low-maintenance living just moments from town.
