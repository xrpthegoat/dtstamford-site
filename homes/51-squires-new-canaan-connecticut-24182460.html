About this home

Nestled on a quiet cul-de-sac just minutes from town this 5br/5.2ba Colonial offers exceptional space, comfort, and flexibility. Designed for both everyday living and entertaining, the home features a spacious kitchen that was renovated in 2005 with a large breakfast room that flows seamlessly into an oversized family room with custom built-ins and a fireplace. Along with the formal living and dining rooms, the home also has a private office, large finished lower level ideal for a playroom, rec room, or fitness space, large mudroom, and a 2 car garage. The upper level features five bedrooms, including two primary suites. Two additional bedrooms each have their own baths, while a separate bedroom suite with an adjoining sitting room provides exceptional flexibility for an in-law, au pair suite, or 2nd office. Outside, enjoy a heated pool, slate terrace, and flat backyard, creating the perfect setting for outdoor gatherings and summer enjoyment. With its thoughtful layout and flexible living spaces, this home offers comfort and convenience at every turn.
