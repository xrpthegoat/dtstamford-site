About this home

Located in New Canaan&apos;s highly desired Silvermine neighborhood, this sophisticated, single-level ranch offers a rare, mint-condition opportunity perfect for downsizers or busy young families. The home welcomes you with an open, sun-drenched floor plan designed for effortless indoor-outdoor entertainment, highlighted by fine millwork, architectural details, &amp; gleaming hardwood floors. Cooking and hosting are a true delight in the bright, airy kitchen, which features modern appliances &amp; flows seamlessly into a large, sunlit family room. From there, a single step leads out to a private patio perfect for outdoor dining &amp; an expansive, level backyard. The main-floor sleeping wing is thoughtfully designed for privacy, offering the primary suite and bath along with 2 additional bedrooms and a full hall bath. The impressive finished lower level adds approximately 1,400 square feet of versatile living space complete with a half bath, making it ideal for a recreation room, home office, gym, or a potential in-law apartment. Many recent updates provide peace of mind, which partly include newer windows, a newer roof, a freshly paved driveway, and crisp interior and exterior paint. The outdoors functions as a private retreat, beautifully landscaped &amp; enhanced by a new stone entry, a rear stone terrace, &amp; with ample room for a pool. Residents will love the easy living lifestyle this home affords &amp; all just minutes away from downtown New Canaan&apos;s shopping, dining, and major commuter routes.
