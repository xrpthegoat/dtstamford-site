About this home

Have you ever imagined living on prestigious South Avenue and strolling into town for dinner, a movie, or ice cream on a beautiful New England evening? Here is your opportunity. Welcome to highly sought-after Hathaway Common, a charming condominium community known for its beautifully maintained grounds, inviting pool, clubhouse, and warm neighborhood atmosphere with social gatherings and community events throughout the year. This desirable home offers the ease of one-floor living, with an enormous lower level providing exceptional storage or the opportunity to create additional finished living space. A nice surprise is there are hardwood floors under the carpet. A one-car garage and additional parking add to the convenience. The unit also enjoys an unusually generous outdoor space, including an area for your own private garden. The location simply cannot be beat. Enjoy an easy walk to New Canaan&apos;s vibrant village center, featuring award-winning restaurants, boutique shopping, the world-class library, and Metro-North train access to New York City. Just moments away is beautiful Mead Park, offering tennis, pickleball, baseball, and skating. This special South Avenue offering provides the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and community, truly an opportunity not to be missed.
