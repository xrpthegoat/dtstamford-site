About this home

Hamptons sophistication meets timeless Westport charm in this exquisitely renovated 4-bedroom, 3-bath Colonial nestled at the end of a peaceful cul-de-sac on a double lot in the highly coveted Greens Farms neighborhood. Reimagined by acclaimed fashion designer Christian Siriano, this one-of-a-kind residence blends elevated design with exceptional craftsmanship throughout. The stunning new chef&apos;s kitchen features Calacatta Belgia marble countertops and farmhouse sink, premium Grohe fixtures, and a sleek induction stove. Luxurious Carrera marble bathrooms have been completely redesigned with timeless elegance and impeccable attention to detail. Every room is enhanced by Farrow &amp; Ball paint, creating a refined and cohesive aesthetic throughout the home. Sun-filled living spaces offer effortless flow for both everyday living and entertaining, leading to the newly oversized exterior patio. Thoughtfully renovated from top to bottom, this turnkey home delivers designer finishes in every corner. 7 High Gate Road is just moments from beaches, train, shopping, dining, and top-rated schools. A truly rare opportunity to own a fully renovated designer home in Westport, CT.
