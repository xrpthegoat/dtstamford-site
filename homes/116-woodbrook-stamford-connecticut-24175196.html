About this home

Welcome to an exceptional Newfield residence set on 1.57 level acres in a quiet, desirable setting. Fully renovated to the studs in 2014, with continued upgrades through 2025, this home showcases superb craftsmanship, refined millwork, and a no-expense-spared approach throughout. In 2021, a newly constructed two-car garage was added, featuring Penntek flooring and custom cabinetry. A temperature-controlled wine room with a mahogany glass door is seamlessly integrated into the home. The home is filled with natural light and offers a well-designed layout with seamless indoor-outdoor flow. A spacious Azek deck, accessible from both the kitchen and dining room, extends the living space outdoors. The primary suite features a tray ceiling, a well-designed bath, walk-in closet, and French doors opening to a private balcony. The lower level includes a versatile entertainment space, additional bedroom, and full bath, with direct access to the backyard. A whole-house generator provides year-round reliability and peace of mind.
