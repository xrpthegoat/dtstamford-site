About this home

Welcome to 264 Skyview Drive, a beautifully maintained 4-bedroom, 2-full-bath Colonial set on a level 0.46-acre lot in Stamford&apos;s desirable Westover neighborhood. The inviting living room features a wood-burning fireplace and sliding doors leading to a spacious 380 sq. ft. deck overlooking the private backyard. A generous formal dining room flows into the updated custom kitchen with granite countertops, breakfast nook, and additional deck access-ideal for indoor and outdoor entertaining. The first floor also offers a large recreation/playroom with French doors and a convenient laundry room. Upstairs, the primary suite features an en-suite bath, walk-in closet, and two additional dressing/linen closets. Three additional bedrooms with hardwood floors share a full hallway bath. Conveniently located just 3 minutes to the Merritt Parkway, 5 minutes to Downtown Stamford, and 8 minutes to I-95 and the Stamford Train Station, providing an easy commute to New York City.
