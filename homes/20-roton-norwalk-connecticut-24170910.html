About this home

Experience the heart and soul of Rowayton in this rare coastal compound. A 1929 main house and a 1920 cottage pair historic character with seaside charm. A very rare find in 06853! The main house offers a spacious 3,300SF open floor plan. Beautiful living room with fireplace &amp; built ins; spacious eat in kitchen with updated appliances; den with fireplace overlooks garden; wonderful family room w beamed vaulted ceiling opens to patio &amp; private back yard. Laundry, powered room and mudroom complete the first floor. The upper level has 3 spacious bedrooms, 2 ensuite, ample closets, hall bath and crib room or office, plus 2 decks with water views overlooking the pond. Full basement with wine cellar space, walk up attic and detached 2 car garage, freshly painted inside and out. The cottage has irresistible beachie vibe with vaulted ceiling, living room &amp; sleeping area, complete kitchen, full bath, laundry plus a private patio garden. Prefect income producing or in-law suite. Possible lot subdivision. Come experience the Rowayton lifestyle with beaches, restaurants, shops, farmers market, tennis, paddle, pickle, library, nature preserve and so much more. All an easy 60 minutes to NYC on Metro North. Welcome home!
