About this home

Renovated down to the studs in 2023, no expense was spared in redesigning this two-story residence in a beautiful art deco luxury building in the heart of central Greenwich. The eat-in kitchen with marble countertops, custom cabinets and Thermador appliances is open to the dining area and living room with electric fireplace. Beside the kitchen is a built-in home office and door to your private terrace. On the second floor is the primary bedroom with dual closets and an ensuite bathroom featuring dual sinks, a soaking tub, large shower, and marble throughout. The second bedroom, bathroom, and in-unit laundry complete this floor. Amenities include a common rooftop with L.I. Sound and NYC views, a storage unit, and 2 dedicated parking spaces in secure garage. Highest and Best - Monday April 27 - 5 pm.
