About this home

Spacious Colonial in the sought-after Hindley Elementary School district! Offering over 2500sf on the main and upper levels, this 5-bedroom, 3 full and 1 half bath home is designed for comfortable everyday living and entertaining. The oversized primary suite is complemented by generous living spaces, while 500sf finished lower level with wet bar provides an ideal recreation room, media room, home gym, or entertaining space. The eat-in kitchen opens to a large deck overlooking a private, fully fenced backyard-perfect for outdoor dining, children, pets, and summer gatherings. Enjoy the convenience of sidewalks leading to Darien&apos;s train station, downtown shops, restaurants, parks, beaches, and the Darien YMCA with beach view just across the street, offering competitive swimming and outstanding gymnastic programs, nursery school and activities for all ages. Public transportation is nearby, and quick access to I-95 makes commuting a breeze. Move right in and experience the exceptional convenience and lifestyle that Darien has to offer.
