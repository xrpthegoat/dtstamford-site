About this home

Welcome to this sun-drenched, impeccably maintained 3 bed, 2 bath townhouse style home offering refined, effortless living. Ideally located in downtown Greenwich, 20 mins walking distance to the train station taking you directly to Grand Central in NYC, 10 mins walking distance to Greenwich avenue, shops and restaurants, 5 mins walking distance to the elementary school, 10 minutes walking distance to the high school and to much more that Greenwich has to offer. The open concept layout flows seamlessly for everyday living. A spacious living area opens to a private balcony overlooking a tranquil courtyard. The home features one bedroom and full bath on the second level, with two additional bedrooms, a full bath, and laundry on the third floor. 2 unassigned parking spaces and pet friendly.
