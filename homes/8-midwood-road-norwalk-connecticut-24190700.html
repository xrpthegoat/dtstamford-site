About this home

Tucked into a peaceful setting, this well-maintained 3-bedroom, 2-bath ranch combines comfort, functionality, and plenty of living space. The bright living room is perfect for everyday living, while the spacious family room with a wood-burning stove creates a warm, inviting place to gather. The primary bedroom includes its own private full bath, and two additional bedrooms provide flexibility for guests, a home office, or additional living space. Just off the family room and kitchen, the screened-in porch is a favorite spot for morning coffee, relaxing with a book, or enjoying warm summer evenings. The yard provides room to spread out and enjoy the outdoors, and the attached two-car garage adds convenience and extra storage. Conveniently located near shopping, restaurants, parks, and major commuting routes, this home blends the ease of one-level living with the space and features today&apos;s renters are looking for.
