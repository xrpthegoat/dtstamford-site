About this home

Experience the perfect blend of luxury, convenience, and resort-style amenities in Palmer Hill, a premier gated community nestled on the Greenwich/Stamford border. This beautifully updated second-floor midrise home offers effortless single-level living. Flooded with natural light, walls of windows in every room showcase serene, park-like views of the courtyard&apos;s flowering trees. Flexible living with 3 bedrooms (or 2 bedrooms plus a private den/office) and a spacious open-concept living and dining area centered around a cozy gas fireplace. Two private balcony terraces provide the perfect spots for your morning coffee or evening relaxation. Fully refreshed with brand-new flooring, plush carpeting, and professional paint throughout. Equipped with modern cabinetry, granite countertops, and premium finishes. Enjoy secure elevator access directly from the underground parking garage. This home includes two parking spaces, a bicycle room, and convenient in-building mail and parcel pickup. Palmer Hill&apos;s award-winning amenities feel like a year-round retreat: A recently renovated clubhouse featuring a community/party room and meeting space. A fully equipped, state-of-the-art gym and yoga studio. An expansive heated pool, hot tub, and immaculately manicured, park-like grounds. You are just 5 to 10 minutes from downtown Stamford, Old Greenwich, and the Metro-North/Amtrak stations. Shopping and shores just a short distance away.
