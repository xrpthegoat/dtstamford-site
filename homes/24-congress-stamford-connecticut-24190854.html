About this home

Exceptional value with simplified monthly expenses. Welcome home to this bright and inviting three-bedroom, one-bath residence that offers the perfect balance of comfort, convenience, and exceptional value. One of the many advantages of this home is the simplicity of your monthly expenses. Water and sewer are included in the rent, and the tenant is responsible only for electricity, snow removal, and a fixed $200 monthly contribution toward oil-making budgeting easy and predictable. Ideally located near shopping, restaurants, live music venues, beautiful parks, and the waterfront. The spacious floor plan features an eat-in kitchen with stainless steel appliances, a dining room, and a generous living/family room. Recessed lighting throughout, gleaming newly installed hardwood floors, and a brand-new hot water heater add modern appeal. Three spacious bedrooms with built-ins provide comfort, while the private patio is perfect for morning coffee or relaxing evenings. Washer/dryer, tremendous basement storage, one-car garage, and ample off-street parking complete the package. A premium property that happens to be financially smart!
