About this home

Welcome to 110 Woodside Green (110 Summer Street), Unit #3A in Stamford. Rarely available, oversized two-bedroom condominium in the highly sought-after Woodside Green community. Nestled within a charming pre-war complex surrounded by beautifully landscaped, park-like grounds, this pet-friendly residence offers the perfect blend of timeless character, modern updates, and everyday convenience-all with low HOA dues. The spacious interior features gleaming hardwood floors, fresh paint, an updated kitchen with new appliances, a gas range, and an abundance of closet space throughout.
