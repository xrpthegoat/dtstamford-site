About this home

Back on Market! Buyer financing did not go through. Nestled in between the Darien border and Rowayton, this beautifully maintained 4-bedroom, 2.5-bath home offers the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and charm. Ideally situated close to shopping, dining, grocery stores and approximately a 10-minute walk to the Rowayton train station, it is an excellent option for commuters and those seeking easy access to local amenities. The main level features a sun-filled living room with a large picture window and classic fireplace, a formal dining room with access to the expansive deck, and a spacious, updated kitchen equipped with stainless steel appliances. Just off the kitchen, the oversized family room boasts a second gas fireplace and sliding glass doors that open to the backyard, creating an ideal setting for entertaining and everyday living. A dedicated home office overlooking the picturesque grounds provides the perfect work-from-home environment. Upstairs, you&apos;ll find four generously sized bedrooms with hardwood floors, including a spacious primary suite complete with an en-suite bath and ample closet space. The outdoor setting is equally impressive. Set on a beautifully landscaped, level half-acre lot, the property offers mature plantings, expansive green space, and a large deck ideal for relaxing and dining. Additional highlights include neighborhood block parties, a sprinkler system, laundry room, mudroom, and attached one-car garage with plenty of storage.
