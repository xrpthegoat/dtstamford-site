About this home

Great location! Spacious 2-bedroom, 1.5-bath condo located just steps from Cove Island Park. Conveniently close to I-95, Chelsea Piers, the Train Station, and downtown Stamford. The unit features an open-concept living room with a fireplace and sliding doors to balcony. The living room also includes in-wall satellite speakers with a subwoofer, perfect for entertaining or enjoying movies and music. Elevator in building. Building charges: $150 move-in and $150 move-out. Good credit is required.
