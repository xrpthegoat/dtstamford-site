How Much Is My House Worth in Stamford, CT?

Current value ranges by neighborhood, what moves your price up or down, and how to get an accurate number — fast.

Last updated · July 2026

The Stamford citywide median is roughly $710,000–$712,000 in mid-2026 — but your home's specific value depends on neighborhood, condition, and what's actually closed nearby in the last 90 days.

Short answer: Stamford median is ~$712K, but individual homes range from $275K condos to $3M+ waterfront estates. The only accurate way to know your number is a CMA using recent closed sales in your specific sub-market.

What makes a Stamford home worth more — or less?

The factors that move value the most in Stamford:

  • School district: Homes in top-rated elementary school zones command 5–15% premiums.
  • Metro-North proximity: Walkable distance to a station adds meaningful value for commuter buyers.
  • Lot size and privacy: In North Stamford especially, larger lots with privacy trees trade at a premium.
  • Kitchen and bath updates: Dated kitchens and bathrooms are the biggest buyer objection — updated ones add value and reduce negotiation friction.
  • Condition: A well-maintained home with a newer roof, HVAC, and windows commands more and inspects more cleanly.
  • Busy roads: Proximity to a major road or commercial use typically discounts value.

Why isn't my Zillow estimate accurate?

Zillow's Zestimate uses an algorithm that processes public data — it doesn't know about your renovated kitchen, your new HVAC, or the fact that your street has significantly more buyer demand than the next one over. In Stamford's varied market, Zestimates can be off by 5–15% for individual homes. For a pricing decision, a CMA from someone who has walked the comparable homes is meaningfully more accurate.

How do I get a precise value for my home?

A Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) pulls real closed sales of similar homes in your specific neighborhood — adjusted for size, condition, and features — and gives you a realistic list price range. I provide CMAs at no charge, with no obligation to list. Turnaround is typically 24–48 hours.
