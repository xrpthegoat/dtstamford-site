Living in Westover

Westover is an established, upscale neighborhood roughly a mile from Downtown that blends wooded, estate-style properties with in-town convenience. Historic Fort Stamford and green space are nearby, and the tree-lined streets give it a private, prestigious character close to the city center.

Westover real estate & home prices

Housing skews toward larger single-family homes and estates on generous lots, with typical values around $1.25M (estimated) depending on size, land, and finish. Getting around & commuting

Westover is only about a mile from Downtown Stamford and its transportation center, pairing quick access to Metro-North's ~50-minute express to Grand Central with a wooded, low-density setting.
