About this home

Beautifully perched upon 2.75 bucolic acres in affluent Wilton, CT, this freshly painted commercial opportunity is ideal for an owner user and includes multifamily redevelopment potential. The property features 371 feet of direct frontage along Danbury Road with high visibility, a public water connection, and is directly adjacent to the historic Cannondale Village. It is also close to Wilton Center shops, amenities, and restaurants. This listing includes a comfortable studio apartment with a full bath and kitchen, a series of well kept offices, abundant parking, and impeccably landscaped grounds. A peaceful stone patio looks upon a remarkable historic white oak that anchors the rear yard. Inside, original large plank wood floors add warmth and character. A fully finished barn with a loft provides additional office space, storage, or creative repurposing options. The lot is open and usable with no wetlands, giving future redevelopment plans maximum flexibility. If you are looking for a Transit Oriented Development opportunity, the Cannondale train station is only a short walk away, offering commuter convenience and steady local business activity. This is a one of a kind property that is ready for its next chapter. Immediately available at $1,249,000.
