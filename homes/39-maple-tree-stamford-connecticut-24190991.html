About this home

Welcome to this beautifully maintained, townhouse-style home in the highly desirable &quot;Maple Point&quot; community, perfectly situated near the Stamford/Darien border. Spanning four finished levels of versatile living space, this residence offers the perfect blend of suburban comfort and convenient city living. Step inside the main level to find a bright, oversized living and combined dining space with crown molding, large windows with natural light and an open-floor plan to the eat-in kitchen, complete with stainless steel appliances, stone countertops and an adjacent powder room. On the second floor the master bedroom offers a spacious walk-in closet, vaulted ceilings with an ensuite bathroom. Discover the additional bedroom down the hallway and up the staircase is a unique private bonus room perfect for recreational use, home gym and/or office space. The lower level features a dedicated laundry area with shelving and tile floors with a walkout to your own private one-car garage (one additional reserved parking space in lot). Positioned in a premiere location, just 5 minutes from I-95, shopping/dining in Stamford or Darien and a short walk to Metro-North train station. This is a rare opportunity for premium, multi-level living space in a prime location! Available to move-in by September 1st!
