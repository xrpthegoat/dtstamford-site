SoundWaters Marks Decade of Environmental Advocacy with Flotilla

2026-07-11 · patch.com
Editorial note. This is an AI-assisted local roundup drafted from public sources — details may be incomplete or evolving. Verify with the original source before relying on it. Not legal, financial, or investment advice.

This weekend, Stamford's SoundWaters is hosting its 10th Annual Flotilla event. The celebration will bring together paddlers and community members to enjoy the scenic beauty of Long Island Sound while promoting environmental education programs. Since its inception, SoundWaters has been dedicated to fostering a deeper connection between people and nature through various educational initiatives. This year’s flotilla promises an engaging day out on the water for all ages who are passionate about preserving our natural environment.
