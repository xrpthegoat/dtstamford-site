About this home

Situated on a quiet one-way street just minutes from I-95, Route 7, Stew Leonard&apos;s, shopping, dining, Metro-North, and Calf Pasture Beach, this beautifully updated 5-bedroom, 2-bathroom home offers an exceptional combination of location, convenience, and comfort across more than 2,500 square feet of living space in one of Norwalk&apos;s most desirable residential neighborhoods. Thoughtfully designed for both everyday living and entertaining, the home features an abundance of flexible living space, generous storage, and numerous recent improvements throughout. The main level offers a bright and inviting living room with hardwood floors and a classic wood-burning fireplace, creating the perfect gathering space. The stunning, newly renovated kitchen showcases crisp white cabinetry and flows seamlessly into a spacious dining area and a warm, welcoming family room highlighted by vaulted ceilings and abundant natural light. Two bedrooms and a fully renovated bathroom complete the main level, while the second floor offers three additional bedrooms, a full bathroom, and a versatile additional room ideal for a home office, playroom, media room, fitness area, hobby room, or reading retreat. Outside, enjoy a private, fully fenced backyard with a stone patio, perfect for outdoor dining, entertaining, or simply relaxing.
