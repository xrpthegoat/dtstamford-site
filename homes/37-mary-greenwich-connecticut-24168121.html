About this home

Step into this fully gut-renovated, reimagined rare gem in Riverside. Expanded to nearly double its original footprint, this beautiful home has been meticulously designed by Abode Interiors NYC. A striking bend of graceful arches, soaring 9 &amp; 10 foot cathedral ceilings, wide Brazilian oak floors, a marble fireplace, &amp; all-new windows filling the space with light. At the heart is an Architectural Digest-worthy custom eat-in kitchen, beautifully appointed with Calacatta Gold marble backsplash, quartz countertops, Bosch appliances &amp; bespoke millwork. The dining area opens to a deck via 12-ft sliding doors for seamless indoor-outdoor living. Upstairs, a light-filled, spacious lofted living area offers a versatile retreat for relaxing, or entertaining. On a private floor the primary suite is a serene escape, with a custom-designed closet &amp; a spa-inspired bath with soaking tub. Bedrooms are on separate floors, offering privacy. Outside, an oversized deck overlooks an expansive backyard framed by lilacs, irises, &amp; hydrangeas, complete with a charming brick shed &amp; flower beds. All new systems &amp; structures, including a new expanded paver driveway, Hardie siding, dual-zone HVAC on Google Nest, tankless water heater, upgraded electrical, soundproofing &amp; more-offering effortless turnkey living. Ideally located minutes from parks, Riverside Mall, &amp; Old Greenwich shopping.
