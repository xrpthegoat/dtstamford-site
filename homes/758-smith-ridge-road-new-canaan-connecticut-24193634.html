About this home

Nestled at the end of a private lane off sought after Smith Ridge Road, this enchanting 1924 Cape exudes timeless elegance and undeniable charm. Set on 2.2 lush, flat acres, the property offers a serene retreat with expansive grounds ideal for relaxation and hosting. The chef&apos;s kitchen features top tier appliances and quartz countertops that balance beauty with functionality, and it opens seamlessly into the family room for effortless everyday living. The spacious living room showcases French doors that lead to a breathtaking park-like setting with a built-in outdoor grill and stone fireplace. The first-floor primary suite is approached through a cozy sitting room with a fireplace, creating an intimate and inviting entry. Four beautifully finished bedrooms complete the home, each crafted with thoughtful detail and classic appeal. The expansive bonus room above the garage-complete with a full bathroom and steam shower-offers exceptional versatility, perfect for a home gym, recreation room, private office, or guest suite. Brand new septic system!
