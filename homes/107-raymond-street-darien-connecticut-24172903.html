About this home

Peace, Privacy, and Timeless Elegance in Darien Discover the perfect antidote to busy life in this beautifully refreshed, custom-built colonial. Lovingly maintained by its original owners, 107 Raymond Street offers an idyllic blend of quiet sophistication and spacious comfort. Step into a grand two-story foyer with herringbone oak floors, leading to formal spaces rich in architectural detail-including a step-down living room with a fireplace, a private library, and a formal dining room featuring elegant custom paneling. The heart of the home is the gourmet kitchen, equipped with custom inset cabinetry, premium stainless appliances, and a walk-in pantry, opening seamlessly to a dramatic step-down family room with a fieldstone fireplace. Upstairs, the freshly refinished oak floors lead to five bedrooms, highlighted by a magnificent primary suite featuring its own wood-burning fireplace, sitting room, and an enormous, brand-new luxury bath. With a large mudroom, second-floor laundry, a 3-car garage, and massive walk-up attic/basement spaces offering incredible expansion potential, this turnkey home offers the space you need and the serenity you&apos;ve been searching for.
