About this home

Conveniently located near shopping, dining, major highways, and train stations, this inviting three-bedroom, two full bath townhouse at Ledgebrook offers a comfortable lifestyle in a scenic setting surrounded by mature trees and peaceful ponds. Step into your foyer, up one flight of stairs and you are in your ranch style home. Inside, hardwood flooring extends throughout the home, while soaring vaulted ceilings enhance the sense of openness and natural light. The thoughtfully designed layout balances connection and definition, with a see-through fireplace serving as a distinctive focal point between the living and dining areas. Ample closet space throughout the residence provides valuable storage and everyday practicality. Residents of Ledgebrook enjoy access to a variety of amenities, including a pool, exercise room, and beautifully maintained grounds spanning approximately 20 acres. Walking paths and natural surroundings create an atmosphere that feels tucked away, yet remains close to everything needed for daily living.Best of all-end your evening enjoying a perfectly beautiful sunset overlooking the pool and pond!
