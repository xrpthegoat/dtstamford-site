About this home

Set along prestigious Round Hill Road in backcountry Greenwich, this new construction estate offers a rare opportunity to create a modern country retreat of exceptional scale and refinement. At approximately 14,000 +/- SF with 7 bedrooms, the residence blends classic Greenwich architecture with a more contemporary sense of light, glass, and indoor-outdoor living. Plans include gracious formal and informal living spaces, a dramatic entry, private library, expansive kitchen, first-floor guest suite, elevator, and finished walkout lower level with golf simulator, wet bar, wellness gym, and cold plunge. Outdoor amenities include terraced landscaping, stone walls, a 20x40 pool with integrated spa, and a separate pool house with kitchenette, bath, and fireplace. Unique opportunity to customize select finishes and layout while in current stage. Renderings, plans, specifications, and features subject to change. A singular offering for those seeking a generational Greenwich estate with privacy, provenance, and architectural permanence.
