Stamford Students to Ride More Comfortable School Buses

2026-07-12 · stamfordadvocate.com

The Stamford Board of Education is excited to announce a new contract for school bus services. The incoming provider promises an upgrade with 160 air-conditioned buses, ensuring students travel in more comfortable conditions during the school year. This change aims to enhance the overall experience and safety of daily commutes, reflecting the board's commitment to student welfare.

With these improvements, Stamford is taking a step forward in providing modern amenities for its young passengers, making the journey to and from school much more pleasant.
