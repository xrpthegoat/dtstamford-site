About this home

Lovingly maintained by the same owners for more than 40 years, this special ranch-style home is set on 1.35 private acres in one of Norwalk&apos;s most desirable neighborhoods, just moments from Cranbury Park and nearby conservation land and trails. A home ready for its next chapter, it offers nearly 1,900 square feet of living space with hardwood floors throughout the main living areas, a formal living room with fireplace, formal dining room, and a beautifully renovated custom kitchen with granite countertops, GE stainless steel appliances, and tile backsplash. Just one step down from the kitchen, the bright breakfast room features a bow window and sliders to the Trex deck overlooking the picturesque grounds. Three steps below, the sun-filled family room offers a vaulted ceiling, skylights, and peaceful woodland views. The home includes three bedrooms and two full baths, including a spacious primary suite with three closets and a private bath. An oversized two-car garage with high ceilings provides direct access to both the home and basement. The large unfinished basement offers abundant storage and excellent potential for future finished space. Mature landscaping, perennial gardens, an electric pet fence, and full house generator add to the appeal of this thoughtfully cared-for property.
