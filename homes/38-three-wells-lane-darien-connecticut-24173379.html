About this home

Set on two private acres in coveted northern Darien, this exceptional Colonial offers refined living, timeless design, and superior craftsmanship throughout. A gracious living room with fireplace provides a warm and sophisticated gathering space, while the formal dining room is perfectly complemented by an adjacent butler&apos;s pantry, ideal for seamless entertaining. A richly appointed study/library with its own fireplace offers a quiet and distinguished retreat.At the heart of the home, the expansive kitchen and family room open effortlessly to the spectacular outdoor living areas, creating the perfect setting for both everyday living and large-scale entertaining. Outside, the grounds have been transformed into a true private retreat featuring a pool, renovated pool house, outdoor kitchen, BBQ pit, pergola and fire pit - offering an exceptional backdrop for entertaining and year-round enjoyment. The fully remodeled lower level is thoughtfully designed for recreation and relaxation, featuring a custom theater room, walk-in wine cellar, and luxurious sauna. This remarkable property seamlessly combines luxury, comfort, and modern functionality in one of Darien&apos;s most desirable locations.
