About this home

Nestled in a quiet neighborhood this well maintained two family home offers space, versatility, and convenience in a highly desirable location. Set on a generous lot, the property features a spacious backyard perfect for outdoor enjoyment, along with ample parking for residents and guests. Commuters will appreciate the easy access to the Merritt Parkway, while the nearby train station and charming town center filled with quaint shops and local amenities are just a short walk away. The home has been thoughtfully updated, with cabinets and flooring on both the upper and lower levels, and fully renovated bathrooms upstairs and downstairs. Additional improvements include a new fire rated windows in the lower level and brand new carpeting throughout both levels, enhancing comfort and peace of mind. The main level boasts a comfortable layout with five carpeted bedrooms, a full bath, a dedicated laundry room, and an inviting eat-in kitchen. The living room features hardwood flooring, creating a cozy gathering space for everyday living. The lower level offers additional living flexibility, complete with three bedrooms featuring wall-to-wall carpeting, a fully renovated full bath, and a combined living and dining area alongside a functional kitchen ideal for extended family or rental potential. Ideal for investors or multi-generational living, this property offers location, updates, and practicality!
