About this home

Welcome to this beautiful 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom condominium featuring its own private garage with additional storage in the desirable Chesterfield Condominium Assoc. This bright and inviting home offers a living room with soaring cathedral ceilings, skylights, fireplace, and new hardwood floors. The dining room and well-appointed kitchen with granite countertops and a dry bar both provide access to a private balcony, creating an ideal space for indoor-outdoor living. The spacious primary suite features an updated en-suite bathroom, while the generous second bedroom includes a walk-in closet and is complemented by a second full bathroom. Residents enjoy the gated community with two in-ground pools, a large clubhouse with a full kitchen for entertaining, and beautifully maintained grounds. Ideally located across from Ridgway Shopping Center and just minutes to Bull&apos;s Head Shopping Center, Scalzi Park, downtown Stamford, I-95, the Merritt Parkway, and Metro-North and Amtrak service, this home offers exceptional convenience and low-maintenance living in one of Stamford&apos;s most sought-after communities.
