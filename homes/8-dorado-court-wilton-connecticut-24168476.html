About this home

Nestled on a peaceful cul-de-sac, 8 Dorado Court stays true to its Colonial style while offering modern comfort on a private, landscaped 2 acre corner lot. Over 2,300 sq ft of inviting living space begins with a gracious center hall and continues through sunfilled rooms enhanced by custom trim, detailed moldings, and Marvin doublepane windows. The thoughtfully designed kitchen features high-end appliances, radiant heated floors, and built-in mahogany seating, perfect for everyday living and effortless entertaining. A spacious living room with an elegant woodburning fireplace creates a cozy gathering spot year round. Upstairs, four generous bedrooms with California Closets offer flexibility for family, guests, or work from home needs, while the radiant heated hall bath with skylight adds a spa-like touch. An Ipe screened porch extends living space through three seasons and opens to a bluestone patio ideal for outdoor dining. The backyard is a private retreat reached by a footbridge that takes you to a climbing wall and clubhouse. A spacious backyard deck offers the perfect spot to relax, entertain, and enjoy the expansive open space. Additional highlights include a two car garage with EV quickcharge hookup, spacious custom mudroom, large laundry room, storage/potting shed, LeafFilter gutter guards, and a 2017 septic system. A rare blend of craftsmanship, comfort, and natural beauty all located conveniently to Route 7 and Metro North&apos;s Cannondale train station.
