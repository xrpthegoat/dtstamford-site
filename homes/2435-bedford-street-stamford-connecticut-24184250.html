About this home

Quiet oasis with Mid-City convenience! Welcome to this spacious, airy 2-bedroom, 2.5-bath townhome in the coveted, gated Chesterfield II community-where comfort, convenience, and lifestyle meet seamlessly. Designed for everyday living and entertaining, the open-concept layout features a dining room and living room with a cozy fireplace and sliders to a private deck surrounded by mature trees - both rooms showcase beautiful hardwood floors. The refreshed kitchen, is complete with granite countertops, great storage, and updated stainless steel appliances. Upstairs, the primary bedroom is a true standout, boasting a dramatic vaulted ceiling with a skylight and ceiling fan, plus a private en-suite bath with marble tile, a jetted tub, a separate shower, and a double granite vanity. The second spacious bedroom offers 2 deep closets and convenient dual entry to the full bath from both the bedroom and hallway. Just above, a versatile, freshly carpeted loft offers the flexibility to serve as a third sleeping area, home office, or creative space. The finished lower level features new, durable laminated wood floors, adding prime livable space perfect for a family room, home gym, or media lounge. Additional features include a private garage plus 2 additional parking spaces, security guard, clubhouse, and 2 in-ground pools. Minutes from shopping, dining, commuter routes, and both the Downtown &amp; Harbor Point entertainment districts. Truly a home not to be missed!
