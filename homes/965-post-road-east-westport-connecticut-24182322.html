About this home

Position your business in the heart of Westport in this beautifully renovated three-story professional building offering luxury office space with exceptional flexibility. Recently updated with modern finishes throughout, the property showcases bright interiors, hardwood floors, and a sophisticated contemporary aesthetic designed to impress both clients and employees. The second floor encompasses approximately 1,386 square feet and features four private office suites surrounding a spacious central open workspace capable of accommodating multiple workstations, collaborative team areas, or reception functions. Flexible leasing options allow tenants to lease a single office, multiple suites, or the entire floor to best suit their operational needs. Shared amenities include a thoughtfully designed common area, kitchenette, and private restrooms, creating a professional and comfortable work environment. A dedicated rear parking lot provides convenient on-site parking for both tenants and visitors. Utilities are billed separately as a proportionate share in addition to base rent. Ideally suited for professional, legal, financial, consulting, wellness, creative, or executive office users seeking a turnkey workspace, this exceptional offering combines flexibility, functionality, and a premier Westport business address.
