About this home

Tucked into one of East Norwalk&apos;s most desirable streets in Sasqua Hills, 22 Cavray Road presents 7,800 sq ft of refined, flexible living space in a location defined by both convenience and coastal appeal-just minutes to the beach, train, and the centers of Westport and South Norwalk. Designed with both scale and elegance in mind, the home features sun-filled interiors, oversized windows, high ceilings, hardwood floors, and custom millwork throughout. The kitchen serves as a central gathering space, appointed with a generous island, quality appliances, and a spacious breakfast area, and flows seamlessly into the home&apos;s principal living spaces-well suited for both everyday living and entertaining. The main level offers a balanced composition of formal and informal rooms, anchored by two honed stone fireplaces that lend warmth and architectural character. Three sets of French doors open to a bluestone patio with outdoor fireplace and koi pond, creating a private &amp; inviting outdoor setting. A separate lower-level bedroom, full bath, and den provide a well-considered arrangement for guests, in-laws, or au pair. Upstairs, the primary suite offers a quiet retreat with two walk-in closets and a luxurious bathroom finished in Carrara marble. Three additional en-suite bedrooms, a media room, and a finished third floor provide exceptional flexibility for a variety of living arrangements. A rare opportunity in the coveted Sasqua Hills neighborhood!
