About this home

Fabulous Newfield location offers this beautifully appointed 3-level townhome offering spacious, flexible living. Dramatic two-story entry foyer welcomes you into the open-concept main level featuring hardwood floors, 9-foot ceilings and a stylish kitchen with quartz countertops, stainless steel appliances and abundant workspace. The sun-filled living room/dining area with sliders to a private patio with pergola and yard is perfect for relaxing or entertaining. A powder room and direct access to the garage complete the main level. Upstairs you&apos;ll find the primary bedroom with walk-in closet, full bath with double vanity and radiant heated floor, plus two additional bedrooms, a full hall bath and convenient laundry. The walk-up attic provides exceptional storage space. Finished lower level offers outstanding versatility with a possible fourth bedroom or family room, full bath and office/gym, ideal for guests, working from home or recreation. Additional highlights include; recently painted interior and solar panels for enhanced energy efficiency and reduced utility costs. Ideally located directly across from Newfield Green Shopping Center and just minutes from houses of worship, Barrett Park, dining, shopping and vibrant downtown. A wonderful opportunity to enjoy low-maintenance living in a highly convenient location.
