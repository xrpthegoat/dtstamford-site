About this home

Experience refined luxury living at The Classic, one of downtown Stamford&apos;s most prestigious high-rise residences. This fully remodeled 1BR 1BA condominium blends sophisticated design with exceptional convenience, creating the perfect urban retreat. The elegant doorman building welcomes residents with a beautifully appointed lobby. Inside, the sun-drenched unit showcases brand-new designer flooring, crown molding, designer finishes, and an inviting open-concept living and dining area that extends seamlessly to a private balcony. The stunning chef-inspired kitchen has been thoughtfully reimagined with quartz countertops, gray shaker cabinetry, premium stainless steel appliances, a stylish tile backsplash, gas cooking, and breakfast bar seating. The spacious primary bedroom offers private balcony access, a generous walk-in closett, while the beautifully updated bath features a storage vanity and tub/shower combination. Residents enjoy an impressive collection of resort-style amenities, including a rooftop indoor pool, fitness center, resident lounge, rooftop terrace with sweeping city views, and an outdoor dog park with grilling areas. Complete with in-unit laundry, garage parking, gas cooking, and hot water included in the common charges. Step outside and enjoy one of Stamford&apos;s most walkable locations, just moments from acclaimed restaurants, vibrant nightlife, theaters, parks, and Metro-North, placing the very best of downtown living right at your doorstep.
