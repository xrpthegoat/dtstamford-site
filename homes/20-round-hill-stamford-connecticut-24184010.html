About this home

A classic 4-bedroom colonial in one of North Stamford coveted neighborhoods, Laurel Ledge Park. The main level of the home features a large, fire-lit, living room that opens to the dining room, anchored with matching built-in hutches. The eat-in kitchen has granite counters and stainless appliances. The island has seating for a morning cup of coffee. The paneled family room features a wall of glass inviting splashes of sunlight throughout the day. The home office could also serve as a first-floor bedroom if needed. A powder room and laundry room complete the first floor. Upstairs you will find four generous sized bedroom including the primary bedroom suite, with walk-in closet. There are beautiful oak floors in almost every room. The heated lower level has a massive game room complete with a fireplace, bar, and built in shelving. The game room has sliders leading outside to a flagstone patio. There is also a large unfinished portion of the basement available to be finished or used for storage. The property has an irrigation system and a propane-powered, whole-house generator. Recent upgrades to the home include the furnace and AC units. The 1-acre yard is tastefully landscaped and features a spacious terrace bathed in filtered sunshine for daytime fun, and equipped with landscape lighting for evening entertainment.
