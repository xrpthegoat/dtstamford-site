About this home

Rarely available, charming Glenbrook 2F opportunity! Both units have 2 bed 1 bath with spacious layouts that live much larger, lovely hardwood floors throughout, and secured front and back entrances for each. The First-floor unit offers a living room, dining room, beautiful galley style kitchen and an additional bonus space off the living room; which could be imagined as a home office, gym, or playroom. Upstairs, the larger of the two units, find a similar layout, with an added flexible room, currently being utilized as a home office. The second floor is cooled by mini splits and accesses a full attic for additional storage. The washer and dryer are located in the basement &amp; services both units. The finished basement adds even more possibilities with a kitchenette and full bath. Additional features: separate utilities for convenience &amp; efficiency, gas heat &amp; tankless hot water, detached two-car garage, ample off street parking, and private backyard space. Incredible walkable location to restaurants, shops, recreational activities and train: Glenbrook station is steps away w/express train to Manhattan in 55 minutes. This is your opportunity to own a multi-family property with modern amenities, strong rental flexibility and a prime Stamford location-ideal for investors or owner-occupants alike.
