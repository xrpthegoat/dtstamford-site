About this home

Welcome to 21 Culloden Road, a spacious and meticulously maintained 2-family residence situated in the highly desirable Glenbrook neighborhood. This turnkey property features two large, light-filled units, making it an incredible opportunity for investors or owner-occupants/ multi generational family living. Unit 1 (First Floor) features 3 bedrooms, 1 full bath, separate living and dining rooms, central air, and an expansive eat-in kitchen. Unit 2 (Second Floor) features 4 bedrooms, 1.5 baths, in-unit laundry, central a/c and an equally spacious eat-in kitchen. Major capital improvements offer peace of mind, including a fully replaced roof and new water heater (both 2021). Both units will be delivered vacant at closing. Step outside to a flat, usable backyard-a private outdoor oasis perfect for grilling, gardening, or relaxing. The oversized driveway provides ample off-street parking for multiple vehicles and features a new gardening shed for extra storage. Just minutes from the Glenbrook and Stamford Transportation Centers (Metro-North/Amtrak express to NYC), I-95, and Chelsea Piers. Enjoy easy access to Cove Island Park&apos;s beaches and the vibrant dining, shopping, and nightlife of Downtown Stamford. Whether you are looking to expand your rental portfolio or live in one unit while renting the other, 21 Culloden Road is the perfect place to call home.
