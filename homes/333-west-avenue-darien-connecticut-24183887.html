About this home

Privately positioned within Darien&apos;s sought-after Noroton Greens community, this exceptional residence offers a sophisticated blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience. Thoughtfully designed for effortless living and entertaining, the home features expansive living spaces, timeless finishes, and an ideal layout for today&apos;s lifestyle. Oversized windows fill the home with natural light, while custom plantation shutters add warmth, elegance, and privacy throughout. The gracious floor plan provides seamless flow between the living and dining areas, creating an inviting atmosphere for both everyday living and special gatherings. The spacious kitchen offers abundant cabinetry, generous workspace, and easy access to the home&apos;s main living areas. A standout feature is the large private primary suite, offering a peaceful retreat complete with a spacious changing room/walk-in closet and a well-appointed en-suite bath. Enjoy a low-maintenance lifestyle in one of Darien&apos;s most desirable communities, just a short walk to Darien Commons and the Noroton Heights Train Station. Downtown Darien, beaches, parks, restaurants, and I-95 are all just a short drive away.
