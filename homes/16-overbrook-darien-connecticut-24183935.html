About this home

Set on a beautifully manicured one-acre lot, this home welcomes you w/ soaring 9-foot coffered ceilings and a fabulous flow w/ bright, open, spacious rooms and wonderful natural sunlight, making every room feel both generous and livable. The heart of the home is a large gourmet kitchen featuring marble countertops, stainless steel appliances, and a center island, ideal for gatherings and everyday living. The kitchen opens to a casual dining space and a spacious family room w/ a fireplace. A formal living room, formal dining room w/ French doors, mudroom w/ great storage, powder room, and access to the 3-car garage, with ceiling height designed to accommodate double stacking for up to 6 vehicles, complete the main level. Step outside from the kitchen to a private deck overlooking an expansive yard w/ mature plantings and a separate outdoor firepit space, perfect for hosting friends or enjoying quiet evenings under the stars. Upstairs, the luxurious primary suite is a true retreat, boasting an expansive walk-in closet, his and hers spa-inspired bathrooms with a double marble-topped vanity, oversized shower, and a classic soaking tub, plus room for a private sitting area. 4 add&apos;l bedrooms w/ great closets and a laundry room round out the second floor. As a bonus, an additional 1,000 square feet in the exceptional finished basement offers a second family room, home gym, and private office. Beyond the home itself, the location is second to none.
