About this home

Casual coastal living with a vacation vibe all year long here at Dolphin Cove. Set on the border of Old Greenwich close to Innis Arden Golf Club, the residents of the private Dolphin Cove waterfront community enjoy over 3 acres of lush green lawns, sandy beach, in ground heated pool, tennis and pickle ball courts, clubhouse, boat slips and yacht club. Open concept, bright and crisp interiors enjoy sparkling water views from just about every room. The primary bedroom has a wall of glass overlooking the water, with sliders out to the deck. Two additional second floor bedrooms share a bath. A private ensuite makes for the perfect home office or weekend guest space. An oasis to soothe the soul.
