About this home

Welcome to this immaculate Colonial nestled on a quiet dead-end street in the desirable East Norwalk neighborhood. This beautifully maintained 3-bedroom, 2 full and 2 half bath home offers a bright, open layout with hardwood floors throughout and an abundance of natural light. The spacious kitchen flows seamlessly into the inviting family room with fireplace, where french doors open to a private patio and level backyard, perfect for entertaining, outdoor dining, or relaxing. The main level also features a sun-filled living room and formal dining room ideal for gatherings, while upstairs you&apos;ll find a spacious primary suite with soaring ceilings and a beautifully appointed en-suite bath. Two additional generously sized bedrooms offer plenty of natural light and ample closet space. The finished lower level provides exceptional flexibility with a private home office, half bath, and a large family/recreation room ideal for working from home, a media room, gym, or play space. Conveniently located just minutes from the East Norwalk train station, Calf Pasture Beach, South Norwalk (SoNo), Saugatuck, I-95, and Route 7, this move-in ready home offers the perfect combination of comfort, style, and an unbeatable location.
