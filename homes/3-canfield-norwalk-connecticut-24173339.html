About this home

Hidden behind Shorehaven Country Club in a private gated waterfront enclave, 3 Canfield Crossing offers a rare combination of privacy, a naturally protected waterfront setting, and an immersive connection to the water. Unlike many waterfront homes, the views here aren&apos;t reserved for a single room. Nearly every space-from the kitchen and living areas to the bedrooms and balconies-captures the ever-changing tides, creating a seamless connection to the surrounding landscape. Completely reimagined in 2024, the home was designed for effortless indoor-outdoor living. A chef&apos;s kitchen with custom cabinetry, premium appliances, and a butler&apos;s pantry opens to expansive living and dining spaces, while a NanaWall connects the interior to the waterfront terrace and infinity-edge pool beyond. With four finished levels connected by elevator, six ensuite bedrooms, and two separate guest or au pair opportunities, the home offers exceptional flexibility for both everyday living and entertaining. Whether enjoying a quiet morning on the terrace, kayaking or paddleboarding from your backyard, spending the afternoon at nearby Shorehaven Country Club, or dining just minutes away in Westport, Rowayton, or South Norwalk, the lifestyle is as compelling as the setting. This is a waterfront residence where timeless design, exceptional privacy, and an extraordinary connection to the water come together in one of Fairfield County&apos;s most distinctive coastal settings.
