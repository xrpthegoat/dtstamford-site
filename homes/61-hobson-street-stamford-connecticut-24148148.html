About this home

Set in Stamford&apos;s coveted Shippan Point waterfront community, this renovated home offers a flexible, livable layout. The main level features hardwood floors, a chef-caliber kitchen with high-end appliances and generous prep space, a large dining area, a comfortable living room, and a wraparound deck overlooking a private patio. Three nicely sized bedrooms on the main level include one of two primary suite options, each with a full bath and custom-designed walk-in closet. The lower level adds an office/or 5th bedroom, a spacious family room with exercise equipment, and a private suite, with access to the patio and a top-of-the-line Sundance Maxxus six-person spa. An easy walk to the water and neighborhood beaches, and minutes to Metro-North, Harbor Point, and downtown Stamford. Must see to appreciate.
