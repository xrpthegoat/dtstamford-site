About this home

Welcome to Woodland Terrace, offering a must-see residence in a charming community just a short walk from Springdale Village and its array of restaurants, bars, shops, and conveniences. This bright and inviting 2-bedroom, 1.5-bath townhouse features a spacious open-concept living and dining area filled with natural light, laminate flooring, recessed lighting, and direct access to a private patio overlooking the beautifully maintained courtyard and grounds-perfect for relaxing, outdoor dining, and entertaining. The kitchen offers abundant cabinetry, granite countertops, a tiled backsplash, and a separate dining area surrounded by windows. Upstairs, two spacious bedrooms offer generous closet space and hardwood floors beneath the wall-to-wall carpeting, complemented by an updated full bath. Enjoy the rare convenience of a private garage plus two additional outdoor parking spaces. Common charges include water, hot water, and sewer, in addition to the many services provided by the association. Ideally located near the Springdale Metro-North station, Waveny Park, and Sterling Farms Golf Course, with easy access to the Merritt Parkway, I-95, shopping, and downtown Stamford, this home combines space, convenience, and an exceptional location.
