About this home

Luxury meets convenience in this stunning fully detached townhome offering 2,617 sq ft across 4 finished levels in an unbeatable Stamford location on the Darien border. THE ONLY STANDALONE UNIT AT MAPLE POINTE, this meticulously maintained 3 bedroom, 2 full &amp; 3 half bath home delivers the feel of single family living w/ the ease of a maintenance free lifestyle. Perfect for commuters, enjoy walking distance to both train stations Glenbrook (0.4 miles) &amp; Noroton (1 mile), nearby parks, beaches, restaurants, Darien Commons, &amp; easy access to I95. Sun filled open concept main level living/dining rooms feature gleaming floors, Hunter Douglas shutters, crown molding. Updated kitchen offers st/st appliances, granite counters, large pantry, new backsplash, freshly painted cabinetry, cozy dining nook, &amp; the only unit w/ sliders leading to 2 private bluestone patios perfect for entertaining. Upstairs, the luxurious primary suite features a spa inspired bath with double sinks, jetted tub+walk in shower, huge WIC, &amp; private balcony w/trex style deck. An additional bedroom &amp; full bath complete the second floor. The 3rd floor loft offers incredible flexibility w/ a 3rd bedroom, separate media room w/ speaker system, &amp; office space w/ a half bath. The finished lower level includes the ultimate den w/, powder room, laundry, &amp; excellent storage. Upgrades include a brand new high efficiency on demand gas water heater, new upstairs AC, newer main level flooring, whole house paint, &amp; more.
