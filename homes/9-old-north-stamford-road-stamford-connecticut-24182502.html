About this home

Welcome to Chesterfield, Stamford&apos;s premier gated community! This beautifully maintained tri-level townhouse offers the perfect blend of comfort, privacy, convenience, and what a view!! NOTICE, the tax card for town is incorrect, this is a 2 full bathroom and one half bath, 3 level 1600 sq foot townhome, with second bedroom on lower level.
