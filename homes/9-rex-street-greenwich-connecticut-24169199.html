About this home

Beautiful 4-bedroom, 3-full bath Colonial home built in 2000, situated at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac. This residence offers water views of Caroline Pond along the Byram River directly from the front porch. The home features 9-foot ceilings and hardwood floors throughout. Key highlights include: Main Floor: A light-filled open flow featuring an eat-in kitchen, a living room with a fireplace, a dining room with a bay window, a private office, and a mudroom entry. Second Floor: A primary suite with a full bath and walk-in closets, plus three additional spacious bedrooms and a hall bath. Outdoor Space: An entertaining patio equipped with a wood-burning pizza oven and a fire pit. The property provides easy access to Pemberwick Park&apos;s hiking trails, tennis courts, and playground, and is located just minutes away from the train station and Greenwich Avenue.
