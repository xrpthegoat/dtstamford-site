About this home

Welcome to 71 Blackberry Drive, where contemporary updates meet exceptional design. This beautifully renovated home features a dramatic open-concept floor plan with soaring vaulted ceilings, exposed wood beams, and expansive living and dining spaces filled with natural light. The custom kitchen is equipped with premium cabinetry, quartzite countertops, a large center island, farmhouse sink, and stainless steel appliances, making it ideal for both everyday living and entertaining. Stylishly updated bathrooms, spacious bedrooms, and quality finishes throughout reflect the extensive improvements made to the home, including a new roof (2023), updated windows and doors, newer HVAC, and numerous interior upgrades. Step outside to multiple decks and patio areas overlooking a private, wooded setting that provides the perfect backdrop for outdoor gatherings or quiet relaxation. Completing the property is an oversized paver driveway, attached garage, and a convenient Stamford location with easy access to shopping, dining, parks, and major commuting routes. According to the field card, the property is listed as containing 2 bedrooms and 2.5 bathrooms. However, the dwelling currently contains 4 bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms. The total gross living area, including the finished lower level, is 3,208 square feet, comprised of 1,367 square feet in the lower level, 1,486 square feet on the first floor, and 355 square feet on the second floor.
