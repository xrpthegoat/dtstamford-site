About this home

Stunning European-style mini-estate where rolling golf course greens meet breathtaking views of the Long Island Sound. Crafted with a timeless stone exterior, cedar roof, hardwood floors, and exquisite granite surfaces, this home offers an unparalleled lifestyle of privacy and sophistication. The heart of the home is an open-concept layout featuring a soaring 2-story family room with dramatic French doors that open to a backyard oasis. The chef&apos;s kitchen is equipped with high-end appliances, custom cabinetry, and a massive center island. The main floor boasts a formal dining room, a dedicated office with built-in bookshelves, and a luxurious primary suite featuring vaulted ceilings, a private screened porch overlooking the golf course, and a spa-like bath with a soaking tub and custom walk-in closet. Upstairs, a stunning balcony looks out to the Sound, leading to 4 generous bedrooms: 2 en-suites and 2 sharing a Jack-and-Jill bath. The lower level is the ultimate entertainment hub, offering 3,000 sq ft of finished space designed as a private club with a state-of-the-art gym, connoisseur&apos;s wine room, spacious billiards room, and full bath. Step outside to a private paradise with manicured gardens, a sparkling gunite pool, and a pool house with a full bath. A fully equipped outdoor kitchen makes summer hosting an absolute breeze. Do not miss this rare opportunity to own a resort-style masterpiece.
