About this home

Located in a desirable neighborhood in Old Greenwich, this contemporary home offers a spacious and thoughtfully designed floor plan with five bedrooms and three full bathrooms. The main level is centered around an expansive living room with a fireplace, creating a warm and inviting space for both everyday living and entertaining. The kitchen features a center island and opens to a bright breakfast nook, while a separate dining room provides an ideal setting for gatherings. A first-floor bedroom with a walk-in closet and en-suite bathroom offers flexibility for guests, multigenerational living, or a private home office. The second floor features four generously sized bedrooms, including a spacious primary bedroom, along with two full bathrooms and ample closet space throughout. Thoughtfully designed with well-proportioned living spaces and a functional layout, this home offers comfort, versatility, and an exceptional location.
