About this home

Set on a beautifully landscaped, level property in South Wilton, 18 West Meadow offers exceptional indoor and outdoor living with convenient access to Westport, Norwalk, I-95, the Merritt Parkway, shopping, dining, and train service. This 4-5 bedroom home features a renovated kitchen with Absolute Black granite countertops, a large center island, breakfast area, Wolf cooktop and wall ovens, and a Sub-Zero refrigerator. The kitchen opens to the family room with custom built-ins, a wood-burning fireplace, and sliding doors to the stone patio. Sliders from both the family room and dining room create a seamless connection to outdoor living spaces. The main level also includes an office with a bay window, built-in bookshelves, gas fireplace, and backyard views. A formal living room features additional built-ins and a gas fireplace, while a large pantry provides ample storage. Upstairs, the primary suite includes a windowed walk-in closet, Juliet balcony overlooking the backyard, and a renovated, bath with soaking tub, walk-in shower, dual vanities, and private water closet, all with Porcelainosa fixtures. Three additional bedrooms are located on the upper level, including two with en-suite baths. A walk-up attic offers future expansion potential. Above the garage is a flexible living area with a bedroom, full bath, and separate access through the mudroom. The backyard features a stone patio, irrigation system, fenced vegetable garden, expansive lawn, and a whole house generator.
