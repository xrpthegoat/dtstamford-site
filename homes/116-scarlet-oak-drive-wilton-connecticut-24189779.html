About this home

Nestled on beautifully landscaped grounds framed by classic New England stone walls, this exceptional 4-bedroom, 2.5-bath Colonial blends timeless architecture with modern comfort in one of North Wilton&apos;s most desirable settings. Designed for everyday living and entertaining, the sun-filled interior features spacious rooms, custom built-ins, and quality craftsmanship throughout. The chef&apos;s kitchen offers a Wolf gas cooktop, double wall ovens, stainless steel appliances, abundant cabinetry, and generous prep space, opening to a four-season sunroom with a pellet stove. The formal dining room features a wood-burning stove, and French doors leading to an elevated balcony overlooking the private grounds. A living room with a fireplace provides additional gathering space, while the dramatic great room showcases cathedral ceilings, a fireplace, oversized windows, window seating, and a versatile loft. The main level also includes a private office, mudroom, laundry room, and powder room. Upstairs, the spacious primary suite features a walk-in closet and updated bath. Three additional bedrooms and a full bath complete the second level. The walkout lower level offers flexible finished space for a media room, playroom, fitness area, or home office, plus a work area, additional refrigerator, and beverage station.
