About this home

An exceptional opportunity to own a thoughtfully crafted 2015 Colonial in one of Greenwich&apos;s most desirable in-town neighborhoods. Set on level grounds at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac, this home offers four finished levels of natural light, high ceilings and refined finishes throughout. The heart of the home is an expansive kitchen with a large center island that flows effortlessly into the family room and opens through French doors to a private terrace and backyard. Five en-suite bedrooms, including a flexible lower-level guest suite, provide comfort and privacy for family and visitors alike. Spaces for work, fitness and recreation, with premium features including a whole-house generator, HEPA air filtration and water filtration systems make this home as functional as it is beautiful.
