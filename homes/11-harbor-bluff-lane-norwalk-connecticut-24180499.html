About this home

Commanding panoramic views of Long Island Sound, this extraordinary coastal retreat offers a front-row seat to the dramatic interplay of sea, sky &amp; light. Thoughtfully designed by Louise Brooks of Brooks &amp; Falotico, the residence blends timeless elegance w/ exceptional craftsmanship and over 9,000 sqft of refined living space across 4 elevator-serviced levels. Designed for both relaxed living &amp; sophisticated entertaining, the home features a stunning chef&apos;s kitchen w/ professional-grade appliances, fireplace &amp; imported French heated stone floors. Sun-filled living &amp; dining rooms offer French doors that lead to a private terrace overlooking Tavern Island, Rowayton Harbor, &amp; the sparkling Sound. 5 beautifully appointed en-suite bedrooms provide comfort &amp; privacy, including a luxurious primary suite w/ a recently renovated spa bath, steam shower, soaking tub, custom vanities &amp; walk-in closets. A spacious private office with built-ins offers a serene work-from-home setting, and guests can enjoy a charming 3rd fl. bedroom w/bath. A spectacular top-floor entertainment space w/ kitchenette opens to a deck with sweeping waterviews for gatherings &amp; sunsets. The lower level is dedicated to wellness &amp; recreation, featuring a gym, sauna, wineroom, &amp; abundant storage. Nestled within Harbor Bluff, an exclusive enclave of just 12 custom homes, residents enjoy a lock-and-leave lifestyle w/ the privacy of a Single-family residence. Coastal Connecticut living at its finest.
