About this home

Timeless elegance meets modern comfort in this stunning 1936 brick Colonial, beautifully updated while preserving the warmth, character, and craftsmanship of its vintage roots. Set in one of Westport&apos;s most desirable coastal locations, this 5-bedroom, 4.5-bath antique home is filled with architectural detail and storybook charm at every turn. Graceful arched doorways, a curved staircase, detailed moldings, leaded decorative windows, cozy window seats, original crystal and brass hardware, and three fireplaces create an atmosphere that feels both refined and deeply welcoming. Each room has been thoughtfully refreshed with an eye toward comfort, function, and timeless style, while honoring the home&apos;s original soul. Outdoor living is equally inviting, with lovely gardens, a spacious patio, a built-in grill, and a firepit perfect for relaxed evenings under the stars. Enjoy an exceptional Westport lifestyle just moments from Compo Beach, Longshore Club, the train, and Saugatuck&apos;s beloved restaurants and shops. The result is a rare blend of old-world beauty and updated livability - a home that feels elegant yet warm, sophisticated yet inviting. 5 Farnham Court is a truly special Westport residence for those who appreciate character, quality, location, and enduring design.
