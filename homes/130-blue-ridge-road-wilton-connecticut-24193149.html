About this home

Beautifully renovated and impeccably maintained, this sun-filled 3-4 bedroom home offers timeless style, quality craftsmanship, and exceptional indoor-outdoor living on a picturesque, meadow-like property. Soaring ceilings, walls of windows, and an open, inviting layout create the perfect setting for everyday living and entertaining. The chef&apos;s kitchen features premium cabinetry, quartz countertops, a large center island, Wolf six-burner cooktop, Bosch wall ovens, and a walk-in pantry, flowing seamlessly into the beamed family room with wide-board floors, wood-burning fireplace, and French doors to an expansive stone patio complete with multiple seating areas, a custom outdoor fireplace, and spa. The main level includes two bedrooms, a renovated full bath, and a flexible den with fireplace and built-ins that can serve as a fourth bedroom or home office. Upstairs, the private primary suite features vaulted ceilings, a walk-in closet, and a beautifully appointed ensuite bath. Recent improvements include a new 50-year roof, high-efficiency propane boiler, sump pump system, renovated basement, CarbonArmor garage wall stabilization, polyaspartic garage floor coating, and fresh interior paint. Central air, whole-house generator, two-car garage, and a level driveway complete this move-in ready home, ideally located just minutes from Wilton&apos;s town center, top-rated schools, and commuting routes.
