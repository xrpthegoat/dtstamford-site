About this home

Prominently situated at the end of a private enclave surrounded by nature is where you will find 4 Nappa Lane. This beautifully renovated home offers a serene escape while being perfectly positioned just minutes from everything Westport has to offer. Step inside to discover a bright, open interior where modern updates meet effortless style. The heart of the home features a fully renovated kitchen that flows seamlessly into spacious living areas, all framed by large windows and sliders to the outdoor deck that bring the lush greenery of the outdoors in. Each room has been thoughtfully refreshed with refinished hard wood floors, beautifully tiled kitchen and baths and a neutral palette, creating a sophisticated backdrop for any lifestyle. The primary suite serves as a tranquil retreat, complemented by an additional en-suite, and 2 other well-appointed bedrooms that offer versatility and flexibility. A partially finished walkout lower level offers a flexible space for work or play. The back garden provides a private oasis for relaxing or entertaining. With its blend of modern luxury and a secluded, natural setting, this home is a rare rental opportunity in a prime location.
