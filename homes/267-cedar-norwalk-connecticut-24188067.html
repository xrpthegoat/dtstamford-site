About this home

Skyview is a conveniently located condominium community in West Norwalk, close to shopping, dining, transportation, and major commuter routes. Set on the second floor toward the rear of the complex, Unit 2D offers a bright, private setting with a comfortable open layout. This two bedroom unit features recessed lighting, a fireplace, a newer kitchen, an updated bathroom, and a brand new in unit washer and dryer. Windows in every room bring in natural light and refreshing seasonal breezes. Step outside to a private balcony, perfect for morning coffee or relaxing at the end of the day. An attached garage with additional storage is located directly below the unit. Commuters will appreciate quick access to I 95, as well as nearby South Norwalk and Darien train stations. A local bus route also provides connections to the train stations, Stamford, and surrounding areas. Norwalk Community College is just minutes away. Pets may be considered with landlord approval and a $350 annual pet rent. No smoking. Applicants must provide a credit check (720+ score required), proof of income and employment, and professional references.
