About this home

31 Gray Farms Rd nails the wish list for today&apos;s buyer: new construction, high-end finishes, space to entertain, and a location that can&apos;t be beat. Over an acre in Stamford&apos;s Newfield area, the home is surrounded by other new builds and minutes to trains MetroNorth (NYC)/Amtrak, I-95, the Merritt Parkway, JCC, Sterling Farms, and houses of worship. Inside, a bright two-story foyer opens to an oversized living room, formal dining that can expand for large gatherings, and a showpiece kitchen with a huge island, breakfast room, Sub-Zero/Wolf appliances, walk-in pantry + butler&apos;s pantry. The kitchen overlooks a spacious family room with a gas fireplace and direct access to the rear deck, making this entire main floor perfect for everyday living or entertaining. A first-floor en suite bedroom and recessed powder room offer flexibility for guests or extended family. Upstairs, the vaulted primary suite includes two walk-in closets, a sitting area, and a spa bath with soaking tub, heated floors, and oversized shower. Four more bedrooms, three baths, laundry room w/slop sink, wide landing and MANY closets complete the upstairs. Finished walk-out lower level has high ceilings, rec room, gym/media space, bedroom, full bath, and mudroom to the oversized epoxy garage. Backyard has flat multi-tiered areas blending perfectly into that New England vibe. Modern systems throughout make this home move-in ready and built for how people actually live.
