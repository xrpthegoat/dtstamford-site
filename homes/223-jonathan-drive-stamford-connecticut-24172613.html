About this home

Set on 2.77 manicured acres in North Stamford, 223 Jonathan Drive is a stately red brick colonial that seamlessly blends classic elegance with modern updates. Set back from the quiet road, the timeless facade creates a grand first impression, leading to the home&apos;s gracious foyer with sweeping staircase. Offering nearly 5000 square feet of living space, this extensively renovated home features 6 bedrooms, 4 full and 1 half baths. The updated kitchen boasts all-new cabinetry and appliances, while the luxurious primary suite includes a spa-like bath with soaking tub, double vanities, glass shower, and walk-in closet. Enjoy the private, resort-like backyard with a beautiful pool - perfect for summer living and entertaining. New landscaping, walkways, and patio enhance the outdoor setting. Also included within the majestic property is a barn with two horse stalls. The basement offers great flex space and a gym. Elegant, updated, and set in a serene North Stamford location, this is a rare offering that combines space, sophistication, and lifestyle.
