About this home

99 Hickok Road is a beautifully updated and meticulously maintained Dutch Colonial set on stunning landscaped property with exceptional privacy and outdoor living. Thoughtfully improved over the years, the home blends timeless elegance with modern comfort and functionality. Designed for both everyday living and entertaining, the home features gracious formal and informal spaces, a paneled first-floor office, updated kitchen with premium appliances, first-floor ensuite bedroom, new HVAC, whole-house generator, and smart-home/Sonos integration. The flexible second floor includes a spacious primary suite with sitting area and walk-in closet, two bedrooms with Jack-and-Jill bath, a large bonus/media room, and an additional flexible room ideal as a second office or potential fifth bedroom. A newly added full bath and second-floor laundry enhance convenience and versatility. Outdoor spaces feature bluestone patios and walkways, Belgian block detailing, mature landscaping, irrigation, lighting, and a newly paved driveway-creating a private, resort-like setting perfect for both relaxation and entertaining. Exceptionally cared for and turnkey, this pristine home is set on one of New Canaan&apos;s picturesque tree-lined streets.
