About this home

Build your dream house on this beautiful property close to town in the Old Hill section of Westport. This .87 acre lot in a AA zone has views of town and river through the trees. There is a small single-family home that was converted into two apartments in the 1930s by its then-owners. This can be a perfect ADU opportunity as part of a new house to be built. Have an office on one level and an in-law suite on the other. The land is rolling, elevated, and ideally positioned high off Kings Highway North. This is a rare and exciting opportunity for the next owner who can create something truly special. Each level in the house has a separate entrance and features a living room, one-bedroom, one-bath and eat-in kitchen. A truly unique offering with potential for flexible living opportunities. House is being sold AS IS. Please perform diligence with the town for required building regulations. Sewer hookup available on Kings Highway and that assessment has been paid in full.
