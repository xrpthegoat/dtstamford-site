About this home

64 Sanford Lane, a combination of design and functionality was meticulously renovated in late 2024, offering 6,103 sq ft of functional space. Situated on a quiet, tree-lined street, it offers 7 bedrooms &amp; 5.5 bathrooms on a flat, corner lot in the popular neighborhood of Pepper Ridge, known as Case Acre Estates. The main level is an open flow built for everyday living and large-scale entertaining. The gourmet kitchen is equipped with 2 ovens, 2 dishwashers, a large stainless farmhouse double sink, and an island with seating for 5. The walk-in chef&apos;s pantry has 2 additional refrigerators. Seamless indoor-outdoor living which transitions to a covered front porch, a wrap-around porch, and a generous deck featuring a large pergola. The main level has a warm dining room with a fireplace, a living room with a stone fireplace, a den that opens to the kitchen, and two flexible bedrooms with a semi-en suite bath that was built with ADA measurements in mind. The upper level features 5 bedrooms, highlighted by 2 primary suites. The primary suite is spacious, with 2 full-beds, a couch, 2 walk-in closets, a claw-foot soaking tub, and a large walk-in shower. A finished, walkout basement provides space for a kid cave recreation, media, and wellness options. Additions include a separate laundry/folding station, an over sized two bay garage, a mudroom cubby area, a whole-house generator, and a private outdoor shower. Located equal distance from I-95 and the Merritt and walk to everything!
