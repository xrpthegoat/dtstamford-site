About this home

Custom condominium completed spring 2021 with ideal location downtown in the Village of New Canaan. Available for rent starting 4/07/2026. Can be viewed starting 04/07/2026. Short walk to shops, restaurants, grocery stores, and train. Featuring 9ft. ceilings and hardwood floors throughout. First floor includes custom kitchen with imported stainless steel appliances, quartz countertops, and wine fridge. Sun-filled family room features a gas fireplace, Samsung smart TV, custom built-ins and floating shelving. The first floor study/bedroom includes custom built-ins and is the perfect home office for working remotely. Full bathroom off the foyer provides the flexibility to use the study as a fourth bedroom, if desired. The second floor includes the main bedroom boasting a gas fireplace, large closet, and luxurious bath with soaking tub and shower as well as another bedroom and en-suite bathroom. Ask about pets. Criminal, credit and eviction search required. Images include staged furniture which is no longer present at the property.
