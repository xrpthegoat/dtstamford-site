About this home

Fully Furnished, WATERFRONT PARADISE! Tranquil location offers unrivaled access to Panoramic Ocean views of Long Island Sound. A short walk across the Wooden Footbridge w/ Sherwood Mill Pond on your left and LI Sound on your right leads to the gated private Island of Compo Mill Cove &amp; a handful of unique waterfront homes. The property itself has been customized from top to bottom w/ exceptional thought &amp; detail. Only house on the Cove w/ a secure, storm surge concrete wall. Featuring over 3950 sq ft of living space, 5 beds, 5.2 baths, an open floor plan w/ wall to wall windows that offer 180 degree views of the ocean from every room. First fl features a living room w/ fireplace, family room, beautifully appointed wet bar, dining area, &amp; gourmet kitchen w/ exclusive granite, Viking appliances, modern design, &amp; a first fl bedroom w/ ensuite bath. Upstairs offers 3 additional bedrooms including a primary w/ a fireplace, private balcony w/ outdoor fireplace, &amp; bathroom w/ sauna shower, tub, &amp; double vanity. Highest quality materials were used in every corner of the home, from beautiful marble to custom imported Italian sici tiles, to state of the art electronic &amp; digital components that can be used from within the home or remotely no detail was overlooked. Outdoors is equally as impressive w/ an infinity pool, outdoor kitchen, shower, terrace w/ fireplace, lush gardens, &amp; beautiful landscaping that sit behind a gated, secure stone wall border. 1 hr from NY
