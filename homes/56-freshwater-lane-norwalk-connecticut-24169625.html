About this home

Nestled at the end of a cul-de-sac and bordering Cranbury Park, this exceptional custom-built 2013 residence offers an extraordinary blend of luxury, privacy, and resort-style living.Spanning 4,698 SF, the home features 3 generous bedrooms,3 full and 1 half baths,plus a flexible finished space, ideal for a 4th bedroom, guest suite, or home office.Designed with an open-concept layout, custom millwork, and refined finishes throughout, the home exudes timeless elegance and modern comfort.The gourmet eat-in kitchen showcases marble countertops, an oversized center island,custom cabinetry,premium appliances, and a spacious pantry,flowing into the dining and living areas for effortless entertaining.The living room opens directly to the patio and heated saltwater pool,creating a seamless indoor-outdoor lifestyle.Additional main-level highlights include a spacious family room, private office w/custom built-ins, mudroom, oversized laundry room with dual washers and dryers, plus an attached 3-car garage.The luxurious first-floor primary suite offers tranquil property views, an expansive walk-in dressing room, spa-like bath, and direct pool access. Upstairs, a generous den opens to a balcony overlooking the grounds, while two ensuite bedrooms feature walk-in closets. Prof. landscaped grounds include irrigation, a fenced garden and a picturesque, stocked pond with fountain and fishing access.This home has all the bells and whistles.Convenient to the train and major highways!
