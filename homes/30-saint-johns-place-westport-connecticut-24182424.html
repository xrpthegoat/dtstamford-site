About this home

Saint John Place is the street people who know Westport quietly hope opens up. It almost never does. Reimagined in 2007 by Renato Gasparian Associates and refreshed in 2022, this house has all the right moves. Inside, the main level opens up the way you hope it will. The great room stretches nearly thirty-four feet and flows into the dining area and a kitchen built around a center island, with a custom walk-in pantry right behind it. A sun room pulls in the morning light. There&apos;s a separate family room, plus a real office with a door that closes. Upstairs, five bedrooms give everyone somewhere to land. The primary suite has a spa bath with a soaking tub, a separate shower, and two walk-in closets (because who likes to share?). The laundry is up here too, in its own room. The outdoor spaces close the deal. A bluestone patio wraps around a good portion of the house with a stone fireplace. The deep flat lot is screened by mature plantings and gives you the privacy people leave town to find. You&apos;re in the heart of Westport&apos;s in-town neighborhood, which means the car can stay in the driveway. Walk to a different cuisine every night, the shops, the Library, the Westport Country Playhouse, summer nights at the Levitt Pavilion. The Saugatuck River is right there, and a celebrated omakase counter is a few minutes on foot. When you do want the car, the beach is a short drive away. Beautiful house, easy life, almost everything a walk from the door. Tour online @ 30saintjohn.com
