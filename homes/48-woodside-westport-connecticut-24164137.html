About this home

A rare opportunity to own new construction in Westport&apos;s coveted Old Hill neighborhood, offering refined design, a swimming pool, and a walkable lifestyle near Post Road and downtown. Tucked within one of Westport&apos;s most sought-after enclaves, this newly constructed 6-bedroom, 5.5-bath home blends architectural sophistication with modern livability. Natural light, clean lines, and well-proportioned spaces create an environment that feels both elevated and effortlessly comfortable. At the center of the home, a beautifully designed kitchen opens to expansive living and dining areas, creating a seamless flow for both everyday living and entertaining. The layout flows easily with ample room for work-from-home, multi-generational living, and evolving lifestyle needs. A main-level suite with a full bath provides privacy and versatility-ideal for guests, a dedicated office, or an extended family. Every detail accentuates comfort, function, and understated luxury. A back yard patio with pool abutting a nature preserve offers privacy with open space and the ease of in-town living. Positioned near Nash Pond and close to local nature trails and amenities, the home also offers exceptional convenience-walkable to the Post Road and just minutes to downtown Westport, shopping, dining, and Metro-North train service to New York City. Luxury new construction in Old Hill, Westport, CT-where location, design, and lifestyle align in one of the town&apos;s most desirable settings.
