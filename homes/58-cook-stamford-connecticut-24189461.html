About this home

Davenport Point | Long Island Sound Views on a Private Peninsula. A rare chance to build a stone&apos;s throw from the water. Set on a private peninsula just past the Old Greenwich line, this level, buildable lot opens to wide, year-round views of Long Island Sound and Stamford Harbor from its upper elevations, with sailboats and moorings scattered across the channel and the far shore beyond. Graded and ready for your architect, it is a trophy site in a market where land this close to the water almost never comes available. Whether you build your own waterfront home or a spec residence, this parcel lets you start clean. The 0.41 acre flat lot (17,860 square feet) has natural gas, city water, and city sewer already run to it. Design a coastal home up to roughly 7,000 square feet, subject to town approvals, and set the upper floors and rooftop deck to frame the water. Bring your own architect and builder. No builder tie-in, no compromise on your vision. Life here runs on the water. Launch from the neighborhood dock and spend long summer afternoons sailing out across the Sound. Walk to John J. Boccuzzi Park and its tennis courts, beach, marina, kayak and paddleboard rentals, and shoreline trails. Enjoy a sunset dinner at Prime, right on the harbor, or hop the seasonal Harbor Point water taxi across the channel. Private roads, an active association, and a full calendar of seasonal events give the neighborhood a real sense of place.
