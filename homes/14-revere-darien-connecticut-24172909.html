About this home

This beautifully renovated 5,200-square-foot home offers an exceptional blend of timeless elegance and modern luxury in one of Darien&apos;s most coveted central locations. Step inside to discover a spacious, light-filled open floor plan designed for both everyday living and effortless entertaining. The inviting family room with fireplace flows seamlessly into the gourmet kitchen and expansive breakfast area, creating the heart of the home, while an additional oversized living space added during the 2019 expansion offers incredible flexibility for relaxing, gathering, or entertaining. A dedicated office provides a quiet workspace, and the beautifully appointed formal dining room is ideal for more elegant occasions. Every bathroom in the home has been thoughtfully redesigned with high-end finishes, and the residence was extensively expanded and renovated in 2019. Upstairs, five generous bedrooms provide plenty of space for family and guests, including a retreat-like primary suite complete with a spa-inspired bath. Outside, a handsome bluestone patio overlooks the private yard - perfect for morning coffee, summer barbecues, or relaxing evenings at home. Ideally situated in the heart of Darien, this move-in-ready home is just a short walk to top-rated schools, parks, town, and train, offering a rare opportunity to own a meticulously renovated home in one of Darien&apos;s most desirable locations
