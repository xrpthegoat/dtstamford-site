About this home

Welcome to The Benjamin Weed House, a distinguished and storied estate set across 3.5 exquisite acres-where historic provenance meets refined modern living. Expanded and meticulously curated over time, this exceptional five-bed, four-bath residence offers a rare blend of architectural integrity and contemporary sophistication. Inside, soaring ceilings, wide-plank hardwood floors, and masterful craftsmanship create an atmosphere of understated elegance. The chef&apos;s kitchen is both functional and beautiful, appointed with a Sub-Zero refrigerator, Wolf cooktop, granite countertops, and an expansive dining area designed for effortless entertaining. The grounds are nothing short of extraordinary-evoking the romance of an English country garden with fountain, mature specimen plantings, hydrangeas, and peonies woven throughout the landscape. A collection of thoughtfully designed outdoor amenities elevates the estate to a private retreat: a newly constructed post-and-beam barn in pine and cedar, a fully renovated guest house with kitchen and bath, a tennis court, and a resort-style gunite pool. The stone terrace-complete with fireplace, grill, and pergola-creates an idyllic setting for al fresco dining. A detached 3 car garage is complemented by a beautifully finished second-floor flex space, ideal for a studio, office, or wellness retreat. The new guest house offers a luxurious and private accommodation for extended family and guests.
