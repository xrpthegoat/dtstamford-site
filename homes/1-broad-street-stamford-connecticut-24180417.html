About this home

Elevate your lifestyle in this stunning 12th-floor residence at Park Tower, one of downtown Stamford&apos;s premier luxury high-rise communities. Offering approximately 1,112 square feet of thoughtfully designed living space, this sophisticated one-bedroom, one-and-a-half-bath home combines modern elegance with comfort. The sun-filled open-concept layout features rich flooring, oversized windows, and a private terrace with breathtaking views of the city skyline and nearby park. The gourmet kitchen is designed for both everyday living and entertaining, showcasing granite countertops, a center island, stainless steel appliances, a newly installed dishwasher, and a wine refrigerator. The spacious primary suite serves as a private retreat, complete with generous closet space and a luxurious en-suite bath featuring a dual-sink granite vanity, walk-in shower, and separate soaking tub. A convenient in-unit washer and dryer further enhance the home&apos;s functionality. Residents of Park Tower enjoy an exceptional collection of amenities, including 24-hour concierge and doorman, a state-of-the-art fitness center, indoor pool, resident lounge, secure building access, and shuttle service to Metro-North. Ideally located in the heart of downtown Stamford, this residence places you just moments from acclaimed restaurants, shopping, entertainment venues, transportation, and Mill River Park, offering the perfect blend of luxury, convenience, and urban living.
