About this home

Sport Lovers Dream - Set at end of a quiet cul-de-sac on Stillman Lane, this custom-built 2013 residence offers 4 finished levels of thoughtfully designed living space on 1.2 level acres w putting green. High ceilings, abundant natural light and seamless flow define the main level, where formal living and dining rooms connect effortlessly to an expansive open-concept kitchen and family room with fireplace. Upstairs, find a primary suite with spa-inspired bath with heated floors, whirlpool tub + steam shower plus 4 en-suite bedrooms. The walkout LL is centered around recreation + wellness w gym, golf simulator, indoor putting area, glice for skating on, rec room + wine rooms. Professionally designed landscaping by James Doyle, expansive flat lawn + outdoor kitchen complete the property.
