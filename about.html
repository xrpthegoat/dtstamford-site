About John

John works the corridor he's named for: the condo towers, rental buildings, and homes in and around downtown Stamford. Day to day that means pricing and marketing sales listings, setting renters and buyers up on real-time MLS searches so new matches reach them the moment they list, and negotiating on the buy side across Stamford's neighborhoods and the wider Fairfield County market.

Rather than run a practice on printed flyers and stale portals, he built his own tooling — dtstamford.com's every-3-hours MLS refresh — so clients browse the same live inventory he does. Off the clock, that same instinct produced StamfordLive.com, the community events map he co-founded to make it easier to find what's happening around the city.

He is licensed by the State of Connecticut (RE.0817347) and practices under broker Jarah Tuttle LLC. Clients have rated the work 5.0 across 89 Google reviews.
