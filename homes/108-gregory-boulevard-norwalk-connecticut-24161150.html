About this home

Enjoy the ideal waterfront living in the enchanting community of Marvin Beach surrounded by the beauty of Long Island Sound, Gregory Point, Taylor Farm, and the Norwalk Harbor. This amazing 4 bedroom 3 1/2 bath Nantucket Style Colonial has been completely remodeled by an established Fairfield County custom builder and designed for indoor/outdoor entertaining, extended family, or working from home. With a blend of classic design, timeless appeal, modern upscale amenities, and open concept the home is an ideal haven for creating lasting memories and enjoying nature at it&apos;s best. The first floor includes porch access to the formal living room with custom central staircase, powder room, large open gourmet kitchen with with island, formal dining area, and family room. Just a few steps away from the family room is the private great room, mudroom, and 2 car garage. The second floor offers a private primary suite that includes dramatic High ceiling, oversized walk-in closet, en-suite bathroom with separate shower and double vanity, and laundry room just steps away. Exit the master bedroom suite to 3 large additional bedrooms including guest room suite with full bath. All rooms have oversized windows for plenty of sunshine and 4 inch select grade white oak. Just a few of the features include quartz countertops, stainless steel appliances, custom staircase, and high end tile and fixtures.
