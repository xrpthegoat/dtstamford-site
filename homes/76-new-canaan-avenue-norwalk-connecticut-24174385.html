About this home

Welcome to the desirable Birches condo complex! This light-filled and spacious 2-bedroom, 2-bath corner unit features a primary bedroom suite with its own private bath, plus an additional full hall bath. This move-in-ready, first-floor condo offers an open layout with hardwood floors throughout, a gourmet kitchen with granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, custom cabinetry, and a large island perfect for entertaining. Sliders lead to an expansive private patio and garden area, offering great indoor-outdoor living. Additional highlights include in-unit laundry, central air, a security system, elevator access, private storage space, and an underground garage with 2 parking spaces. Pet friendly (2 pets per unit allowed, 50lbs or under). Conveniently located just minutes to I-95, the Merritt Parkway, shopping, restaurants, parks, and public transportation-ideal for commuters!
