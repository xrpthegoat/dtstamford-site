About this home

This immaculate and lovely home in the desirable Spring Hill area offers a thoughtful layout with flexible living space throughout. The main floor features a brand-new kitchen with all new appliances, a formal dining room with plenty of space for entertainment, and a primary bedroom as well. Neutral tile flooring runs through the kitchen and dining room, while the rest of the main floor features beautiful hardwood floors. An office or bedroom and a full bathroom complete this level. With second floor flexibility, you will find three additional spacious bedrooms and full bath, making it easy to create a second primary suite if desired. There is also extra space that could be used as a den or possible kitchenette, offering potential for an in-law setup or income-producing apartment with a separate entrance. A good-sized deck off the second floor provides a bird&apos;s-eye view of the neighborhood. The landscaped yard features irrigation, perennial plantings, and a lovely fenced-in area. This home is ideally located for commuters, with quick access to Route 95, Route 25, Metro-North and the Merritt Parkway, along with many nearby amenities. This property with five bedrooms must be seen in person to fully appreciate its condition and versatile possibilities.
