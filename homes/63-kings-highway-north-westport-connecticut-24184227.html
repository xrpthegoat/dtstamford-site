About this home

Welcome to 63 Kings Hwy North in Westport! A yearly furnished rental with an inviting front porch welcomes you home to this light-filled historic four bedroom cape within walking distance to the wonderful town of Westport. The front to back living room boasts a wood burning fireplace for cozy evenings and a large dining room is great for entertaining family and guests. The renovated kitchen will delight the cook in the family. Spend time on the back patio overlooking a large open garden area. Enjoy all that Westport has to offer its&apos; residents, to include exceptional public and private schools. Enjoy the Longshore Golf Course, the Longshore Club Park with three pools and tennis courts. Compo Beach for summer fun. The Westport Playhouse. And a walking riverfront downtown featuring gourmet restaurants and boutique shops. All this within one hour to NYC. Owner prefers to rent the home furnished yearly, but could be available unfurnished for $6,500 per year, or ask about for six months.
