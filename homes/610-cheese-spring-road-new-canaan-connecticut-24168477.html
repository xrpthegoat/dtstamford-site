About this home

Welcome to this stunning custom shingle-style residence set amid sweeping lawns, mature flowering trees, and beautiful natural surroundings in one of New Canaan&apos;s most picturesque settings.This exceptional 4/5-bedroom residence blends timeless New England architecture with modern living and refined architectural detail throughout. The cathedral-ceiling living room with fireplace, walls of windows, and sliders to the deck serves as an inviting and dramatic centerpiece. The open-concept kitchen, breakfast area, and family room are thoughtfully designed for everyday living and entertaining, opening seamlessly to the side porch and terrace overlooking the majestic property. A formal dining room lends warmth and sophistication, complemented by a first-floor guest bedroom and office. Upstairs features 3 additional bedrooms, including one with a balcony overlooking the breathtaking property, plus a lounge and skylit study area. The expansive walk-out lower level offers 1,600+ square feet with recreation room, full bath, and wine cellar. Outside, enjoy peaceful entertaining spaces, a beautiful Wagner gunite pool with gazebo, and a classic red barn with stalls, all surrounded by vibrant seasonal landscaping that enhances the property&apos;s natural beauty year round. A rare offering that combines beautiful grounds, timeless charm, and an exceptional lifestyle, all just minutes from town, schools, and the train.
