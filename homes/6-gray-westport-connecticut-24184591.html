About this home

**Sellers request all offers to be submitted by Sunday 6/28 at noon.** A WATERFRONT offering in one of Westport&apos;s most coveted enclaves. This beautifully composed estate is gracefully sited on more than two private, tree-lined acres, encompassing sweeping views of Sasco Creek, Southport Beach, and Long Island Sound while preserving an exceptional sense of seclusion. Designed to maximize its remarkable setting, the home captures water views from nearly every room and showcases timeless architectural details including custom millwork, random-width hardwood flooring, three fireplaces, cathedral ceilings, and formal and informal living spaces rarely seen in today&apos;s homes.
