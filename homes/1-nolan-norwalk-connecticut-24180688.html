About this home

Welcome to 1 Nolan Street, a classic 2-bedroom Cape Cod on a quiet street in Norwalk, offering 768 square feet of single-level living with expansion potential above. Enter through the front door into a living room that anchors the front of the home, while two bedrooms with closets are positioned on the opposite side, sharing a full bathroom in the hallway. The kitchen has a beautiful backsplash, some newer appliances, and newer cabinets that flow into a bright sun room with direct access to a rear patio, perfect for morning coffee or outdoor dining. The walk-up, fiberglass-insulated attic features gabled dormers and is ready to be finished, providing significant expansion potential for additional living space. The lower level houses a garage, laundry hookups, utility sink, and designated utility areas for mechanicals. Forced hot water oil heat and an aqua booster support the home&apos;s systems. Recent improvements include a 2024 driveway and 2025 water drainage system in the yard. The amazing lot offers outdoor space and the convenience of a patio for entertaining--it&apos;s almost flat and among the best yard for homes in this area. Located on a peaceful street with easy access to shopping including Angelo&apos;s Specialties, Liz Sues Bagels and local amenities, this home presents a unique opportunity to create your vision in a peaceful Norwalk neighborhood at an unbeatable price.
