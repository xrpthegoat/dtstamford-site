About this home

The best vacations are the ones you never have to leave. Set on the shores of Compo Mill Cove in the heart of Westport&apos;s coveted Compo Beach neighborhood, this exceptional FEMA compliant Michael Greenberg-designed waterfront home offers the ultimate coastal lifestyle. State-of-the-art systems, including geothermal heating and cooling and tankless hot water, provide exceptional efficiency and year-round comfort. Begin your mornings with a walk along Compo Beach, spend afternoons moving between your private beach and pool, and end the day dining on the waterfront terrace beneath the stars or strolling over to one of Westport&apos;s renowned restaurants. Designed to embrace the water from every angle, the home features exposed beams, wide-plank hardwood floors, and water views from nearly every room. The chef&apos;s kitchen opens seamlessly to spacious living and dining areas, creating an effortless flow for entertaining. Offering five bedrooms and three-and-a-half baths, there&apos;s plenty of room for family and guests to experience one of Westport&apos;s most coveted waterfront settings. Rarely does a home combine architectural pedigree, resort-style amenities, and an irreplaceable location so beautifully. This is waterfront living at its finest.
