About this home

Welcome to 2 Point Road, a rare offering within the gated waterfront community of Wilson Point. Nestled on an extraordinary property surrounded by mature landscaping, this architecturally significant mid-century modern home was thoughtfully designed to embrace its natural setting, capturing water views and creating a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor living. Filled with natural light and over 5600 sqft of living space, the home features an open floor plan anchored by a stunning modern kitchen, spacious dining area, and inviting living room. At the heart of the residence is a tranquil courtyard, bringing beauty and serenity to everyday living. The first floor includes a private primary suite, home office, and two additional bedrooms. Additional highlights include an oversized mudroom, a separate guest or in-law suite above the three-car garage, and a walkout lower level offering a family room, gym, and direct access to the expansive heated gunite pool and spa and surrounding grounds. Wilson Point offers a truly exceptional lifestyle with private beach access, tennis, a clubhouse, dock and boat mooring options and unmatched privacy within one of Fairfield County&apos;s most sought-after waterfront associations. A rare opportunity to own a timeless home in an extraordinary setting.
