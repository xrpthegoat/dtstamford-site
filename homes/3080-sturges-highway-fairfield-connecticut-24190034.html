About this home

Welcome to 3080 Sturges - Fully Renovated Home set on 2.44 private acres in Fairfield&apos;s Greenfield Hill enclave on the Westport border. Offering a Westport mailing address together with Fairfield&apos;s acclaimed schools and amenities, this 7,317-square-foot modern residence blends architectural scale with contemporary comfort and luxury. A dramatic two-story foyer introduces interiors defined by soaring ceilings, skylights, and oak hardwood floors throughout the home. Just off the foyer, a screened porch offers a peaceful setting for indoor-outdoor living and entertaining. Designed for both everyday living and entertaining, the main living spaces center around a gourmet eat-in kitchen appointed with Sub-Zero refrigeration, a Wolf range, Wolf double ovens, Wolf built-in microwave, Miele dishwasher, Marvel undercounter ice machine, quartz countertops, and a gas fireplace. Complementing the kitchen is a walk-in pantry featuring a second full refrigerator, wine refrigerator, and custom cabinetry for exceptional storage and organization. Adjacent to the kitchen, the formal dining room features custom built-in cabinetry and ample space for entertaining. Additional highlights include a grand living room with dramatic cathedral ceilings, expansive skylights, and exceptional scale, flowing into an adjacent music room with a custom tray ceiling. The home also features a private office with fireplace, billiards lounge, fitness studio, and a second laundry room.
