About this home

Escape to your own private retreat on nearly three acres of lush, rolling land. This beautifully renovated residence offers the perfect balance of secluded tranquility and incredible convenience, situated just moments from Darien and Rowayton, local shopping, and just 1 mile from the Rowayton train station. Inside, experience the effortless flow of one-level living. The space is bathed in natural light, with top-tier craftsmanship and custom cabinetry throughout.
