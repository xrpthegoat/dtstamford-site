About this home

Privately nestled in Westport&apos;s coveted Old Hill neighborhood, this extraordinary five-bedroom New England residence offers a rare opportunity to own a home where timeless character &amp; modern comfort come together beautifully. Thoughtfully updated while preserving its remarkable architectural details, the residence features sun-filled interiors, beautifully scaled rooms, &amp; an inviting warmth throughout. Original elements reflect the home&apos;s storied past, while carefully integrated updates provide exceptional comfort for today&apos;s lifestyle. A standout feature is the distinctive pub room with its original wood bar - an unforgettable setting for intimate gatherings, celebrations, or quiet evenings at home.
