About this home

Welcome to this sun-drenched 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom ranch, perfectly situated on a quiet, highly desirable cul-de-sac in the heart of Mid-Ridges. Offering the perfect blend of suburban tranquility and everyday convenience-located just 5 minutes from the Merritt Parkway and less than 20 minutes to the Stamford train station and I-95. Step inside to an open and inviting floor plan bathed in beautiful natural light. The spacious living room and dining area flow seamlessly, creating an ideal setup for daily living and hosting guests. The bright eat-in kitchen features direct access to a private brick-paver patio, which effortlessly connects to a larger back patio, making indoor-outdoor entertaining a breeze. Down the hall, the primary suite serves as a peaceful retreat complete with double closets and its own en-suite bath. Two additional generously sized bedrooms and a second full hallway bath complete the main level. The living space extends to the finished lower level, featuring two distinct rooms: a second living room and a versatile space ideal for a home office or gym. This level also includes dedicated laundry, a spacious storage room, and direct access inside to your 1-car garage. Outside, enjoy your own sanctuary on an oversized, private .75-acre lot framed by lush greenery and beautiful landscaping, offering endless room to relax or play. *Square footage listed does not include finished lower level.
