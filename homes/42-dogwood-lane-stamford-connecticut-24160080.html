About this home

Exceptional custom-built 4 to 5 bedroom Colonial in desirable North Stamford! This impressive 4,644-square-foot residence welcomes you with a dramatic two-story entry hall and offers sophisticated living on a beautifully landscaped 1.1-acre lot. Four generous bedrooms and four full baths plus one half bath provide ample space for family and guests. A versatile bonus in-law suite over the three-car garage features convenient back stair access, perfect for multi-generational living or au pair accommodations. The prime North Stamford location offers tranquility and privacy while remaining just minutes from the Merritt Parkway for easy commuting. Experience the perfect balance of luxury, functionality, and location in this distinguished home.
