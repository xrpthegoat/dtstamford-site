About this home

Move right into this lovely 2-bedroom townhouse featuring a stunning kitchen and bathroom. The newly renovated finished lower level offers additional living space with a durable luxury vinyl floor. As an end unit this home provides added privacy and an abundance of light. Enjoy the spacious, new back patio and garden area overlooking a charming grassy courtyard - perfect for summer entertaining. There is a new 40-gallon hot water heater. The parking lot is just steps from the unit. The desirable Sylvan Knoll community is conveniently located off of exit 9 on I-95. It is walking distance to Cove Island Park, the beach and Chelsea Piers, and just a short drive to town, shopping, and the train station. Located on 13 park-like acres with sidewalks throughout, Sylvan Knoll is a pet welcome community. Units are all owner occupied. They cannot be rented. Real estate taxes are included in the common charges.
