About this home

Tucked at the end of a private shared driveway far off the main road, this exceptional Wilton colonial has the feel of a quiet cul-de-sac - offering the perfect blend of privacy, space, and convenience. The heart of the home is an expansive great room that flows into a spectacular kitchen - ideal for everyday living and effortless entertaining - with sliders opening to a wraparound deck overlooking a beautiful, flat yard. The formal dining room is made for holidays and dinner parties alike. Upstairs, the generous primary suite is a retreat unto itself, complete with a walk-in closet, full bath, and a private office. Three full bathrooms serve the home, with a flexible fourth bedroom upstairs perfect for guests, a playroom, or a home office. A third garage bay provides excellent storage. Perfectly positioned in the heart of Wilton - just minutes to town center, Georgetown, Cannondale Station, and major highway access.
