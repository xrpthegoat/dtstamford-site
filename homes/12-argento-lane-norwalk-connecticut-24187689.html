About this home

Best of both worlds: The privacy of a stand-alone custom modern farmhouse and the low-maintenance of a thoughtfully designed, HOBI Award-winning planned community, The Preserve. Showcasing exceptional craftsmanship, superior quality, and a trend-forward aesthetic, this home was designed and enhanced from the ground up by the owners offing an ideal blend of luxury and modern amenities. A light-filled open floor plan is enhanced by oversized windows that illuminate wide-plank hardwood floors, artisan millwork, and sophisticated designer finishes throughout. At the heart of the home, the chef&apos;s kitchen features white inset cabinetry, Quartz countertops, premium stainless steel appliances, and a striking contrasting oak center island with dramatic waterfall Quartz top. The kitchen flows effortlessly into the fireplaced family room and the elegant dining area. Sliding glass doors open to a private patio overlooking one of the community&apos;s premier homesites with owner acreage supplemented by open space and fencing. Additional highlights include a dramatic two-story foyer, custom built-in mudroom storage, a luxurious primary suite with dual walk-in closets and a spa-inspired bath, stylish barn doors leading to the laundry room, and a newly FINISHED LOWER LEVEL featuring a full bath, spacious recreation room with hardwood floors and fluted wood focal wall with hidden door to gym. Sited within easy reach of commuting routes, shopping and dining. Only 4 miles to Calf Pasture Beach!
