About this home

Rarely available four bed circa 1900 home in Westport&apos;s coveted Saugatuck neighborhood, thoughtfully updated to blend historic character with modern comfort and energy efficiency. Original architectural details and mill work remain throughout, preserving the charm of its era while offering the upgrades today&apos;s renters seek. Welcoming entry features an enclosed front patio and entrance leading to a well-proportioned living room that flows into the dining area. Upgraded rear mudroom adds functionality. The thoughtfully designed kitchen features a custom wood block island serving as both workspace and a natural gathering place, proportioned with an efficient layout, it maximizes storage and workflow. A new electric range, plus an in-home washer and dryer, add convenience. A finished attic provides flex space for an office, playroom, fitness area, or guest retreat. A private backyard is designed for entertaining, with a beautifully renovated Ipe wood patio for outdoor dining and gatherings, plus a circular stone seating area for relaxing throughout the seasons. A backyard playset is also available for tenants. Owner upgrades include heat pumps, solar panels to help reduce utility costs, upgraded electrical service, central air, dual Bosch compressors, an oil-fired furnace for year-round comfort. Detached one-car garage provides parking and storage. Mins from Metro-North, restaurants, shops, waterfront, and downtown, this home offers exceptional convenience and coastal CT living!
