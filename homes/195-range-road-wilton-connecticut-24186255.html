About this home

This charming home is set on over 2 flat acres in desirable South Wilton and offers the ease of single-level living just minutes from town, schools, and commuter routes. Thoughtfully maintained, the home features 3 bedrooms and 2 full bathrooms, along with a living room with wood-burning fireplace, separate dining room, and kitchen with marble counters. A second living area with a wood stove opens to a screened porch and spacious deck, overlooking the property with ample room for a pool. Recent improvements feature an AC and heating system, hot water heater, garage doors, and new roof. Additional features include a portable generator wired to the house and a 2-car garage. With its rare combination of level acreage, single-level living, and sought-after location, this property presents a special opportunity.
