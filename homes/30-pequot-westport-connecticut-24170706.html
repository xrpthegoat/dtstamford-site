About this home

Coveted Old Hill neighborhood! This stone and shingle 10 room colonial is detailed with custom millwork, recessed lighting and beautiful hardwood floors throughout. The front to back living room with marble fireplace offers peaceful views through expansive windows. The formal dining room with picture window is adjacent to a generous country kitchen featuring a bay window, granite countertops, a gas cooktop and double ovens. The oversized family room boasts a vaulted ceiling and access to main level laundry area. The rear of the house features a 58 ft long deck overlooking private acreage. The home contains 5 bedrooms and 4 full bathrooms. The primary suite includes a full bathroom, dressing area, linen closet and a walk in closet with built-in armoire. This home has fabulous closet space with each bedroom offering a minimum of 2 closets. The walk out lower level hosts a private den / office with wet bar and sliders to a stone patio. There is an additional laundry area and storage space that leads to an oversized 2 bay garage. Central air conditioning will keep you comfortable during summer months while five zone heating, Thermopane windows and extra insulation keep this home energy efficient all year long. Westport&apos;s luxury shops, fine restaurants, relaxing beaches and award winning school system combine to make this an irresistible value!
