About this home

Nestled on a tree-lined road, this storybook Cape offers 3 bedrooms, 1 full and 1 half bath, surrounded by lush grounds offering privacy and timeless character. A welcoming front porch, immersed in nature, is the perfect place to enjoy your morning coffee. The tiered backyard features multiple terraces and a winding path to a hilltop shed ideal as a yoga studio, home office, or creative escape. Inside, a center-island kitchen flows into the breakfast room and living room. The spacious dining room is perfect for entertaining, while the primary suite offers a sitting area, office nook, and gas fireplace. The back patio creates seamless indoor-outdoor living. Easy walk to Binney Park, town, and shopping. Offered for the first time since 1998, this enchanting retreat is truly one of a kind.
