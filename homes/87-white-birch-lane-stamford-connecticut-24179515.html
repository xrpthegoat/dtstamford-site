About this home

Privately sited on a flat .39-acre lot with mature landscaping in Stamford&apos;s peaceful Newfield neighborhood this large, nine-room, four to 5 bedroom, three full bath Cape Cod blends classic charm with exceptional versatility. The heart of this special home is a dramatic wood-paneled family room. Open to the fully equipped kitchen, it features a vaulted, beamed ceiling, picture windows front and back and a cozy wood-burning Vermont Castings stove. A formal living room with its own fireplace and beautiful hardwood flooring and a formal dining room also with the same hardwood flooring and convenient china closet, complete the first floor principal rooms. Flexibility defines the home&apos;s floor plan, offering 4 to 5 bedrooms, including a main-level bedroom ideal as a primary, guest room, or home office and hall bath. Two of the additional 4/5 bedrooms and second of the 3 full baths are located over the garage. Accessed from the family room or a separate entrance at the mudroom, these rooms offer potential for an in-law suite, multi-generational living, or a private home business. The fourth and possible fifth bedroom and third full bath are on the second floor accessed from the main front staircase. This Newfield charmer offers a rare opportunity to enjoy quiet tranquility without sacrificing everyday convenience to Stamford&apos;s best amenities.
