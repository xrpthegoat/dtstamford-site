About this home

Welcome to Sasqua Hills in East Norwalk. Perched high above Shorehaven Golf Club with sweeping views of Long Island Sound, the iconic estate at 7 Sasqua Road is a rare generational treasure. Built in 1950 and lovingly held by the original family for the past 76 years, this remarkable home has been cherished by five generations. Often described as &quot;Old Hollywood Glamour,&quot; the interior has long been a setting for unforgettable gatherings, where Broadway legends and jazz icons once performed in the grand living room. Rich in character and originality, the home offers an extraordinary collection of preserved period details throughout. The inviting family room features warm pecan wood paneling, a custom bar, walls of built-in bookshelves, a fireplace, and a spectacular picture window. The formal dining room is equally impressive, with carved wood built-ins and ample space for a table of twelve. At the heart of the home, the dramatic sunken living room showcases a barrel ceiling, featuring twin baby grand pianos, a wall of full-height windows, and original hardwood floors, creating a one-of-a-kind atmosphere for entertaining and music. The original eat-in kitchen has served as the backdrop for countless family meals and celebrations. A stunning powder room with its original custom mirror niche adds another distinctive architectural touch. A long, dramatic hallway leads to two spacious main-level en-suite bedrooms.
