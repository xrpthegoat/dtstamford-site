About this home

Completely renovated and move in ready, this exceptional three level townhouse in the sought after Fawn Ridge community offers 2 bedrooms, 2 full bathrooms, a one car garage, and a versatile loft. Thoughtfully redesigned with high end finishes, this home combines style, comfort, and functionality. The stunning new kitchen features custom cabinetry, granite countertops, a designer backsplash, and new stainless steel appliances, including a range, dishwasher, and hood. The open concept living and dining area showcases hardwood floors, recessed lighting throughout, custom built ins, and a cozy wood burning fireplace. A new sliding glass door leads to a freshly painted private balcony, perfect for relaxing or entertaining. Both bathrooms have been beautifully renovated. Radiant heated floors in the entry, office, and primary bathroom add everyday comfort. Additional upgrades include upgraded windows, elegant wood staircases, a Nest smart home system, Ring security camera, new garage door, fresh interior paint, and updated lighting throughout. Enjoy resort style amenities including a pool and tennis court, just minutes from Wilton Center, award winning schools, shopping, dining, Metro-North, and major commuting routes. Immediate occupancy available. Move right in and enjoy maintenance free living at its finest.
