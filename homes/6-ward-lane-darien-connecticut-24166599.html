About this home

Summer starts at 6 Ward Lane! Enjoy the pool, clubhouse, and carefree lifestyle at Villager Pond. This bright, airy and freshly painted townhouse is ideally situated less than a mile from Darien Train Station, downtown, shopping, dining, and beaches. Sun-filled living spaces feature an open floor plan connecting the living room and dining area, with a fireplace adding warmth and character. High ceilings and oversized windows enhance the sense of space. The private patio provides an intimate outdoor setting, perfect for morning coffee, afternoon cocktails, or quiet evenings. Kitchen and 1/2 bath complete the main level. Upstairs, the primary suite offers new carpeting, a walk-in closet, and a beautifully updated bath, with a washer and dryer conveniently located adjacent to the suite. An attached garage adds everyday ease, complemented by attic and crawl space storage for added storage. Pets Allowed / No Smoking
