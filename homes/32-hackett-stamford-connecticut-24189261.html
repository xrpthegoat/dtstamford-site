About this home

Excellent opportunity to own a well-maintained two-family home just moments from Downtown Stamford, offering exceptional flexibility for both owner-occupants and investors alike. Each spacious unit offers approximately 1,200+ sq. ft. with a functional layout featuring a bright living/family room, large eat-in kitchen, three generously sized bedrooms, and a full bath. Both units are equipped with central air conditioning, in-unit washer and dryer, and separately metered gas and electric utilities. The first-floor unit showcases beautifully refinished hardwood floors, an updated eat-in kitchen with direct access to a covered rear porch, and abundant natural light throughout. The second-floor unit offers an equally spacious floor plan with hardwood flooring, a renovated kitchen, oversized primary bedroom, and excellent storage. The full basement provides separate storage areas, workshop space, and additional utility access. Outside, enjoy off-street parking and a level yard. Ideally situated within walking distance to Downtown Stamford&apos;s vibrant restaurants, shops, entertainment, parks, and the Metro-North train station, with quick access to I-95 for an easy commute. Offering strong rental potential, flexible living arrangements, and an unbeatable location, this is a rare opportunity to own a quality multifamily property in one of Stamford&apos;s most convenient neighborhoods.
