About this home

Prepare to be amazed by this exciting new construction! A collaboration of master European craftsman of Sobot Trims LLC and renowned local builder, Joe Tomas. This thoughtfully designed residence consists of 5900+ SF on 3 finished floors, plus a partially finished lower level. The main level boasts a spacious 15&apos; X 40&apos; Great Room encompassing a state-of-the-art kitchen, informal dining area and family room with gas fireplace. A dramatic 2 story entry opens to a formal dining room with glass wall, which could alternatively function as a home office. An additional &quot;flex&quot; room on the main level could be a playroom, media room or a formal living room. Oversized Marvin windows allow an abundance of natural light in every room. On the second level there is a stunning Primary Bedroom suite with oversized bathroom, 2 walk in closets and an attached flex room for nursery, sitting room or home office. There are 3 additional bedrooms and 2 full baths on this level. On the third level you will find a large space for an additional family room, home theater or play space, a 5th bedroom and full bathroom. The lower level completes the package with a gym and extensive space ready to be designed to meet your needs. This energy efficient home is fueled by propane gas and has spray foam insulation throughout. Outside there is a large bluestone patio that is pre-configured for an outdoor kitchen. Plenty of play area and room for a pool makes the yard a year-round delight.
