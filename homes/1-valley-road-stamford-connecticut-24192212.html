About this home

Welcome home to this beautifully maintained 2-bedroom, 2.5-bath townhouse ideally situated in a quiet cul-de-sac community just minutes from the heart of downtown Stamford. Thoughtfully designed for today&apos;s lifestyle, the spacious floor plan features gleaming hardwood floors, recessed lighting, and a welcoming living room centered around a cozy fireplace. The updated kitchen offers stainless steel appliances, ample cabinetry, and flows effortlessly into the dining and living areas, creating an ideal space for everyday living and entertaining. Upstairs, the generous primary suite includes a large walk-in closet and a private full bath, while the second bedroom is equally spacious and served by an additional full bathroom. The conveniently located second-floor laundry adds everyday ease. Enjoy your morning coffee or unwind in the evening on the covered private balcony. Additional highlights include one reserved underground parking space, extra outdoor parking, and an unbeatable location within walking distance to downtown Stamford&apos;s restaurants, shopping, parks, Metro-North, and public transportation. Move right in and enjoy low-maintenance living in a highly convenient location.
