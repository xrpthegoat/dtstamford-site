About this home

*Highest and Best Deadline Tuesday 6pm* Welcome to 17 Newfield Street, a unique 1955 Cape Cod offering 4 bedrooms and 2 full baths on an amazing lot in Norwalk--including 3 bedrooms upstairs. Step inside to an inviting and cozy living room with fireplace and large front window. The first floor flows from living area to kitchen, which connects to a sun room at the back--perfect for morning coffee or casual dining. A full bathroom and one bedroom complete the main level. Upstairs, three bedrooms share a hall bathroom with tub and shower, plus an office space at the back of the home. The finished layout includes 1,585 square feet with hardwood floors believed to be under the existing carpet. Central air keeps the home comfortable year-round, while forced air oil heat provides warmth in winter. The basement offers two utility rooms with washer-dryer hookups and storage. Outside, enjoy plenty of storage with a detached 1-car garage and a carport adjacent to it. The 0.31-acre property provides space to spread out, with plenty of space for yard games, or expansion of entertaining space with some creativity--it&apos;s a blank slate. This home is close to Norwalk&apos;s shops, great dining, access to I95 and Metro-North. 17 Newfield delivers solid bones, a uniquely private yard, and flexible living space to customize.
