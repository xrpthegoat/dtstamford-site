About this home

Tucked away on a private, serene wooded setting in sought-after South Wilton, this beautifully updated home offers the perfect blend of tranquility and convenience. Enjoy the peaceful sounds of a babbling brook while being just minutes to the train to NYC, major highways, top-rated schools, Wilton Center shopping, and dining. Set on just shy of an acre, the home features a brand-new renovated kitchen, updated bathrooms, hardwood floors, new stainless steel appliances, a spacious primary suite with walk-in closet, and a huge two-car garage. A rare opportunity to enjoy blissful privacy surrounded by nature without sacrificing commuter convenience. Location, location, location.
