About this home

Welcome to the Hayes House, one of Stamford&apos;s most distinguished full-service residences, where sophistication, comfort, and convenience converge. This elegantly updated 1-bedroom, 1-bath condo offers an expansive, sunlit layout with unobstructed east-facing views and a private balcony that spans the full length of the unit. The grand living and dining areas are framed by wall-to-wall windows and finished with rich, refinished hardwood floors, elegant new crown molding, and custom cellular window shades. HOA includes heat and cooling, keeping utility costs low. New HVAC units ensure comfort throughout the seasons. The sleek kitchen opens to the dining area, creating a seamless flow for everyday living &amp;entertaining. The oversized bedroom is a true retreat, easily accommodating a king-sized bed and enhanced by abundant closet space. The bathroom is fully tiled, featuring a timeless shower/tub combination and a spacious linen closet at its entry. Residents of the Hayes House enjoy a curated lifestyle with amenities including a 24-hour doorman, reserved garage parking, fitness center, community lounge, private storage locker and an outdoor courtyard with patio seating and grilling stations. This pet-friendly building is ideally located just a few blocks from the heart of Downtown Stamford, with quick access to top-rated restaurants, nightlife, parks, and cultural venues. With the Metro North to NYC minutes away, this refined home delivers city living at its finest!
