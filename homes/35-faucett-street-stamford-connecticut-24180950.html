About this home

Vintage Charm Meets Modern Living! This sunny Glenbrook Colonial blends classic character with today&apos;s updates and is ready to impress. The bright main level features hardwood floors, custom moldings, a cozy living room with wood-burning fireplace, formal dining room with bay window, and a modern kitchen with stainless steel appliances and pass-through window. A stylish half bath and heated front porch offer great year-round bonus space or a home office. Upstairs includes 3 spacious bedrooms, an updated full bath, and a walk-up attic with potential to finish. The large finished basement adds flexible living space with a kitchenette, laundry area, and room for a gym, office, or playroom. Outside, enjoy a low-maintenance patio, private backyard with mature plantings, privacy hedge, garden space, vinyl siding, efficient gas heat, and detached 1-car garage. Set on a quiet dead-end street and prime corner lot in desirable Glenbrook, just a short walk to the train, Hope Street shops/restaurants, Stark Elementary, downtown Stamford, and more. Move-in ready with the perfect blend of charm and updates.
