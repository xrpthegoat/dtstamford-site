About this home

Welcome to Bishop Meadows, one of Stamford&apos;s most desirable Cove-area communities. This spacious three-level townhouse has approximately 1,700 square feet of living space. The main level has hardwood floors, a large living room with fireplace and sliders to a private deck, separate dining area, kitchen with abundant cabinetry and counter space, and a half bath. Upstairs are two generously sized bedrooms and two full baths, including a primary bedroom with private bath. The finished lower level adds approximately 640 square feet of flexible living space, ideal for a family room, home office, gym or guest space. Complex amenities include an in-ground pool and clubhouse. Conveniently located near Cummings Beach, parks, shopping, dining and transportation.
