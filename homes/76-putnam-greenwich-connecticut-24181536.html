About this home

Nestled within a beautifully maintained and privately secluded community, this exceptional corner-unit home offers the perfect blend of comfort, natural beauty and convenience. From nearly every window, you&apos;ll enjoy views of mature trees and lush landscaping creating a serene, park-like setting that feels worlds away from the everyday. The oversized windows frame the beautiful scenery surrounding the premises, creating a seamless connection with nature, meanwhile the sun-drenched living room features large south-facing windows filling the space with natural light throughout the day and the bedrooms offer breathtaking views of winter sunsets.
