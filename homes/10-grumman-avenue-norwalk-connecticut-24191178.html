About this home

Welcome to 10 Grumman Avenue, nestled in the heart of the highly desirable Cranbury neighborhood! This beautifully maintained raised ranch has been thoughtfully updated and is ready for its next owners. The spacious kitchen features granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, abundant cabinetry, and Anderson windows, creating the perfect space for everyday living and entertaining. Hardwood floors, fresh paint throughout, an updated full bath, and a built-in Ring security system add style, comfort, and peace of mind. A standout feature is the main-level addition, offering an expansive dining and gathering area ideal for hosting holidays, celebrations, and making lasting memories with family and friends. Step outside to your private retreat, complete with a fully fenced yard, spacious deck, and custom-built stone patio with an outdoor fireplace-perfect for relaxing or entertaining. Additional highlights include a two-car garage and newer roof for added peace of mind. Ideally located just minutes from Calf Pasture Beach, the Westport town line, shopping, restaurants, parks, and commuter routes, this home offers the perfect blend of location, updates, and comfortable living.
