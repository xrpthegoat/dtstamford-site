About this home

Experience refined living in this exceptional modern Colonial, privately set on over an acre in coveted North Stamford. Offering more than 8,000 square feet, this impressive residence features 5 bedrooms, 5 full baths, and 1 half bath, blending timeless elegance with sophisticated design. A dramatic marble foyer welcomes you into beautifully appointed interiors with hardwood floors throughout. The gourmet kitchen is designed for effortless entertaining, featuring granite countertops, custom built-ins, and premium stainless steel appliances, including a Viking cooktop and warming drawer, Wolf double convection ovens, Sub-Zero refrigerator, two Bosch dishwashers, and a Frigidaire microwave. The inviting family room with fireplace and built-ins, elegant living room with a second fireplace, and richly paneled cherry library offer refined yet comfortable living spaces. The luxurious primary suite is a private retreat with a sitting room, tray ceiling, private balcony, expansive walk-in closets, and spa-like ambiance. A finished lower level, bonus room, and finished attic provide exceptional flexibility. Professionally landscaped grounds create a private setting, complemented by a heated three-car garage. Ideally located just minutes from premier shopping, dining, and the Merritt Parkway, this remarkable home offers the perfect blend of luxury, privacy, and convenience.
