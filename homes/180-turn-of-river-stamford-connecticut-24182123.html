About this home

Welcome to this beautiful, turn-key end-unit townhome offering effortless living in the highly sought-after Riverturn complex. The inviting main level features gleaming hardwood floors and an open floor plan, including a well-apointed Living Room with gas fireplace and sliders to a private deck, seamlessly flowing into the Dining Room. A granite eat-in Kitchen and convenient Powder Room complete the first floor. Upstairs, the spacious Primary Bedroom showcases a wonderful vaulted ceiling with skylight, hardwood floors, two generous California closets and a Full Bath with a whirlpool tub &amp; separate shower. A second large Bedroom also enjoys hardwood floors and a generous closet along with access to the hall Full Bath, making it perfect for guests or family. The finished, sun-filled loft with skylight provides flexible space for a home office, gym, or additional living area, along with added walk-in storage. The lower-level Family Room offers bright, versatile space and direct access to the laundry area and one-car garage. Enjoy serene surroundings with views of the charming courtyard in front and a peaceful babbling brook backdrop from the deck. Ideally located just minutes from the Merritt Parkway, shopping, dining, Downtown, and Harbor Point, this home truly offers the best of suburban living with convenience at your doorstep.
