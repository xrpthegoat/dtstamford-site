About this home

The simple pleasures of summertime: escape to spectacular waterfront living. With expansive views of L.I. Sound &amp; the Norwalk River, this direct waterfront home offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle &amp; bustle of everyday life. Take in the breathtaking water views from your private patio or enjoy morning coffee on the balcony as the sun rises over the shoreline. Jump in for a refreshing swim or launch one of the 3 sea kayaks &amp; 2 paddleboards from the jetty. Paddle to nearby waterfront restaurants for sunsets, seafood, and live music. Floor-to-ceiling accordion glass walls open from both the living &amp; dining rooms to the deck, creating a seamless indoor-outdoor connection &amp; allowing the coastal breezes &amp; panoramic views to fill the home. A SONOS sound system adds the perfect soundtrack to your stay. Relax on the bluestone patio below, or float peacefully on the water as the evening sun illuminates the East Norwalk shoreline. Watching the birds and marine life glide by is simply magical, few settings are as serene &amp; restorative. Ideally located within walking distance to vibrant South Norwalk, offering a wide array of international dining, shopping, and entertainment, and the popular Maritime Aquarium. The Metro-North Station is 10-min. walk (approx 55 min NYC). Enjoy biking or jogging along scenic coastal routes to Rowayton &amp; Westport, or rent a boat (Rex Marine) &amp; hire a captain- a memorable day fishing on the Sound. Bring your bathing suits, RELAX &amp; ENJOY the SHORE!
