About this home

A rare Shorehame Association lot with unobstructed Long Island Sound sight lines offered as an amazing opportunity to build new or reimagine the existing residence in one of Old Greenwich&apos;s most sought after waterfront settings. The .28 acre property sits within a private association that includes sandy beach access, a boat launch ramp for paddle boarding, kayaking and boating and a large communal waterfront lawn. The current home features a large living room with direct Sound views, a formal dining room, a kitchen and family room opening to a stone patio facing Long Island Sound, and a primary bedroom suite with private bath. Three additional bedrooms and full bath occupy the upper level, sharing the same water views. Gas heating, central air conditioning and an attached 2-car garage are in place. A beautiful and serene location in a private waterfront association, Long Island Sound views and steps to Tod&apos;s Point - all within minutes of the Old Greenwich train station, Old Greenwich shops and dining and schools.
