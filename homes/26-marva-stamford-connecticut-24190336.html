About this home

Nestled on a quiet cul-de-sac in desirable Lower North Stamford, this beautifully maintained 3-bedroom, 2.5-bath Colonial offers the perfect balance of privacy and convenience. Enjoy a peaceful setting just minutes from shopping, restaurants, and the Stamford Museum &amp; Nature Center. A bright, sun-filled foyer welcomes you into a spacious floor plan designed for comfortable everyday living. The main level features a generous living room, formal dining room, private home office/guest space, and an eat-in kitchen w/granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, and sliding glass doors leading to a spacious deck overlooking the level, fully fenced backyard. Just a few steps up, the impressive 13&apos; x 24&apos; family room is the heart of the home, featuring vaulted ceilings, skylights, a cozy wood-burning fireplace, and a charming Juliet balcony. The finished lower level provides additional living space/guest area, perfect for a recreation room, media room, fitness area, or home office, along with a separate storage room. Upstairs, the spacious primary suite offers a walk-in closet and an en-suite bath with dual vanities, a standing shower, and a Jacuzzi tub. Two additional generously sized bedrooms and a full hall bath complete the upper level. Additional features include hardwood floors, Central A/C, city water, and attached two-car garage. With its versatile floor plan, peaceful setting, and exceptional Lower North Stamford location, this home is one you won&apos;t want to miss.
