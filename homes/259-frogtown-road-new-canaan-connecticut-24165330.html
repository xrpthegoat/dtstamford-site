About this home

FrogPond Estate is a modern Scandinavian masterpiece unlike anything New Canaan has ever seen - an architecturally rare achievement set on 2.25 pristine, private acres, moments from the train and village. Spanning 11,836 plus square feet of sleek, sun-drenched living space, this 7-bedroom, nine-and-a-half-bath residence is defined by its 12-foot ceilings, bespoke millwork, and designer lighting - details that elevate every room into something memorable. Four thoughtfully placed laundry rooms, multiple fireplaces, and a fully custom build throughout speak to a level of intentionality rarely found at any price point. At the heart of the main level, a gourmet kitchen with double islands anchors an open floor plan crafted for both quiet daily living and effortless large-scale entertaining. Walls of glass dissolve the boundary between inside and out, opening to covered porches and private balconies that complete the estate&apos;s serene, Scandinavian sensibility. A separate 1,190 sq. ft. guest cottage - complete with its own island and laundry - offers the perfect complement to the main residence, ideal for extended family, guests, or a private retreat. Secluded yet connected, grand yet warm - FrogPond Estate is a singular home that must be experienced to be understood.
