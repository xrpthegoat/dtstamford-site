About this home

Significant Price Improvement! Motivated seller has repositioned this exceptional home to present an outstanding opportunity in one of Norwalk&apos;s most sought-after neighborhoods. Nestled on a picturesque tree-lined street in Cranbury, this beautifully maintained 4-bedroom, 3.5-bath Colonial offers a rare combination of flexible multigenerational living, exceptional privacy, and generous indoor-outdoor living. Set on beautifully landscaped grounds, the home provides a peaceful retreat complete with an inground pool, spacious patio, and multiple outdoor areas perfect for relaxing or entertaining. An inviting foyer welcomes you to a spacious living room and formal dining area, while the updated eat-in kitchen features custom cabinetry, quartz countertops, and a center island. Just beyond, the stunning family room with sliding glass doors opens seamlessly to the backyard. The thoughtfully designed layout includes a main-level suite with a private bath and direct deck access, ideal for an in-law, au pair, or guests. Upstairs, the primary retreat features a walk-in closet, full bath, and attached office, nursery, or potential 5th bedroom. Two additional generously sized bedrooms and another full bath complete the upper level. Enjoy the convenience of being just minutes from Schools, Cranbury Park, Wegmans, Stew Leonard&apos;s, Oak Hills Golf Course, Metro-North, I-95, and the Merritt Parkway, offering the perfect blend of privacy, space, and easy access to Norwalk&apos;s best amenities!
