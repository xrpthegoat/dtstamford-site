About this home

Amazing opportunity, move-in ready 2-bedroom, 2-bath townhouse in the Sylvan Knoll community. This beautifully maintained home features a spacious updated kitchen with/ custom cabinets, granite countertops and stainless steel appliances, renovated bathrooms, and a finished lower level offering flexible living space ideal for a family room, home office, gym, or guest area. Recent utility updates provide added value and peace of mind. Overlooking a beautifully landscaped courtyard, the home also offers a private, updated rear patio and garden area-perfect for outdoor dining, entertaining, or simply relaxing. Nestled on 13 park-like acres, Sylvan Knoll is a pet-friendly, owner-occupied community with sidewalks throughout. Ideally located just off Exit 9 of I-95, it is within walking distance to Cove Island Park, the beach, and Chelsea Piers, and only minutes from downtown Stamford, shopping, restaurants, and the train station. Common charges include real estate taxes. Please note that units are owner-occupied and rentals are not permitted.
