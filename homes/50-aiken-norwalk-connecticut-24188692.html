About this home

Welcome to Rolling Ridge, one of Norwalk&apos;s most desirable condominium communities. This top-floor 1-bedroom, 1-bath condo offers a bright and functional layout with an updated kitchen, spacious living and dining area, and in-unit laundry. Step outside to your private balcony overlooking mature trees, offering a peaceful setting and added privacy with no neighboring balconies directly beside you. Residents enjoy resort-style amenities including an inground pool, clubhouse, tennis/pickleball courts, dog park, scenic pond with fountains, and walking paths. Conveniently located just minutes from Route 7, I-95, the Merritt Parkway, Merritt 7 train station, shopping, restaurants, and everything Norwalk has to offer.
