About this home

Elegant 2023 new construction offering 5 bedrooms, 4 full and 1 half baths on four finished levels of exceptional living. Designed w/custom millwork, sophisticated finishes, and all-new systems throughout. The gourmet kitchen features premium SS appliances, dual seating areas, and a walk-in pantry, opening to beautifully scaled living and entertaining spaces. A sun-drenched living room w/ fireplace, formal dining room with custom built-ins, and two private home offices complete the main level. The luxurious primary suite is a private retreat w/fireplace, spa-inspired bath &amp; oversized walk-in closet. The finished third floor offers a spacious recreation room, bedroom, and full bath, while the lower level with high ceilings includes a family room, fitness studio, full bath and optional sauna. Outdoor living is equally impressive with a heated in-ground pool, level lawn, and covered porch featuring a built-in grill station, TV, and ceiling fans. Ideally situated on a picturesque tree-lined street, this turnkey residence seamlessly blends timeless elegance with modern luxury.
