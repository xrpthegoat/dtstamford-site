About this home

Step into the timeless charm of this 1920s Classic Colonial, perfectly situated on nearly an acre of lush, manicured grounds. With 2,570 square feet of refined living space, this home effortlessly blends vintage character with modern convenience. The interior features four spacious bedrooms and two-and-a-half baths, offering a thoughtful layout that feels both grand and inviting. The heart of the home opens up to a beautiful, spacious deck and yard, providing a serene backdrop for morning coffee or afternoon relaxation. For those who need extra space, the 1,269-square-foot unfinished basement offers incredible storage potential, complemented by the convenience of a two-car attached garage. The location is truly unbeatable, placing you just moments away from local favorites like The Little Barn, Big Y, and an array of popular shops and restaurants. This is a rare opportunity to enjoy a quintessential New England lifestyle in a premier setting. PLEASE SEE ADDITIONAL REMARKS RE: UTILITIES AND LAWN CARE.
