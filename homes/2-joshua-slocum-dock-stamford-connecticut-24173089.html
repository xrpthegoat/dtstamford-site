About this home

A RARE opportunity to own in the coveted Dolphin Cove neighborhood of Stamford. Dolphin Cove offers a 3 acre club area directly on the LI Sound. The club includes 2 swimming pools, full bar set up and entertaining area, 450 feet of beach, tennis court and racks for small boat storage. 2 Joshua Slocum Dock is on the market for the very first time since it was custom built in 1978. Lived in and lovingly cared for by its original owners for nearly 5 decades, 2 Joshua Slocum Dock is a gem just steps away from the waters of the Long Island Sound. Ideal for boating enthusiasts, paddle boarders and kayakers or simply enjoying the peaceful waterfront setting from your own backyard. Treasure and value the deeded dock slip just steps from the back patio. Enjoy sun drenched rooms throughout all with hardwood floors and an updated kitchen with stainless steal appliances and quartz countertops. Enjoy your morning coffee on one of two second floor decks or in the three season tiled sunroom off of the formal living room. Other features include two fireplaces one is wood burning and the other has a gas insert. There is a utility room off the kitchen with laundry and a drinks fridge and two sets of French doors that lead to the outdoor patios. This property is ready for its next chapter. Whether you envision preserving its timeless character or updating it to reflect your own style, the possibilities are endless. House is being sold as is.
