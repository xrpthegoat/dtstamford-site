About this home

Welcome to 11 Lycett Court, a well-loved raised ranch nestled at the end of a cul-de-sac street. Owned by the same family for over 46 years, this 4-bedroom, 2.5 bath home is ready for its next owners to make new memories. The main level features hardwood floors throughout, a spacious living room, dining room and eat-in-kitchen leading out to a huge deck with access to the yard offering endless options for playing, entertaining, or gardening. The primary bedroom includes an ensuite bath, while two additional bedrooms and a full bath complete the main floor. The lower level offers flexible living space with built-in bookshelves, a wood-burning fireplace, laundry, and a 4th bedroom or office option. Additional highlights include central air and a 2-car attached garage. Conveniently located near Oak Hills Park, restaurants, SoNo, the Merritt Parkway, I-95 and Metro North. Your search stops here!
