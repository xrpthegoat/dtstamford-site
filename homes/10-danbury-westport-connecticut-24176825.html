About this home

Completely renovated Nantucket inspired cottage with cobblestone apron and crushed oyster shell driveway available for Winter rental. 90 steps from Compo Beach! Easy flowing Living/dining room with exposed distressed beams and oversize windows maximizing water views from almost every room. Cooks kitchen w Thermador appliances, a coffee bar and all the accessories you could wish for. Large deck off kitchen w fire pit table &amp; a Weber grill for BBQs whenever you like. Deep manicured lawn w sprinkler system, outdoor shower and bonus studio w Wifi possible office or gym. Second floor includes a Master bedroom with private deck with sectional &amp; fire pit to enjoy spectacular sunsets and a another bedroom with two twin beds. Lawn service and housekeeper maintained by owner. Outdoor shower. Westport also has a municipal country club called Longshore which offers a newly renovated 18-hole golf course, tennis and paddleboard courts, two swimming pools, and sailing school (with boat, kayak and canoe rentals) Enjoy water community living at its best! 10 minutes to Metro north (commuter bus available) and I95.
