About this home

Welcome home to easy, low-maintenance living in a beautifully maintained community featuring mature landscaping, attractive curb appeal, and a refreshing in-ground pool all supported by an affordable HOA. This desirable end-unit offers the convenience of single-floor living while enjoying the added privacy and natural light that comes with its premier location. Thoughtfully designed and efficiently sized, this charming condo maximizes both comfort and functionality. A cozy fireplace creates a warm and inviting atmosphere, central heat and air make for perfect climate control all year round, and the private balcony (new in October 2024) provides the perfect spot to enjoy your morning coffee or unwind at the end of the day. The convenience of in-unit laundry (new smart washer/dryer in 2021), a private attached garage, and a dedicated storage unit make everyday living exceptionally easy. Adding even more value and peace of mind, the home features a new high-efficiency propane furnace (December 2025). Ideally located, this unit is a commuter&apos;s dream, offering seamless access to NYC whether by car or by train. The neighborhood boasts sidewalk-lined access to the Merritt 7 commuter train station only .3 miles away, and the complex is just minutes from the Merritt Parkway and I-95. Everyday conveniences are unmatched, with local grocery stores, the SoNo Collection mall, and premier shopping along Main, Connecticut, and Westport Avenues all just minutes away.
