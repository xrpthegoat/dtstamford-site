About this home

Tucked behind a tall fence flanked by stone pillars. Less than 5 minutes to town, schools, beach and train. Easy one floor living. Sun-filled main level open floor plan featuring a Living Room with fireplace, a large well-equipped Kitchen with a spacious Dining Area and Breakfast Bar, and a Family Room with walls of windows inviting nature in. There is also a private office next to the first floor Primary Bedroom with a full Bath. Several French doors are strategically placed to access the oversized deck that spans the length of the rear of the house, and there is a staircase leading to the picturesque backyard. Upstairs you will find the 2nd Bedroom with vaulted ceiling, skylights and a full Bath which can easily be used as a Studio or Office. The finished walk-out lower level offers the 3rd Bedroom and full Bath plus a large flex space for a Playroom, Recreation. Lower level would make for a fabulous au-pair or in-law suite. The Mudroom connects the house to the 2 car Garage, and above the Garage is a large unfinished space that is approximately 22X24. House has a generator. So much to love about this wonderful property.
