About this home

Stunning townhouse in the heart of downtown Greenwich. This 3 bedroom, 3 1/2 bath townhome has it all and just a short walk brings you to the Metro, Bruce Park and some of the areas finest restaurants and shopping. This updated and beautifully furnished townhome has the feel of a single family home. High ceilings and fine details are sure to please the most discerning tenant.
