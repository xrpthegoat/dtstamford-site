About this home

Experience the best of coastal living in this stunning Rowayton retreat, perfectly positioned on the tranquil waters of Farm Creek. Available for rent for a flexible term from September through April, this home offers the rare choice of being delivered furnished or unfurnished to suit your lifestyle. Inside, the vibe is effortlessly chic and &quot;beachy,&quot; defined by a fresh palette, new light hardwood floors, and an abundance of natural light that pours through oversized windows. The open-concept layout creates an inviting flow, centered around a beautifully renovated kitchen and modern baths. With four spacious bedrooms and three full bathrooms, there is plenty of room to breathe, including a versatile finished third floor that works perfectly as a home office, gym, or creative studio. The exterior is a true waterfront sanctuary. Step out onto the expansive deck to take in the crisp salt air and panoramic views of Farm Creek-a vista shared by almost every room in the house. Whether you&apos;re sipping morning coffee or watching the sunset, the backdrop is spectacular. The property also features a private dock, offering direct water access for kayaking or simply enjoying the rhythmic beauty of the tides. This is an incredible opportunity to settle into a turn-key, upscale home in one of the area&apos;s most coveted locations. Home can also be rented furnished for $24,000 per month.
