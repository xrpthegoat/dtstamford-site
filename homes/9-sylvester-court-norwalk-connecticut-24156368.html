About this home

Spend your days on the water and end each evening with spectacular west-facing sunsets. Welcome to an exceptional waterfront retreat on coveted Gregory Point overlooking Norwalk Harbor. Beautifully renovated and truly move-in ready, this modern coastal farmhouse captures panoramic water views from nearly every room. The sunlit chef&apos;s kitchen with wood-burning fireplace flows seamlessly into a casual family room wrapped in glass. A large formal dining room, a spacious living room and adjacent sunroom set the stage for effortless entertaining. Upstairs, the serene Primary Suite features sweeping harbor views, a sitting area with coffee bar, spa bath with soaking tub and steam shower, and multiple walk-in closets. Three additional bedrooms, two baths, and two bonus rooms (one lined with windows) offer exceptional flexibility. A first-floor office or fifth bedroom and custom mudroom complete the layout. Outside, enjoy a wraparound porch, bluestone patio, and your own stationary pier with floating dock - boating begins at your doorstep. Attached garage plus separate detached garage. Moments to beaches, golf, tennis, and local dining. One hour to NYC.
