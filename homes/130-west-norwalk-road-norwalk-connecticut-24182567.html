About this home

*Highest and best 6/29 at 6pm* Welcome to 130 W Norwalk Road, a gated, four-bedroom ranch on park-like 2.04 acres with sweeping Five Mile River views in West Norwalk. This 3,188 square foot home delivers a flexible layout that lives much larger--the first floor holds four bedrooms in a smart split arrangement around open living spaces, while the finished basement adds a fifth bedroom, full bath, laundry, and a sprawling recreation room. Enter to find the living room with a fireplace flowing into the dining room and kitchen, which opens directly to a large deck overlooking the river. The spacious and bright eat-in-kitchen has custom cabinets including a breakfast bar, granite counters, and ample space for the at-home chef including a gas stove. An enclosed sun room with its own deck access extends the entertaining footprint. The first floor includes two full bathrooms. Downstairs, the recreation room anchors the finished basement alongside the fifth bedroom, laundry, full bath, utility areas, and an oversized two-car garage with interior access. Outside, the flat backyard is built for leisure -- pool, hot tub, and river views frame the grounds. Located close to Darien&apos;s amenities, and all the shopping around CT Ave, it couldn&apos;t be more convenient. The Darien train is a few minutes down the road, and I95 is even closer. Built in 1958, this property offers a rare combination of acreage, waterfront setting, flexible floor plan, and recreational amenities.
