About this home

Great opportunity for an owner occupant or investor to own this oversized (4,376 sq. ft.) 2-family in a prime Cove location. This is one of the largest multi-family houses in the Cove with plenty of room for all! Front unit is the original cape style house featuring a large country kitchen, dining room, living room, main floor primary bedroom and bath, plus two bedrooms and bath on the second floor. There is direct access to the basement and laundry from this unit. Rear Unit is oversized contemporary style 3-4 bedroom, 3.5 bath townhouse set back from the road. Enter via a 2-story foyer into a country kitchen with breakfast bar and adjacent dining area which opens onto a step-down family room with corner fireplace, Mexican tile floor, skylight and also a private deck. Spacious living room with bow window, powder room laundry room and C/A in this unit too. The second floor consists of a generous size primary bedroom suite with walk in closet and bath with Jacuzzi plus two good sized bedrooms and hall bath. Ample parking and new privacy fence. Enjoy an active Cove lifestyle within walking distance to popular Cove Island Park, beach, marina, playground, walking trails, ice rink etc. This convenient location is also close to Chelsea Piers Sports and Entertainment Complex and only a minute to 1-95 and near downtown Stamford, too!
