About this home

Modern elegance meets effortless convenience in this beautifully updated 2-bedroom, 2.5-bath end-unit townhouse offering nearly 2,000 sqft in Springdale&apos;s sought-after River&apos;s Edge community. This light-filled townhome features an open-concept layout with hardwood floors and seamless flow between the living and dining areas, highlighted by oversized windows and sliders leading to a private balcony-perfect for grilling or entertaining. The recently remodeled kitchen showcases sleek cabinetry, quartz countertops, gold hardware, and stainless steel appliances, creating a stylish and functional space for everyday living. Upstairs, the spacious primary suite offers soaring cathedral ceilings, generous double closets, and an en-suite bath with double vanity, jetted tub, and separate walk-in shower. A generously sized second bedroom, full bath, and linen storage complete the level. The third-floor loft-ideal as a home office, media room, or potential third bedroom-adds incredible flexibility. The lower level features an attached garage, laundry area, and bonus space currently used as a gym, along with ample storage. Enjoy access to community amenities including an in-ground pool, fitness center, and clubhouse. Just moments to Springdale village, local cafes and dining, Metro-North for an easy NYC commute, and convenient access to the Merritt Parkway, with nearby New Canaan and Darien just minutes away-this turnkey home offers the perfect blend of style, comfort, and location.
