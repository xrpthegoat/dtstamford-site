About this home

Set in a fantastic East Norwalk location just minutes from the beach, this exceptional NEW CONSTRUCTION home offers luxury, quality craftsmanship, and thoughtful design throughout. A striking chandelier welcomes you into the elegant foyer, setting the tone for the beautifully appointed interior. The main level features a private home office, a stylish powder room, and a great room with a stunning fireplace and sliders that create effortless indoor-outdoor living, opening to the blue slate patio and newly fenced backyard. The gourmet kitchen is a chef&apos;s dream, showcasing white cabinetry, quartz counters, stainless steel appliances and abundant workspace. The mudroom is the perfect drop zone for backpacks, coats, etc. &amp; everyday essentials and has access to the attached two car garage. Upstairs, the luxurious primary suite is filled with natural light &amp; features a spa-inspired bath with soaking tub, over-sized shower and a large walk-in closet. Three additional bedrooms provide plenty of space, including one with a private en-suite bath, while the remaining bedrooms share a beautifully appointed full hall bath. A spacious laundry room completes this level. The third floor is truly impressive, offering a versatile space perfect as a game room, media room, fitness area or whatever best suites your lifestyle. The lower level is also finished. Meticulously built showcasing evident pride of craftsmanship, this quality-built home is a rare opportunity to enjoy shoreline living!
