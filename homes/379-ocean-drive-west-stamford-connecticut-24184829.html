About this home

Completed in 2023 and designed by Michael Smith Architects, this extraordinary six bedroom waterfront residence offers more than 8,700 square feet of luxury living along one of Stamford&apos;s most coveted stretches of Ocean Drive West. Built two feet above FEMA flood elevation requirements and featuring sweeping views of Long Island Sound from every principal room, the home combines timeless coastal architecture, exceptional craftsmanship, and modern construction. Thoughtfully designed for both grand entertaining and everyday living, the residence balances expansive gathering spaces with private retreats, all oriented toward the water. A cedar shake exterior, stone detailing, and sunrise to sunset exposure define the home&apos;s striking waterfront presence. Across three levels, terraces, balconies, and principal living spaces are carefully positioned to capture panoramic views of Long Island Sound, creating a seamless connection between the interior and the surrounding landscape. The result is a residence equally suited for weekend escapes, year round living, and large scale entertaining.
