About this home

Beautiful Colonial built in 2005 with 4 bedrooms, 2.5 baths, abundant storage, and excellent potential for an in-law suite. Designed for both everyday living and entertaining, this sun-filled home features hardwood floors, central air, natural gas heat, and an open floor plan. The welcoming main level offers a spacious living room, formal dining room, and an expansive family room with a cozy gas fireplace. The updated eat-in kitchen features ample cabinetry, generous counter space, and direct access to an oversized Trex deck overlooking the fully fenced backyard-perfect for outdoor dining, entertaining, or relaxing. Upstairs, the impressive primary suite provides a peaceful retreat with a walk-in closet and a private bath featuring a Jacuzzi tub. Three additional generously sized bedrooms, a full hall bath, and the convenience of a second-floor laundry room complete this level. The finished third floor offers exceptional flexibility, making it ideal as a playroom, media room, home office, fitness area, or guest space, while an additional storage room provides even more functionality. The walk-out lower level offers abundant storage and presents excellent potential for an in-law suite, recreation room, or additional finished living space. Ideally located just minutes from I-95, Metro-North, shopping, restaurants, parks, and all that Norwalk has to offer, this exceptional home combines space, comfort, and convenience.
