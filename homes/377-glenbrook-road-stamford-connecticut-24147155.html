About this home

Welcome to 377 Glenbrook Road Unit 5, a beautifully maintained and spacious condominium offering three levels of comfortable living in a convenient Stamford location. Step inside to find a bright and inviting main level featuring gleaming hardwood floors, a cozy wood fireplace, and an open flow between the living and dining areas perfect for both everyday living and entertaining. The dining space opens seamlessly to a private patio, creating an ideal indoor outdoor lifestyle for relaxing or hosting guests. The kitchen is thoughtfully laid out with ample cabinetry and workspace, making meal prep both functional and enjoyable. Upstairs, the primary suite offers a peaceful retreat complete with its own private balcony, full bath, and generous closet space, while additional bedrooms provide flexibility for guests, home office, or additional living needs. The finished lower level adds even more versatility with a spacious family room perfect for movie nights, a home gym, or play area. This well appointed unit combines comfort and convenience with assigned parking, access to nearby shopping, dining, and public transportation, and a highly walkable location. A rare opportunity to enjoy multi level living with both patio and balcony spaces in one of Stamford&apos;s most accessible neighborhoods.
