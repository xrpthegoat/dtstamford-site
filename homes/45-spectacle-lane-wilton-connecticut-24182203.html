About this home

Tucked away on peaceful and highly sought-after Spectacle Lane, this property offers an exceptional blend of country-like privacy and convenient access to Wilton&apos;s top-rated schools, both Wilton and Ridgefield&apos;s charming town centers, and major commuter routes. It stands as a rare architectural masterpiece, having been lovingly restored to flawlessly showcase the soulful character and timeless craftsmanship of a 1925 home-an effortless allure that was recently featured in New York Magazine. The estate provides an incredibly versatile living arrangement with a total of three bedrooms. The main house features two character-filled bedrooms and light-filled living spaces, while a separate, private one-bedroom guest quarters offers the perfect setup for weekend visitors, extended family, an au pair or home office. A standout feature of the property is the dramatic, dedicated art studio/home office in the main house, boasting impressive vaulted ceilings and an abundance of natural light. Throughout both residences, the thoughtfully designed spaces serve as a blank canvas, offering incredible aesthetic flexibility that effortlessly accommodates any decor style-from classic traditional to sleek modern minimalism.
