About this home

*Highest and Best Deadline on Tuesday 5pm* Welcome to 41 Ledgewood Drive, a 4-bedroom colonial on 0.36 acres in a quiet and desirable West Norwalk neighborhood with convenient access to the Merritt and I-95. Step inside to a flexible first-floor layout featuring a sunroom that fills with morning light, plus two versatile bedrooms currently used as an office and playroom. The updated kitchen has a large peninsula and breakfast bar and includes GE Profile appliances, a propane range, and a mini wine fridge. Upstairs, the primary suite offers a private bath with double sinks, stall shower, and linen closet, plus generous closet space. Two additional bedrooms and a full bath with tub/shower combo completes the upper level. Recent updates include Anderson windows throughout (2019), a remodeled downstairs bath (2022), freshly painted interior and exterior (2020-2026), new hot water heater (2024), and a paved driveway (2024). Recessed lighting brightens every room, while custom blinds add a polished touch. Outside, a private deck overlooks wooded views, perfect for morning coffee or evening gatherings. The flat front yard and invisible dog fence make outdoor living easy. An attached basement garage provides convenient parking and storage. All of this located close to both I95 and the Merritt, and some great shops including Angelo&apos;s Specialties and amazing restaurants. With friendly neighbors and a peaceful setting, this home offers both space and flexibility for your next chapter.
