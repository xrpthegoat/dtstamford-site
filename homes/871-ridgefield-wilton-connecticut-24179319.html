About this home

On the market for the first time in 60 years, this quintessential Connecticut antique awaits its next chapter. Built in 1830 and nestled along scenic Ridgefield Road, this historic gem sits on 2.20 breathtaking, park-like acres. Nature lovers will adore the magnificent gardens, lovingly established years ago by the former owner and her Garden Club friends. Inside, the main house features 7 rooms all oozing with period charm, character, and warmth. Rare wide-plank hardwood floors, exposed beams, classic fireplaces, and custom built-ins showcase the unmatched craftsmanship of a bygone era. The layout includes 3 cozy bedrooms and 1 full and 1 half bath, offering a unique opportunity to preserve history while adding modern touches. Complete with a separate 800 sq ft legal accessory structure on site, this magical estate offers endless potential in a top-rated school district. Don&apos;t miss this rare piece of Wilton history!
