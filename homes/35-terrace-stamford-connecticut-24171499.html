About this home

Luxury. Location. Lifestyle. Welcome to this stunning 3BR/3BA detached luxury townhome in the heart of Stamford. Completed in 2021 and elevated with curated, bespoke high-end finishes throughout, including gorgeous whitewashed hardwood flooring, this sun-drenched modern residence offers a seamless open-concept layout designed for everyday living, effortless entertaining, style &amp; functionality. The designer gourmet kitchen features top-of-the-line stainless steel appliances, generous custom cabinetry, large center island, wine fridge, and a dedicated coffee bar all flowing seamlessly into the sophisticated living and dining area, anchored by a gas fireplace. Step outside to an expansive wraparound deck and private patio - the largest in the development - ideal for entertaining, relaxing, or taking in the stunning downtown city views.
