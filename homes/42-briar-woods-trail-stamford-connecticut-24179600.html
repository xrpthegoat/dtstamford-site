About this home

Walls of glass, soaring ceilings, skylights &amp; incredible natural light define this stunning North Stamford contemporary, set on a private cul-de-sac in the desirable Briar Woods Assoc. Designed to bring the outside in, this 4 bed/4.5 bath home has a true treehouse feel. The dramatic two-story entry opens to an airy, flexible floor plan w/hardwood floors, multiple fireplaces, vlt&apos;d ceilings &amp; seamless indoor/outdoor living &amp; entertaining. The renovated kitchen features custom wood cabinetry, an oversized island, thoughtful storage &amp; easy flow to the dining area, expansive wraparound deck &amp; mudroom w/half bath. A first-floor bdrm w/adjacent full bath is ideal for guests, office or playroom. Upstairs, the spacious primary suite w/ vlt&apos;d ceilings offers a fireplace, walk-in closet, private balcony &amp; fabulous spa-like bath, while two add&apos;l bdrms, renovated shared bath &amp; open loft complete the 2nd floor. The finished, walkout lower level offers incredible flexibility w/fireplace, rec room, built-in cherry bar, full bath, storage &amp; in-law suite potential. Enjoy morning coffee, summer bbqs or evenings under the stars from the private elevated setting, expansive deck &amp; firepit area. See feature sheet for the many recent upgrades incl generator hookup, refreshed mudroom, remote monitoring &amp; more. All part of a special 12-home association w/ pool, tennis, dining area &amp; cabanas, close to parks, hiking, shopping, restaurants, Merritt Parkway &amp; all North Stamford has to offer.
