About this home

Perfectly positioned just steps from downtown Darien, the train station, schools, and the vibrant new Corbin District, this beautifully updated Colonial offers the ideal blend of convenience, charm, and move-in-ready living. Freshly painted throughout with newly refinished hardwood floors, this 5-bedroom, 3.5-bath home features generous living spaces including a formal living room, dining room, family room, rec room, and an open kitchen designed for both everyday living and entertaining. Set on .70 acres, the backyard is a true surprise, featuring an expansive multi-level deck ideal for outdoor dining, entertaining, and relaxing. Beyond the property lies an additional 1.19 acres of protected open space, creating a beautiful natural backdrop and an unexpected sense of space. Timeless curb appeal, abundant living space, and an unbeatable in-town location make this a truly exceptional opportunity. Walk to downtown Darien, the train station, schools, restaurants, and the vibrant new Corbin District while enjoying all the comfort and flexibility of a move-in-ready home.
