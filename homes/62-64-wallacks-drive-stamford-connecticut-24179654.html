About this home

Welcome to historic Wallack&apos;s Point, arguably Stamford&apos;s most prestigious waterfront enclave. Situated on more than an acre with nearly 100 feet of direct Long Island Sound frontage, this extraordinary former boathouse has been transformed into a remarkable luxury compound. Defined by striking architectural design, soaring walls of glass, and sweeping water views, the property seamlessly blends sophisticated interiors with exceptional outdoor living and entertaining spaces. Every room is thoughtfully positioned to capture the beauty of the Sound, creating a private sanctuary unlike any other. Complementing the main residence, a converted carriage house offers a separate guest retreat, multigenerational living option, home office, or potential income-producing opportunity. A rare and compelling offering where striking architecture meets unparalleled waterfront living.
