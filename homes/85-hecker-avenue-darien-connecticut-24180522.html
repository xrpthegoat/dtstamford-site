About this home

Welcome to 85 Hecker Avenue, a stunning custom rebuild in the heart of Darien&apos;s coveted Noroton neighborhood, where modern luxury meets everyday convenience. Thoughtfully designed with 4 spacious bedrooms and 5 full bathrooms, this turnkey home features an open-concept floor plan centered around a beautifully appointed kitchen and inviting living spaces, along with a dedicated home office for today&apos;s flexible lifestyle. The finished walkout lower level offers exceptional bonus living space, perfect for entertaining, guests, a gym, or media room. Upstairs, the luxurious primary suite serves as a private retreat, while the additional bedrooms provide generous space and comfort.
