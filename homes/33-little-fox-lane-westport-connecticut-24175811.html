About this home

Tucked away on a quiet cul-de-sac in Westport&apos;s prestigious Red Coat neighborhood, this beautiful contemporary home offers exceptional privacy on 2+ serene acres with direct access and views of the Saugatuck River. Designed with a timeless mid-century modern aesthetic, the open floor plan is centered around a dramatic vaulted living room featuring a circular fireplace, wet bar, and walls of glass that fill the home with natural light. The main level lives like a ranch, offering a spacious primary suite and two additional bedrooms, all conveniently located on one floor. Large windows throughout frame picturesque views of the pool, spa, and surrounding landscape, creating a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor living. The walkout lower level features a generous family room, den, 3 additional bedrooms, a convenient kitchenette, and a pool changing room with shower. Walls of glass overlook the pool and bring abundant natural light into the space. A private pathway leads to the Saugatuck River, where you can wade, fish, or launch a kayak right from the property&apos;s riverfront access. Ideally located just minutes from the YMCA, Merritt Parkway, downtown Westport, and the town&apos;s award-winning schools, this rare rental opportunity combines architectural character, resort-style amenities, and an exceptional Westport setting. ** LANDLORD COVERS POOL AND LANDSCAPING; COULD ALSO BE FURNISHED
