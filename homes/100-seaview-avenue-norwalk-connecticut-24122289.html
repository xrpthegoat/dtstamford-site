About this home

PENTHOUSE LEVEL DIRECT WATERFRONT VIEWS in one of East Norwalk&apos;s most coveted waterfront enclaves, this residence offers an exceptional frontrow vantage point over Long Island Sound and the harbor. An expansive balcony becomes your private perch for sweeping water views, glowing sunsets, and cooling summer breezes. Inside, an open kitchen leads to the dining area and through to the incredible living room with a wall of glass to the terrace. An office/den area also has inspiring views. The primary BR suite also has glass doors to the balcony and signature views. A large dressing rm w/ abundant closet space plus en-suite bath adds to the appeal. The 2nd BR has built-ins, good closet space and an accompanying full bath. The lifestyle is enhanced by a waterfront heated pool, marina, promenade, and a gathering room ideal for hosting friends and neighbors. From this location, you can stroll or bike to beaches, train stations, local shops, highly-rated restaurants, the dog park, the SoNo Collection anchored by Bloomingdale&apos;s and Nordstrom, and the Maritime Aquarium. Quick access to I95 and the Merritt makes commuting effortless-NYC is just 50 miles away. The secured building includes elevators, a storage room, and a reserved carport space. A rare opportunity to enjoy the best of Fairfield County&apos;s coastal lifestyle. This is an exceptional opportunity to enjoy multi-million dollar views and amenities at a popular price point.
