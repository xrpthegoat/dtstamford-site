About this home

Awesome 4BR, 2.5B on a tree-lined cul-de-sac. Exterior newly painted. Cathedral ceiling with 2 skylights in the entry/foyer. LR provides an open concept. Catwalk overlooks the LR and foyer and connects the 4 upstairs BR&apos;s. LR features a gas fireplace and French doors opening to the full-length, rear deck. The eat-in kitchen has stainless-steel appliances with a new stove and granite counter tops, adjoining the DR. Flat-screen TV in the kitchen is for tenant use/enjoyment. TV room on the first floor opens to the rear deck, complete with French doors. Multiple solar panels on the rear roof lowers electricity costs. Natural gas generator automatically fires to minimize power loss. Located in the Glenville section of Greenwich, 1/2 mile from a park, playing field, Glenville Elementary School and the Western Greenwich Civic Center, which has its own fitness center, day care and basketball courts for Greenwich residents. Nearby shopping center sports a large grocery store, across the street from 4 restaurants, a deli, medical and dental offices, a pharmacy, dry cleaners, a bank and a nail salon Gas station is within walking distance for auto repairs. Home is 1.5 miles from the Merritt/Hutchinson River Parkways &amp; 3 miles to downtown Greenwich, the Greenwich train station and I-95. 3 miles from Byram Beach, the boat launch/boat slips and the town pool. Convenient to the town&apos;s golf course. Greenwich Hospital/Yale New Haven Health Center is about 3 miles away.
