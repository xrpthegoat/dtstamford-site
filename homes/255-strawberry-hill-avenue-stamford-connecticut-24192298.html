About this home

Welcome to Unit E5 at 255 Strawberry Hill Avenue, a spacious and inviting two-bedroom, two-bath condominium offering approximately 1,169 square feet of comfortable living space in Stamford&apos;s Newfield neighborhood. The open-concept layout is filled with natural light and provides an easy flow between the living and dining areas, creating a versatile space for everyday living and entertaining. The primary bedroom features a newly updated en-suite bathroom, while the second bedroom offers flexibility for guests, a home office, or additional living space. A second full bathroom adds convenience, and in-unit laundry makes daily routines easier. Enjoy private outdoor space with both a balcony and patio, ideal for relaxing or enjoying fresh air. Residents also have access to the community pool. Parking includes one assigned space along with one visitor parking space. Conveniently located near downtown Stamford, shopping, restaurants, parks, beaches, Harbor Point, the Stamford Transportation Center, I-95, and the Merritt Parkway.
