About this home

Beautifully maintained and thoughtfully updated Cape Cod in sought-after Springdale, offering 4 bedrooms and 2 full bathrooms with a bright open-concept layout. Step inside to a welcoming living room with a cozy fireplace, flowing into the dining area and a sun-filled eat-in kitchen with vaulted ceilings. The kitchen provides seamless access to a private, level backyard, complete with an above-ground pool and a beautiful brick BBQ with stainless steel attachments-perfect for outdoor entertaining and summer gatherings. The main level features two spacious bedrooms, a renovated full bathroom, along with a well-appointed mudroom and laundry room for everyday convenience. Upstairs, the third bedroom connects to a fourth bedroom and is complemented by a stylishly renovated second full bathroom. Outdoors, the oversized backyard offers lush landscaping and an above ground pool brings a peaceful setting for entertaining or simply relaxing. The property backs directly to Sleepy Hollow Park, providing additional privacy and green space. This home was completely transformed in 2019 through 2022 with a huge addition, new kitchen, added dormer to the second floor, new roof/siding 2019, 2 new bathrooms, a new gas furnace, tankless hot water system, pool 2018, freshly painted 25/26 and much more. Conveniently located within a 1-mile radius of Springdale shopping center, dining, parks, schools, and the Springdale train station, making commuting and daily errands effortless.
