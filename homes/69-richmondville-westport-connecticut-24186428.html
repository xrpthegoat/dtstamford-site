About this home

Escape to your own private sanctuary in this sun-filled, flexible layout home located in the highly desirable In-Town neighborhood, close to all of Westport&apos;s amenities. Offering a rare blend of dramatic natural beauty and everyday convenience, this property features breathtaking, unobstructed views of a waterfall on Lee&apos;s Pond, which flows directly into the historic Saugatuck River. This is a 5-bedroom house (3 bedrooms have the waterfall view) with an upstairs playroom. 2 of the 5 bedrooms have their own den area and a full bathroom-including a back staircase providing a separate living area with great privacy and independence for guests, an au pair, or in-laws. Launch your kayak right from your backyard and enjoy a scenic paddle directly into vibrant downtown Westport. Enjoy shopping, dining, and community events just minutes away. Host unforgettable summer cookouts on the patio while relaxing to the serene, soothing sounds of the waterfall.
