About this home

Experience the best of Marvin Beach coastal living in this never lived in craftsman owned residence, newly completed in 2026 and ready for immediate occupancy. Thoughtfully built from the ground up with exceptional attention to detail, this rare offering blends craftsmanship with finishes in one of East Norwalk&apos;s coveted waterfront neighborhoods. The interior features soaring ceilings, custom accent walls, curated finishes, and an open layout designed for modern living. At the heart of the home, the gourmet eat in kitchen flows seamlessly into a spacious family room, creating the perfect setting for everyday living and entertaining. Four generously sized bedrooms provide comfort and flexibility, including a luxurious primary suite with a walk in closet and spa inspired bath. A second bathroom with soaking tub and a separate laundry room with full size washer and dryer add convenience and style rarely found in rental market. Enjoy year round comfort with six independently zoned mini split systems. Outside, a private patio and yard provide the backdrop for outdoor dining, morning coffee, or relaxing after a day at the beach, while off street parking adds convenience. Just moments from Calf Pasture Beach, marinas, parks, restaurants, shopping, and the East Norwalk train station, this exceptional home offers a walkable coastal lifestyle. Luxury, location, and brand new construction come together in a way rarely available in Marvin Beach. Immediate occupancy available.
