About this home

State of the Art construction from the ground up, this exceptional home has been thoughtfully transformed with a complete first-floor gut renovation taken down to the studs, the addition of a second story, and a beautifully finished lower level-all completed in 2025-2026. Offering three finished levels of modern living, this 4-bedroom, 3-full-bath residence is set on a lovely .53-acre property just minutes from downtown New Canaan, award-winning schools, Waveny Park, and the Metro-North train. The main level showcases a stunning new kitchen featuring quartz countertops, quality finishes, and an open-concept design that flows seamlessly into the living and dining areas-ideal for both everyday living and entertaining. Also on the first floor are a full bathroom, a spacious bedroom, a separate room perfect for a home office, and additional flexible bonus space to accommodate a variety of lifestyles. Upstairs, the beautiful primary suite offers a generous walk-in closet and a luxurious ensuite bath. Two additional bedrooms share a well-appointed full bathroom, and a convenient second-floor laundry room completes this level. The finished lower level is filled with natural light and provides two additional rooms ideal for a home office, fitness area, playroom, or media space, along with ample storage. Additional highlights include beautiful hardwood oak flooring, recessed lighting, contemporary finishes throughout, and abundant storage.
