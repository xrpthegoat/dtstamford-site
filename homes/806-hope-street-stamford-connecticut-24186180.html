About this home

Welcome home to this spacious and sun-filled townhome offering the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and flexibility in Stamford&apos;s desirable Springdale neighborhood. Designed for today&apos;s lifestyle, this three-bedroom, 3-1/2 bath residence features hardwood floors, and generously sized rooms throughout. The inviting living room is anchored by a fireplace and a beautiful bay window that fills the space with natural light, creating the perfect setting for relaxing evenings or entertaining guests. The eat-in kitchen offers granite countertops, a breakfast bar, walk-in pantry, and direct access to outdoor space. A main-level laundry area adds convenience to your daily routine.
