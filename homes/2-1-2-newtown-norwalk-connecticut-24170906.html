About this home

Rare opportunity on one of Norwalk&apos;s most charming streets! This beautifully renovated Cape perfectly blends timeless character with modern comfort. Light, bright, and meticulously maintained throughout all 1,898 sq ft, the pride of ownership is evident from the moment you arrive. Soaring first-floor ceilings create an airy, open feel that sets this home apart - generous height that amplifies the natural light and gives every room a sense of gracious space.
