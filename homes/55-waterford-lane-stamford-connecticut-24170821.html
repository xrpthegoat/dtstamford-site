About this home

Turnkey townhouse living with the space, storage, and private outdoor area rarely found in a Stamford condo. Beautifully maintained and move-in ready, 55 Waterford Lane Unit 2 offers 3 bedrooms, 2.5 baths, an attached garage, and a flexible lower level with walk-out access. The main level is bright and inviting with hardwood floors, an easy living/dining flow, and a well-appointed kitchen featuring granite counters and stainless steel appliances. Sliders from the dining area open to a private deck and fully fenced backyard, giving the home a natural indoor-outdoor connection for everyday use, grilling, and entertaining. Upstairs, three comfortable bedrooms are set apart from the main living space, including custom closet organization for added function. The lower level adds valuable flexibility for a home office, gym, media space, mudroom, or additional storage. Central air, central heat, off-street parking, and a small five-unit community make daily living simple. Set in a convenient Stamford location near Ridgeway, Scalzi Park, downtown restaurants and shops, Metro-North, public transportation, and I-95, this is a polished, low-maintenance townhouse-style home that lives larger than expected.
