About this home

Steeped in history yet thoughtfully reimagined for modern living, this expanded and recently updated antique home offers the rare combination of timeless character, exceptional functionality, and a coveted location just an easy stroll to downtown Westport. Designed for both everyday living and easy entertaining, the home features warm gathering spaces and private retreats. A chef&apos;s kitchen with Thermador range and walk-in pantry opens to the home&apos;s inviting living and dining areas. Over $600K of recent updates including the stunning, newly-redesigned primary suite - a true sanctuary, with a new focal wall with custom cabinetry and gas fireplace, a well-equipped bath with dual sinks, tub and shower, plus a distinctive turreted sitting room, an idyllic space to relax. Multiple home office options. A new outbuilding elevates the property to an entirely new level. Designed as a private wellness retreat, this extraordinary Onsen-inspired space features hot and cold plunge pools, a sauna, shower, gas fireplace, and loft, creating a resort-quality experience. Outside, the sunny, level property features a new, walled-garden, crushed-stone patio and a 2nd patio, allowing for entertaining, gardening, and enjoying the peaceful surroundings. Room for pool! Town water/gas/sewer. Rich in character, beautifully updated, and uniquely equipped for modern wellness and work-from-home living, this exceptional residence offers a lifestyle rarely found in this historic, near-town neighborhood.
