About this home

Beach Town Living at Its Finest! Experience the pinnacle of Westport living in this stunning new architectural gem. Situated in the prestigious Hunt Club neighborhood, this classic Colonial is set on a private rear lot offering privacy and serenity. Built to the highest standards by a local craftsman, the home spans four expansive levels of living space with 6 bedrooms, 5.1 baths, and seamless transitional design. The light-filled, open-concept floor plan is outfitted with every modern amenity, anchored by a true chef&apos;s kitchen featuring a commercial-grade 8-burner Wolf range, a full suite of stainless steel appliances, a classic subway tile backsplash, and a center island built for both cooking and entertaining. The adjoining family room invites you in with a stone fireplace &amp; custom built-ins. The second level offers a thoughtfully designed layout for guests alike: a luxurious primary suite with a spa-like bath featuring a soaking tub and double vanity, two bedrooms connected by a Jack-and-Jill bath, and two additional bedrooms sharing a full hall bath &amp; second-floor laundry room. The fourth level delivers exceptional flex space and a sixth bedroom with its own private en-suite bath, ideal for guests, an au pair, or a private home office retreat.
