About this home

Storybook Cape nestled in one of Darien&apos;s most sought-after in-town neighborhoods. From the moment you step through the front door, this home captivates with its warmth, charm, and thoughtfully updated interiors. The heart of the home is the beautifully renovated kitchen featuring new gourmet appliances, Quartz countertops, and an open flow into the dramatic family room with soaring ceilings, fireplace, and custom built-ins. The stunning primary suite offers a true retreat with a renovated spa-like bath, walk-in closet, and French doors that open to a private secret patio. A gracious living room with an additional fireplace, formal dining room, and sun-filled sunroom complete the main level. Upstairs are three additional bedrooms and two full baths. Tucked away off a separate staircase is an impressive great room with custom built-ins and abundant natural light, offering the perfect flexible space for a playroom, office, or media room. Ideally located just moments from downtown Darien&apos;s shops, restaurants, and train, this exceptional home combines timeless character with modern living in an unbeatable location.
