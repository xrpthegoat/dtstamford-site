About this home

Set on one of North Stamford&apos;s most picturesque streets, this classic Center Hall Colonial is ready for its next chapter. With timeless architecture, generous living spaces, and incredible potential, this is a home where imagination can truly take center stage. Featuring 4 bedrooms, 3.5 bathrooms, and a flexible floor plan, the residence is filled with possibilities. A formal dining room creates the perfect setting for gatherings, while two fireplaces add warmth and character throughout. The den and expansive family room provide comfortable spaces for relaxing, working from home, or spending time with family and friends. One of the home&apos;s unique features is the potential for a main-level primary suite, offering flexibility for multigenerational living, aging in place, or simply creating a private retreat on the first floor. Upstairs, the existing primary suite includes an ensuite bath and generous proportions. Surrounded by the natural beauty North Stamford is known for, this property combines privacy, charm, and endless possibilities. Whether restoring its classic elegance, incorporating modern updates, or completely reimagining the space, the foundation is here. A rare chance to create something truly special in one of Stamford&apos;s most desirable settings.
