About this home

Experience refined living in this exquisitely custom ranch on the coveted Norwalk/Rowayton/Darien border. Thoughtfully redesigned with exceptional craftsmanship and timeless finishes, this turnkey residence offers over 5,200 square feet of beautifully finished living space, including 4 spacious bedrooms, 4 full baths, and a flexible layout with potential for a private 2-bedroom in-law suite with its own separate entrance. Designed for effortless entertaining, the open-concept floor plan features a stunning designer kitchen with custom white cabinetry, premium stainless-steel appliances, a massive center island, and seamless flow into the expansive living and dining areas. A spectacular great room with soaring ceilings and skylights fills the home with natural light. The fully finished lower level offers endless possibilities for recreation, a home gym, office, or guest quarters. Recent upgrades include a new roof, new central air system, rich hardwood flooring throughout, and a spacious laundry room with custom cabinetry and utility sink. Set on a private, level 3/4-acre property, the backyard is a true retreat with a brand-new stone patio, exceptional privacy, abundant parking, and a two-car garage. Ideally located on the Darien border with convenient access to New York, this exceptional home offers luxury, versatility, and modern living in one of Fairfield County&apos;s most desirable locations.
