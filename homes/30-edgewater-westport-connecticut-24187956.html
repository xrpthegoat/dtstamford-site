About this home

It is the luxury rental you have been waiting for... Perfectly positioned at the edge of Mill Pond, this exceptional home offers breathtaking views and fabulous sunrises from every room. Whether you are looking for a summer vacation or considering making Westport your home, this is a rare opportunity to experience the very best of coastal living.
