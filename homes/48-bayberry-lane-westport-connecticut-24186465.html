About this home

Set on a breathtaking professionally designed acre, 48 Bayberry Lane is a beautifully reimagined 6,775sf estate offering four finished levels of exceptional living. Thoughtfully enhanced &amp; meticulously maintained by the current owner, the home blends timeless elegance with modern comfort in a private, resort-like setting. A dramatic double-height foyer welcomes sun-filled interiors featuring bespoke details, a formal dining room with butler&apos;s pantry, elegant living room with gas fplc and custom built-ins, and an expansive family room anchored by a wood burning fireplace. Designed with intention for both everyday living &amp; entertaining, the eat-in chef&apos;s kitchen is a culinary showpiece, flowing seamlessly to a custom terrace through walls of glass doors, creating effortless indoor-outdoor living. Created for relaxation and gathering, the spectacular grounds feature multiple dining &amp; lounging terraces, a built-in grill station, extensive stonework, curated gardens, and a large pool with sun shelf, all surrounded by mature evergreens for exceptional privacy. The residence offers 6 spacious bdrms, including a luxurious primary suite w/fplc, 2 walk-in closets, and a spa-inspired bath. A finished 3rd floor &amp; walk-out lower level provide outstanding flexibility with recreation space, gym, office/guest or au pair accommodations. Ideally located near Westport beaches, downtown, dining, shopping, &amp; train, this impressive property offers a lifestyle of convenience &amp; sophistication.
