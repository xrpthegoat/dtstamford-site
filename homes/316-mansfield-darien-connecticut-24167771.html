About this home

Just in time for summer-this exceptional property offers the ultimate in luxury living and outdoor enjoyment. Imagine relaxing poolside by your new heated pool and spa, surrounded by beautifully landscaped grounds and sweeping views of the golf course. Inside, the home is equally impressive. This remarkable colonial seamlessly blends elegance, comfort, and modern convenience-perfect for making unforgettable summer memories and beyond.
