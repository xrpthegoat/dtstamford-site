About this home

Tucked into a picturesque 1.28-acre setting in West Norwalk, this spacious 2,912 sqft split-level ranch offers versatility, natural beauty and endless potential. The home features a large kitchen with center island, dining area and direct access to a deck overlooking the backyard. A formal dining room flows seemlessly into the living room where a fireplace and oversized skylight create a bright, light-filled space ideal for everyday living and entertaining. The home offers 3 bedrooms and 2.5 baths. The third bedroom includes its own bath and sitting area, offering excellent potential for an in-law setup, guest accommodations, or private home office space. The lower level expands the living space with a family room featuring a fireplace, adding even more room to gather. The outdoor setting is what truly makes this property special. A large fenced backyard provides ample space for entertaining, recreation, pets, or gardening, while the scenic stream, tranquil pond, and charming wooden bridges create a storybook backdrop unlike anything else. A whole-house generator adds additional convenience and peace of mind. Rich with character and opportunity, this estate property is being sold &quot;As is&quot; and is ready for its next chapter. No probate delay expected.
