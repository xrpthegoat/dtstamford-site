About this home

Wilton Crest - 2 bedroom, 2 full and 1 half bath townhome with the living room, dining room, kitchen, primary bedroom, primary bathroom, laundry, half bath, and two decks all on one level. The second bedroom and full bathroom are on the second floor, and a full unfinished basement is on the lower floor. The unit is just a short walk from your garage, and just six steps up from the walkway brings you into the front door. Vaulted ceilings, skylights, sliders to the decks provide a light, bright and open feel to this home. Just freshly painted throughout. New hardwood floors in the primary bedroom, and refinished hardwood floors in the living room and dining room. Granite counters and stainless steel appliances in the kitchen.
