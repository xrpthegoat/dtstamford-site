About this home

Convenience and serenity come together at 59 Creeping Hemlock Drive, a beautifully maintained home with an in-ground pool on a quiet street in Norwalk&apos;s desirable Cranbury neighborhood. Major investments include a new roof, new high-efficiency heating &amp; cooling systems, conversion from oil to propane, updated mechanicals, enhanced air filtration, a new hot water system &amp; smart thermostat technology. These improvements provide comfort, efficiency &amp; tremendous savings on utilities. Filled with natural sunlight, this inviting home offers 4 bedrooms &amp; 3 full baths, including newly renovated bathrooms &amp; a finished lower level that provides flexible space for recreation, guests, a home office or gym. This lower level walks out to a large patio and sparkling pool with new marble coping, a new pool pump motor and new pool heater. Hardwood floors flow throughout the main living areas, with new hardwood in the primary bedroom &amp; living room. The dining room with fireplace creates a beautiful gathering space and flows effortlessly into both the kitchen &amp; family room, creating a layout that lives comfortably and is ideal for everyday living and entertaining. The kitchen features granite countertops, stainless steel appliances &amp; abundant cabinetry. Enjoy the privacy of a peaceful neighborhood while being just moments from shopping, restaurants, parks, golf courses, Route 7 &amp; the Merritt Parkway, offering easy access to everything Norwalk has to offer!
