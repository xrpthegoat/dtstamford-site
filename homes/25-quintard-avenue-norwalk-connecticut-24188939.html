About this home

A Rare Opportunity to lease a designer-owned Historic Colonial Farm House masterfully renovated from top to bottom and offered for immediate rent for the first time. The showpiece of the home is a stunning new eat-in kitchen featuring stainless steel appliances, custom built-in coffee bar, abundant cabinetry, generous prep space, and room for an island and dining table. Rich in character yet impeccably modern, this sophisticated residence showcases soaring ceilings, oversized sun-filled windows, and timeless architectural details blended with elegant designer finishes. The main level also offers a gracious living room with pocket doors for effortless entertaining and a spacious first-floor bedroom with an oversized closet. Relax on the expansive covered front porch with seasonal harbor views. Upstairs are three generously sized bedrooms with water views and a beautifully updated full bath with tub. Additional amenities include a rear entrance, shared basement storage, two off-street parking spaces, space for grilling and future yard to play. Ideally located just 15 minute walk to Metro-North and SONO Collection mall, 10 minute walk to SONO restaurants, parks and pickleball.
