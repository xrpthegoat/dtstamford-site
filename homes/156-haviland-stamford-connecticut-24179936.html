About this home

Set on one of North Stamford&apos;s most sought-after lanes, this beautifully refreshed New England Colonial blends timeless elegance with modern functionality, offering an ideal layout for today&apos;s lifestyle. A sun-filled, open floor plan welcomes you with a spacious living room that flows seamlessly into the formal dining room-perfect for both everyday living and entertaining. The eat-in kitchen features all new stainless steel appliances, including a washer and dryer, and opens to a warm and inviting family room complete with custom built-ins and a fireplace. A separate sunroom, framed by French doors, leads to a rear deck and private flagstone patio-creating a perfect indoor-outdoor connection. Flexible spaces throughout the home offer endless possibilities for a home office, sitting room, or playroom. Upstairs, the expansive primary suite provides a true retreat, featuring an en-suite bath, a large exercise or sitting room, multiple closets, vaulted ceilings, and oversized windows that flood the space with natural light. The finished lower level offers additional living space, ideal for a playroom, media room, or private retreat. Ideally located in lower North Stamford, within a mile of The Stamford Museum &amp; Nature Center, Chestnut Hill Park, Tennis Courts, Dog Park plus just minutes from the Merritt Parkway, shopping, top-rated schools, downtown Stamford, and the train station, this move-in-ready home is the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and style.
