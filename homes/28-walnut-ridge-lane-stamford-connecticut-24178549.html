About this home

Welcome to 28 Walnut Ridge Lane, nestled within a desirable enclave of newer homes on a private cul-de-sac, just minutes to vibrant downtown Stamford. Built in 2005 and spanning 4,164 square feet, this exceptional 4 bedroom, 3 full bath, 2 half bath home impresses with soaring ceilings, rich hardwood floors, and floor-to-ceiling windows that flood the main level with natural light. Elegant fixtures, freshly painted interiors, and an open, airy layout create an effortlessly polished feel throughout. The classic center hall Colonial welcomes you with a formal living room and elegant dining room. The recently renovated chef&apos;s kitchen features quartz countertops, a glazed tile backsplash, professional appliances, and a seated breakfast bar island, opening seamlessly to a breakfast area and family room. Step out to the deck for effortless outdoor entertaining. Upstairs, the primary suite offers a walk-in closet and spa-like en suite. Additional bedrooms are generously sized, one with its own en suite and two sharing a Jack and Jill bath, plus a custom cabinetry laundry room. The finished lower level offers over 1,000 square feet of living space including a rec room, gym, office, and possible fifth bedroom. Outside, one of the largest and most private backyards in the enclave awaits, beautifully proportioned for entertaining and play, and complete with a fire pit.
