About this home

Wonderful opportunity to own this charming 4 bedroom 2.5 bath home in desirable South Wilton, ideally situated adjacent to the Norwalk River Valley Trail. Combining timeless character with everyday comfort, this inviting home features hardwood floors throughout and an updated eat-in kitchen with sliders to a deck, perfect for morning coffee. The spacious dining room opens through French doors to a large, 238 sq.ft. 3-season porch with cathedral ceiling and skylights creating a bright and relaxing space to enjoy the outdoors throughout the seasons. The main floor also offers an elegant living room featuring built-ins and a fireplace, a family room, a half bath and mudroom. Upstairs you&apos;ll find four nice-size, light-filled bedrooms and 2 full baths. A 3rd floor bonus room provides flexible space for a playroom, home office or media room and there is an additional large, walk-in storage area. The unfinished walkout basement adds tremendous potential, giving future owners the opportunity to customize and expand the home&apos;s living space to suit their lifestyle. Ideally located in South Wilton, this home offers easy access to commuting routes including Merritt and I95, town amenities, shopping, the Norwalk River Valley Trail, and Wilton&apos;s award-winning schools.
