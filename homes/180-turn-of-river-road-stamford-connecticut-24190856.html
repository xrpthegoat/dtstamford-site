About this home

Move right into this beautifully updated end-unit townhouse in Stamford&apos;s highly desirable Riverturn complex. The main level showcases timeless finishes, beginning with a brand-new kitchen featuring stainless steel appliances, quartz countertops, and a matching quartz backsplash. The open-concept living and dining areas are highlighted by rich dark hardwood floors, elegant lighting, and a cozy gas fireplace. A stylish half bath, accented with grasscloth wallpaper, adds a sophisticated touch to the main level. Upstairs, the sun-filled primary suite features a vaulted ceiling, skylight with a newly installed electric shade, and a recently renovated en-suite bathroom. The generously sized second bedroom offers ample closet space and easy access to the beautifully updated hall bath. The top-floor loft provides endless possibilities-ideal as a home office, playroom, guest room, or creative space. The walk-out lower level offers additional flexible living space with durable vinyl flooring, a chic laundry room with designer wallpaper, and plenty of room for a family room, home gym, or recreation area. The Riverturn complex provides easy access to shopping, restaurants, the Merritt parkway and minutes from Stamford&apos;s downtown area.
