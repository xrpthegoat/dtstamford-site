About this home

Fabulous opportunity along the 5 Mile River!!!! Spacious River-fronting, 4000+SF Custom Contemporary on the border of Darien and New Canaan. Offering unique architectural details and modern flexibility with over 3279 SF of 1st floor living and an additional 764 SF on the 2nd floor. Currently configured as a 3 bdrm, 3 bath home that can easily, transform into a 5+ bdrm, 5 bath home within the existing footprint. Highlights include: Dramatic Cathedral Ceilings, floor to ceiling field stone fireplaces, large 1st floor primary suite (that can function as private in-law quarters), multi-use loft spaces, walls of glass, abundant water and nature views, 4 fireplaces (3 fieldstone and 1 brick), an attached, oversized 2 car garage with loft and bonus outdoor carport, remarkable privacy and a central location with quick easy access to I-95, Merritt Pkwy and the NYC train. A true hidden gem. Being sold As-is with inspections for informational purposes only. A wonderful, one of a kind, must see property!
