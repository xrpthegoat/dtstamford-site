About this home

Beautifully rebuilt in 2006, this custom turn-key retreat offers 4 spacious Bedrooms with the flexibility for a 5th, 3 full &amp; 2 half baths, all set on 2.33 acres of private, picturesque grounds; just 1 hour away from NYC. Arrive to curated landscaping surrounded by mature trees and seasonal blooms, including hydrangeas, geraniums, and climbing mandevilla, complemented by bespoke stonework and hand-crafted stepping stone paths. As you step inside, soaring ceilings and gleaming hardwood floors set the tone for the home&apos;s expansive, open-concept, anchored by a striking spiral staircase that makes an unforgettable first impression. Natural light pours through French doors, seamlessly connecting indoor and outdoor living. The living room, perfectly positioned off the grand foyer and family room, is filled with natural light and features a second fireplace crafted from premium natural limestone. The Family room is a true centerpiece, featuring vaulted ceilings &amp; a grand wood-burning fireplace. Both warm and inviting, it&apos;s the perfect setting for cozy game nights or relaxing with hot chocolate by the fire while watching the snowfall outside. The Chef&apos;s Kitchen is both grand and functional, featuring high-end Viking appliances and designed for effortless entertaining, with seamless flow to the Dining and Living rooms and direct access to the outdoor patio, perfect for al fresco dining on a summer night.
