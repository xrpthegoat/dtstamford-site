About this home

Set on over two acres of exquisitely manicured grounds, this exceptional six-bedroom Georgian estate offers approximately 8,500 square feet of beautifully designed living space, including the finished lower level. Surrounded by mature landscaping and complete privacy, the residence seamlessly blends timeless architecture with modern luxury. Elegant formal living and dining rooms, multiple fireplaces, French doors, custom millwork, and a richly appointed library create an atmosphere of refined sophistication. The chef&apos;s kitchen features premium appliances, custom cabinetry, a marble center island, and a sun-filled solarium breakfast room that opens to the inviting family room. The luxurious primary suite is a private retreat with a fireplace, sitting room, expansive walk-in closet, and spa-inspired marble bath. Five additional bedrooms provide exceptional accommodations for family and guests. Designed for year-round enjoyment, the resort-style grounds feature a spectacular pool and spa, expansive entertaining terraces, an outdoor kitchen with two grills and a wood-fired pizza oven, a gas fire pit, greenhouse, and organic gardens. The finished lower level offers a media room, fitness center, wine cellar, game room, and flexible recreation space. Complete with a heated three-car garage and approved seven-bedroom replacement area, this distinguished Greenwich estate presents a rare opportunity to enjoy luxury living in one of the town&apos;s most coveted private settings.
