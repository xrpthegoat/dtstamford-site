About this home

Welcome to 47 London Lane -a beautifully updated 4-bedroom home tucked away on a quiet cul-de-sac in one of Stamford&apos;s most convenient commuter locations. With a newly remodeled kitchen and bathrooms, plus a 500-square-foot east-facing deck overlooking the Rippowam River, this home blends style, comfort, and everyday livability. The open-concept main level is filled with natural light and features a brand-new kitchen (2026) with quartz and butcher-block countertops, modern hardware, and new appliances. The adjacent dining area opens directly to the expansive deck, creating seamless indoor-outdoor living and an ideal setting for entertaining, relaxing, or enjoying the morning sun. The main level offers four generous bedrooms and potential to create a spacious primary suite, while a fully updated modern bathroom completes the floor. The finished lower level adds exceptional flexibility with a sound-treated family room, wood-burning fireplace, new half bath, laundry area, and large storage room with potential for a guest suite, gym, additional bedroom, or home office. Enjoy a quarter-acre lot, a peaceful backyard overlooking the Rippowam River, and an oversized two-car garage. The cul-de-sac setting offers a quiet residential environment with limited through traffic. Conveniently located minutes from the Merritt Parkway, Metro-North, parks, shopping, dining, and Stamford amenities, this move-in-ready home offers space, flexibility, and modern updates in a desirable location!
