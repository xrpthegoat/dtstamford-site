About this home

Move right into this beautifully remodeled 2-3 bedroom ranch in the highly sought-after Glenbrook neighborhood. Tucked away on a quiet side street, this home offers the perfect balance of peace and convenience, sitting just minutes from local restaurants, shops, the Glenbrook train station, I-95, and Cove Island beach. The bright, open-concept floor plan is an entertainer&apos;s dream. At the heart of the home is a charming, fully updated kitchen featuring quartz countertops and brand-new appliances with natural gas range. Key upgrades include a brand-new roof on both the house and the oversized 2-car garage (which features a bonus loft with endless potential), central AC, an upgraded electrical panel, and newly refinished hardwood floors. With nothing left to do, you can simply unpack and enjoy!
