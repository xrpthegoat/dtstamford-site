About this home

Originally the sole residence on Wildcat Road, this distinguished 1925 stone manor blends timeless architectural elegance with a commanding setting. Located within Darien&apos;s coveted Delafield Island private association, the property is approached by a sweeping circular drive and surrounded by mature landscaping offering a rare sense of privacy, grandeur, and permanence, just minutes from downtown Darien, the Metro-North train, beaches, and Great Island. Rich in character and craftsmanship, the residence showcases architectural details rarely found today, including soaring 12-foot ceilings, a dramatic circular staircase, five fireplaces, Juliet balconies, vintage servant bells, graceful arches, skylights, and a sun-filled glass solarium. Thoughtfully designed for both grand entertaining and comfortable everyday living, the spacious country kitchen opens to an expansive deck overlooking the surrounding natural landscape. Adding to the property&apos;s exceptional appeal, several acres of protected land trust border the rear of the home, preserving a tranquil backdrop and enhancing the estate-like setting. Whether hosting family gatherings, entertaining guests, or simply enjoying the peace and beauty of its surroundings, 15 Wildcat Road offers a unique opportunity to own one of Delafield Island&apos;s most distinctive and historic homes.
