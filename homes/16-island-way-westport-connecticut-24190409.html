About this home

Imagine starting your day with a walk to a private beach, an afternoon on the water, and dinner just minutes away in vibrant Saugatuck. This beautifully furnished home offers the lifestyle that makes Westport one of Connecticut&apos;s most coveted coastal communities. Walk or bike to three private association beaches with Adirondack chairs, picnic areas, kayak racks, and grills, then enjoy the nearby amenities of Longshore Club Park, including golf, tennis, sailing, swimming, and waterfront dining. Just minutes from the train, Main Street boutiques, and acclaimed restaurants, the location offers the perfect blend of seaside tranquility and everyday convenience. Inside, sun-filled living spaces feature soaring ceilings, custom millwork, rich hardwood floors, and wood-burning fireplaces that create a warm yet sophisticated atmosphere. The chef&apos;s kitchen is appointed with professional-grade appliances, an oversized marble island, and generous space for entertaining. Upstairs, four spacious bedrooms, including a serene primary suite with a spa-inspired bath, provide a peaceful retreat after a day by the water. Thoughtfully designed indoor and outdoor living spaces complete this exceptional opportunity to experience the very best of Westport living. Available September 10, 2026 to June 30, 2027
