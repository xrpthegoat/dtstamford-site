About this home

A truly pristine 2 bedroom, 2 1/2 bath end unit Townhouse in very desirable Wilton Crest is a rare opportunity for the next lucky homeowners. Spacious and chic, this lovely home has been tastefully updated throughout. Stunning living room with vaulted ceiling, fireplace, hardwood floor and sliders leading out to deck is ideal for entertaining. The renovated kitchen boasts new Bosch 800 series appliances, granite counters, subway tile back splash, lots of cabinets and a walkout to the charming patio area. Light-filled dining room offers a beautiful view to a serene setting of trees and nature. Expansive first floor primary suite with ample double closets, vaulted ceiling, patio access &amp; luxurious updated bathroom with jetted tub, double sinks, sky light and large stall shower is a dream oasis. Upstairs you will find another generously sized bedroom and full bath. The lower level is finished with LVP flooring &amp; offers flexible living spaces for playroom and office. In addition there is a laundry room with new GE washer/dryer and convenient access to the garage. So many improvements including new A/C in 2021, new furnace in 2022 &amp; new water heater in 2025. Amazing opportunity to live in a coveted community where residents enjoy premium amenities including a pool &amp; tennis courts and can walk into town for shopping and restaurants. This special home, surrounded by trees, is private and offers a lifestyle of convenience and low maintenance. Not to be missed! Showings begin Sat.
