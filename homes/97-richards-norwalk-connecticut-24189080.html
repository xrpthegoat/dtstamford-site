About this home

Welcome to this beautifully renovated top-floor condo offering 1,196 sq. ft. of bright, spacious living. This light-filled 2-bedroom, 2-bath home overlooks peaceful wooded views and features soaring cathedral ceilings, skylights, a cozy fireplace, and two sets of sliding glass doors that open to a large, sun-filled deck-ideal for relaxing or entertaining. The eat-in kitchen is appointed with granite countertops and stainless steel appliances. The inviting living and dining areas offer a comfortable and open layout for everyday living and hosting. A full-size washer and dryer are conveniently located in the unit. Enjoy the community pool and the ease of two reserved parking spaces, including one covered carport. Ideally located close to I-95, train stations, and shopping, this home offers excellent commuter access while maintaining a peaceful setting. Excellent references required.
