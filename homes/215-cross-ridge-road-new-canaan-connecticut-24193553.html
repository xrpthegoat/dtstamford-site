About this home

Come see this spectacular colonial farmhouse as seen in publications such as Connecticut Cottages &amp; Gardens, Country Living, Architectural Digest along with numerous catalogues, books, and advertising campaigns this bucolic and exquisitely designed 5 bedroom, 5.5 bath is not to be missed. Design by a renowned kitchen and bath designer as her personal house and then elevated by a leader in the home industry, this house provides a seamless floor plan. A large bonus space above the garage is illuminated year round by natural light making it the perfect family hangout in the cooler months and a professionally designed garden and fountain provide for exquisite 3 season outdoor living and entertaining space. The basement yields an additional space and a full bath for an effective au pair or in-law suite. Custom high-end window treatments throughout that are also motorized in the bedrooms, combine with perfectly selected light-fixtures to further enhance the home. This house is tucked into the side of Cross Ridge Road providing the feeling of privacy all while maintaining that neighborhood feel. Schedule your tour today as this one will not last long.
