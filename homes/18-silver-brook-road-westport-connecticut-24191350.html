About this home

Welcome to 18 Silver Brook Road, a beautifully updated and meticulously maintained home set on a level, private acre in one of Westport&apos;s most beloved neighborhoods. Located in the sought-after Silver Brook/Bonnie Brook community, this move-in ready residence offers the perfect blend of timeless charm, generous living spaces, and exceptional indoor-outdoor living. The spacious eat-in kitchen flows seamlessly into the vaulted family room, creating a natural gathering place for everyday living. Formal living and dining rooms, a sun-filled office, and a spectacular great room with exposed beams, a fireplace, and French doors opening to the terrace provide exceptional spaces for entertaining and relaxing alike. Upstairs, the serene primary suite features vaulted ceilings, dual walk-in closets, and a spa-inspired bath. Three additional bedrooms, well-appointed baths, and a dedicated laundry room complete the second level. Lovingly cared for and thoughtfully enhanced, the home has seen extensive recent improvements, including pool equipment and liner replacement, smart home upgrades, energy-efficiency enhancements, chimney reconstruction, basement waterproofing, and significant landscaping improvements. Every detail has been attended to, allowing the next owner to move in and immediately enjoy everything this exceptional home has to offer.
