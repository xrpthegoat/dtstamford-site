About this home

Perfectly positioned in Stamford&apos;s sought after Westover neighborhood, this charming home offers comfort, flexibility, and beautiful golf course views. Featuring 3 bedrooms and 2 full bathrooms, the thoughtfully designed layout includes a desirable main level primary bedroom, providing the option for easy one floor living while offering additional space upstairs for family, guests, or a home office. The inviting living room is anchored by a stunning stone wood burning fireplace, while the eat in kitchen serves as the heart of the home with ample space for casual dining and gathering. Spacious living and dining areas are filled with natural light from large windows that showcase picturesque golf course views, and the open front porch is the perfect place to enjoy your morning coffee or unwind at the end of the day. The finished lower level adds valuable bonus space and includes a kitchenette and laundry area with washer and dryer, making it ideal for recreation, extended family, hobbies, or storage. A detached two car garage adds functionality, and the prime Westover location offers convenient access to shopping, dining, parks, and everyday amenities. With scenic views, a flexible floor plan, and a highly desirable location, this home presents a wonderful opportunity in one of Stamford&apos;s most established neighborhoods.
