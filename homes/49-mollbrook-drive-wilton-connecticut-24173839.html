About this home

Tucked away at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac in desirable Lower Lower Wilton on the Westport border, this beautifully updated Colonial offers the perfect blend of privacy, convenience, and lifestyle. Set on 2.35 flat, dry acres with an in-ground pool, this 4-bedroom, 2.5-bath home features approximately 3,500 square feet of thoughtfully designed living space. A newly renovated eat-in kitchen showcases quartzite countertops, stainless steel appliances, and seamless flow for everyday living and entertaining. Spacious living and family rooms each feature wood-burning fireplaces, while a dedicated home office and sun-filled bonus room with polished concrete flooring provide flexible spaces for work, relaxation, or recreation. The second floor offers four generously sized bedrooms, including a spacious primary suite, along with the convenience of second-floor laundry. A walk-out lower level features an exceptional mudroom area with abundant storage and functionality. Additional highlights include city water, an ideal commuter location close to both Wilton and Westport amenities, and a rare combination of level land, neighborhood setting, and move-in-ready condition. Wired for generator. Underground electric.
