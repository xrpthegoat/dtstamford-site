About this home

Tucked away on a quiet lane this spectacular sun-drenched Colonial offers modern living with a seamless connection to nature. A dramatic two-story entry foyer with a graceful sweeping staircase sets an impressive tone, opening to a thoughtfully designed floor plan that lives effortlessly and suits today&apos;s lifestyle The main level is anchored by a wonderful sense of flow - from the inviting living room and elegant and sunny dining room to the beautifully renovated kitchen featuring a large island with waterfall countertops and casual dining area, through to the stylish family room with fireplace. Entertaining comes naturally here, whether hosting intimate dinners, holiday gatherings, or summer evenings on the oversized deck and beautiful stone patio below. Upstairs, the primary suite is a true retreat, appointed with a generous walk-in closet and a luxurious bath. Three additional well-proportioned bedrooms as well as a newly updated laundry room complete the upper level. A versatile bonus room above the three-car garage offers endless possibilities as a media room, studio, or playroom. The finished lower level extends the living space even further, offering a recreation room with slider access to the patio, an additional bedroom, full bath, and a private office - ideal for guests or flexible everyday use. Impeccably maintained and beautifully landscaped, this is a home that is as polished as it is livable.
