About this home

Lovingly cared for by the same family for more than 50 years, this well-maintained, 3-bed, 1-bath ranch is located in the highly desirable Glenville School District. The main level features an open and inviting living room and dining area, an eat-in kitchen, 3 bedrooms, and a full bath. The walkout lower level offers additional living space with a large family room, featuring a fireplace, a laundry room, and direct access to a terrace and private backyard-perfect for relaxing or entertaining. A two-car attached garage provides added convenience. With nearly 1,200 sq ft of remaining FAR, this property offers an exceptional opportunity to expand and customize. A rare chance to make this cherished home your own. Situated in a wonderful neighborhood, this home enjoys a prime Greenwich location close to shopping, parks, transportation, and just minutes from Westchester County.
