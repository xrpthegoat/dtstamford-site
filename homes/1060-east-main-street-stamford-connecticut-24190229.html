About this home

Rare find! This beautifully renovated, move-in ready 2-bedroom townhouse offers comfortable, low-maintenance living in the peaceful Fairlawn complex. The bright, updated kitchen features granite countertops, ample cabinetry, and opens to the dining area, creating a functional space for everyday living and entertaining. The inviting living room provides a warm and comfortable setting, while the modern bathroom and two generously sized bedrooms with ample closet space complete the second floor. Attractive and durable luxury vinyl flooring runs throughout the home, and the basement offers additional storage along with a convenient laundry facility located directly beneath the unit. The HOA covers heat, hot and cold water, snow removal, lawn maintenance, and trash removal, making homeownership virtually stress-free. This pet-friendly community includes two parking permits with the unit. Ideally located just minutes from I-95, public transportation, Metro-North train station, downtown Stamford, shopping, restaurants, parks, beaches, and schools, this home offers the perfect combination of comfort, convenience, and value.
