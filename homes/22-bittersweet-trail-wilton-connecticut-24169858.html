About this home

Welcome to this inviting 4-bedroom, 2-bath Hi-Ranch, ideally situated on a quiet cul-de-sac in desirable South Wilton. Designed for comfortable everyday living and easy entertaining, the main level features a bright living room that flows seamlessly into the dining room and an updated kitchen with granite countertops, opening to a den. The primary bedroom, two additional bedrooms, and a full bath complete the main level. The lower level offers exceptional flexibility with a family room featuring a cozy fireplace, a fourth bedroom, full bath, laundry room, and convenient access to the attached two-car garage. Set on a picturesque, level property, the backyard is ideal for outdoor enjoyment with a generous deck perfect for relaxing, dining al fresco, or entertaining family and friends. Enjoy the best of both privacy and convenience with this sought-after location just minutes from Wilton&apos;s town center, Metro-North train station, shopping, restaurants, parks, and major highways, making commuting and daily errands effortless. A wonderful opportunity to make this welcoming home your own.
