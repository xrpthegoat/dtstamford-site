About this home

Welcome to a slice of heaven &amp; an opportunity to own a piece of history on 2 acres in Back Country Greenwich. Upon entering through the front gate, you will be transported to a tranquil oasis and welcomed into an inviting home that was thoughtfully redesigned and renovated so that the new serves to complement the old throughout. Spacious living room with original beams, bookshelves and window seat is perfect for family time, rainy day reading or entertaining. Hallway leads to formal dining room with built-ins and French doors and also into bright eat-in chef&apos;s kitchen with vaulted ceiling and skylights. Opens to patio and beautifully planted, well maintained grounds. Romantic primary suite/FP opens to outside as well. Next level offers an en suite bedroom as well as two additional bedrooms with shared bathroom. Delightful family room, laundry room and mudroom with laundry are on lower level with access to 2 car garage. Beautiful grounds with back gate access to pond (shared with 2 families) and beyond a trail that leads to Greenwich Trails, offering direct path from garden. This special home and property offer wonderful opportunities for outdoor lifestyle and proximity to the charming town of Armonk, which is 2 miles away. Just half mile to Modern Barn Restaurant, pizza place, wine shop, deli.
