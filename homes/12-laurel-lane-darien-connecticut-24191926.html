About this home

Nestled on a quiet, tree-lined lane in one of Darien&apos;s most desirable neighborhoods, this expanded cape offers the perfect blend of timeless charm, generous living space, &amp; endless potential. A welcoming foyer leads to elegant formal living &amp; dining rooms, where the inviting living room is anchored by a dramatic floor-to-ceiling stone fireplace, while the spectacular oversized family room impresses with soaring ceilings &amp; walls of windows overlooking the private backyard. The kitchen features granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, &amp; seamless access to an expansive deck designed for effortless indoor-outdoor entertaining. Step outside to a beautifully landscaped backyard framed by mature trees &amp; exceptional privacy. The second level features a spacious primary bedroom with multiple walk-in closets &amp; an adjoining oversized bonus room, offering the ideal space for a private home office, nursery, or the opportunity to create a luxurious primary suite. Two additional generously sized bedrooms &amp; two full baths complete the upper level. The finished lower level provides flexibility with a spacious recreation room featuring a second stone fireplace, abundant storage, &amp; unfinished space for future expansion. Perfectly positioned just moments from award-winning Darien schools, Woodland Park, Metro-North, dining, beaches, &amp; major commuter routes, this exceptional home offers the ideal balance of privacy, convenience, &amp; the quintessential Darien lifestyle.
