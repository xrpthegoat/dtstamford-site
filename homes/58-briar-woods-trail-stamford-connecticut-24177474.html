About this home

Tucked into a serene, nature-filled setting, this beautifully updated contemporary ranch offers the ease of true one level living paired with an open, airy layout designed for today&apos;s lifestyle. Sunlight pours through newer windows, illuminating the expansive living spaces and creating a warm, inviting atmosphere throughout. At the heart of the home, the stylish kitchen seamlessly connects to the surrounding oversized living, dining, and family rooms, offering the perfect flow for both everyday living and effortless entertaining. Walls of glass and easy access to the spacious deck invite the outdoors in, overlooking beautifully landscaped grounds framed by mature trees and natural beauty. The private primary suite w/ large walk in closet and en-suite bath feels like a peaceful retreat, surrounded by tranquil views and abundant natural light. Two additional bedrooms complete the main level. The thoughtfully finished lower level expands the home&apos;s versatility w/ a full bath w/heated floors &amp; flexible space ideal for guests, a home gym, media room, multiple work from home offices, or potential 4th and 5th bedroom options. Outside is a private garden area &amp; storage shed. Additional highlights include large 2 car garage, newer AC unit, updated windows, beautiful landscaping, and a private pool, pool house and tennis court exclusively for the Briar Woods community. A rare opportunity to enjoy spacious ranch style living in a setting that feels both peaceful &amp; connected to nature.
