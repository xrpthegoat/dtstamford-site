About this home

Located in the heart of downtown Stamford, this updated 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom condo offers a convenient location just minutes from I-95, Metro North, Bedford Street, restaurants, shopping and nightlife. The unit features an updated kitchen with stainless steel appliances and a gas cooktop, a spacious living room with a fireplace, and access to a private balcony overlooking downtown Stamford. The full bathroom has been tastefully updated, while the large bedroom offers plenty of closet space and city views. Fountain Terrace residents enjoy secured entry, elevators, on site laundry, assigned parking, an inground pool, and a playground. This unit is also one of only four on its floor with direct interior access from the elevator, allowing residents to reach their front door without stepping outside. Whether you&apos;re a first time purchaser, downsizing, or looking for a convenient downtown lifestyle, this move in ready unit offers a great combination of location, comfort, and amenities.
