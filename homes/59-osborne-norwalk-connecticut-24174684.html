About this home

Beautifully appointed, newer townhouse in convenient East Norwalk location. Minutes to train, shops, restaurants, Calf Pasture Beach, Norwalk Aquarium and parks. This 3-bedroom, 2.5-bath home features an open-concept floor plan with high ceilings, hardwood floors, crown molding, recessed lighting, and premium finishes throughout. The sun-filled kitchen showcases Carrara marble countertops, stainless steel appliances, a large center island with seating area, and seamlessly flows into the dining and living areas. The living room features a beautiful gas fireplace and sliders out to a bluestone patio and fenced yard, ideal for al fresco dining and entertaining. Upstairs, the spacious primary suite features a tray ceiling, walk-in closet, and luxurious bath with double vanity and oversized shower. Two additional bedrooms, full bath, and laundry complete the second floor. The finished lower level provides flexible space ideal for a family room/playroom, home office, gym, or media room, plus a large enclosed storage area. Boutique 4-unit association. Attached garage plus additional private parking. Move-in ready coastal living at its best.
