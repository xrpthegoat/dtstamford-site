About this home

Nestled on one of Stamford&apos;s most desirable streets in one of its premier neighborhoods, this exceptional 4 bedroom, 3 full bath home is located on a quiet dead-end street and backs directly to Sterling Farms Golf Course, offering a private and picturesque setting. Completely rebuilt and expanded from the studs up, the home underwent a comprehensive renovation that transformed nearly every aspect of the property. The redesign included an addition featuring a bedroom over the garage, an expanded primary suite, dormers, a bonus room off the upstairs bedroom, laundry in LL and a welcoming front porch. Inside, the home showcases custom millwork, select red oak hardwood floors, a designer kitchen with custom cabinetry, quartz countertops, and premium Thermador and Viking appliances. Additional highlights include radiant heated floors in the primary bath, a finished walk-out lower level, and finished attic space. Beyond the beautiful finishes, all major systems were replaced, including plumbing, electrical, HVAC, insulation, windows, roofing, and siding, providing the benefits and peace of mind of essentially new construction. The exterior was equally transformed with a custom front porch, bluestone walkway, fieldstone patio, new retaining walls, professional landscaping, and an expanded driveway. A rare opportunity to own a meticulously renovated home in an outstanding Stamford location.
