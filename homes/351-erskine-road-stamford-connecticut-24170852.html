About this home

Rich in history and beautifully reimagined for modern living, this extraordinary North Stamford property began as a c.1780 Saltbox Colonial and has evolved into a gracious country estate. Set on 2.7 bucolic acres, the property artfully blends historic character with contemporary comfort. Expanded in the 1940s and meticulously renovated in 2020, the home honors its architectural heritage while accommodating today&apos;s lifestyle with ease and sophistication. Encompassing approximately 7,000 square feet, the main house showcases period details, elegant entertaining spaces, and warm, inviting interiors. Every renovation was thoughtfully executed to maintain authenticity, while introducing refined finishes and modern amenities. The idyllic grounds provide a private retreat, complete with a tranquil pond, heated gunite pool, and historic Lord &amp; Burnham greenhouse. Mature landscaping, expansive level lawns, and serene surroundings create a timeless country setting. Complementing the main residence is a four-car carriage house with a beautifully updated two-bedroom apartment above, ideal for guests or extended family. The charming one-bedroom guest cottage, affectionately known as &quot;The Dollhouse,&quot; provides a storybook setting for visitors seeking privacy and comfort. A truly exceptional country compound, this one-of-a-kind estate offers a rare combination of history, beauty, and versatility in one of North Stamford&apos;s most coveted locations, less than an hour from New York City.
