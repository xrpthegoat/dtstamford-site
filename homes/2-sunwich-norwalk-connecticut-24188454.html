About this home

Two Sunwich Road is a seemingly rare opportunity to own a coastal classic in one of Rowayton&apos;s most coveted private beach associations. Located steps from a private beach and a short stroll to town, this special property captures the essence of seaside living. Wrapped in classic New England weathered shingles, this home exudes warmth and authenticity which is increasingly difficult to find. A gracious covered porch and white stoned patio serve as extensions of the home&apos;s living space. Sure to become favorite gathering spaces whether enjoying a quiet morning coffee or evening aperitifs they perfectly embody the relaxed rhythm of Rowayton living. Inside the home strikes a perfect balance between classic coastal charm and everyday modern functionality. Thoughtfully updated over the years and maintained, it offers bright comfortable living spaces for entertaining and family life. The updated kitchen is the heart of this home. The home offers three well-appointed bedrooms and one and a half baths, offering flexibility for year-round living, weekend escapes or hosting guests. Warm and inviting living spaces are filled with natural light throughout and retain the character and charm that make this coastal home so special. The renovated third floor provides a wonderful additional space that can easily adapt to your needs; whether a home office, fourth bedroom or a playroom. Perfectly positioned with the Rowayton Beach Association, residents enjoy direct access to the private beach.
