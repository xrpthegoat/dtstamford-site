About this home

A Masterpiece of Colonial Elegance and Resort-Style Living on Dunning Road - Perfectly positioned on a coveted cul-de-sac just moments from town on 2 perfectly flat acres, this stately 5-bedroom, 6.5-bath center hall colonial offers an unparalleled blend of sophisticated design and private resort living. Inside, the formal living room sets a dramatic tone with its stately fireplace and a breathtaking wall of windows framing views of the lushly landscaped backyard oasis, while a separate, sun-drenched solarium provides a tranquil and private retreat for quiet relaxation. The heart of the home is a designer kitchen equipped with professional-grade appliances, a massive center island, a walk-in pantry, and an adjacent breakfast room and separate command center-all seamlessly connecting to a formal dining room and opening through floor-to-ceiling glass French doors to the pool and cabana. For everyday gathering, a separate family room features custom built-ins and its own fireplace, complemented by an expansive finished lower level that boasts a third fireplace and endless recreation space. Outside, the property transforms into a premier entertainment venue, complete with a sparkling pool, a rare 2-room/2-bath cabana, and a private tennis court, making this exceptional estate a true sanctuary for modern living.
