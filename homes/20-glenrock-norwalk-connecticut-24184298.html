About this home

Turnkey 2-bedroom, 2.5-bath condo in the highly desirable Glenrock community in Norwalk&apos;s coveted Silvermine area, where condos rarely come to market. This serene and spacious residence has been extensively renovated, including an updated kitchen and beautifully remodeled bathrooms. The open-concept living and dining area features sliding doors to a balcony overlooking lush gardens, as well as a wood-burning fireplace. The primary suite offers an ensuite bath, bay window, and vaulted ceiling. The second bedroom also features an ensuite bathroom. Additional highlights include an upper-level laundry area, attached one-car garage, and large private storage unit. Set on beautifully maintained, heavily landscaped grounds, Glenrock offers a swimming pool, is pet-friendly, and provides a peaceful retreat with exceptional convenience. Enjoy a short stroll to the Merritt 7 Metro-North station for an easy commute to Grand Central, plus close proximity to Calf Pasture Beach, excellent restaurants, and golf.
