About this home

Nestled in New Canaan&apos;s coveted Silvermine neighborhood, this beautifully maintained 4-bedroom, 2.5-bath Colonial offers the perfect blend of timeless charm and modern living. Set on 1.07 private acres just moments from the Silvermine Arts Center, GreyBarns, and all that New Canaan has to offer, this special property combines a sought-after location with exceptional indoor and outdoor living spaces. The heart of the home is the impressive great room, where the spacious kitchen flows seamlessly into the sun-filled family room with soaring ceilings, creating an inviting space for everyday living and entertaining alike. French doors lead to a newly constructed enclosed deck overlooking the expansive, level backyard, providing a peaceful setting to relax, dine, and gather with family and friends. The thoughtfully designed floor plan also includes a dedicated first-floor office, ideal for today&apos;s work-from-home lifestyle. Upstairs, the spacious primary suite offers a private retreat complete with dual walk-in closets and an en-suite bath. Three additional bedrooms provide ample space for family and guests. The finished lower level adds valuable flexible living space perfect for a playroom, media room, gym, or recreation area. Outside, the recently added bluestone patio and beautifully private backyard create an ideal extension of the home&apos;s living space. A rare opportunity to enjoy classic Colonial living in one of New Canaan&apos;s most desirable neighborhoods.
