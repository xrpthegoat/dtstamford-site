About this home

Highest and Best by 2 pm July 13, 2026. Rarely available, this true 3-bedroom, 3-full-bath residence is located in the highly desirable gated Lansdowne community in Westport. Designed for comfortable everyday living, the home offers a spacious, light-filled layout with generous room sizes and exceptional flexibility. The eat-in kitchen flows into the dining room, while the expansive living room &amp; sunroom feature sliders to a private patio overlooking tranquil, park-like grounds, creating a seamless indoor-outdoor setting. The den, located off the living room, features a fireplace &amp; wet bar, offering a warm space to relax, read, or unwind. The sought-after first-floor primary suite offers a walk-in closet and an en-suite bath with a double vanity, separate shower, and jetted tub. A generously sized second bedroom with ample closet space, an additional full bath, laundry, and abundant storage complete the main level, providing the ease of one-level living. Upstairs offers a loft ideal for a home office or flex space, a private third bedroom with an en-suite bath, and a spacious walk-in storage room. A detached two-car garage is conveniently located directly in front of the residence. Residents enjoy beautifully maintained grounds, an in-ground pool, tennis and pickleball courts, and a prime Westport location just minutes to downtown, beaches, shopping, dining, and the train station. A rare opportunity in one of Westport&apos;s most desirable gated communities.
