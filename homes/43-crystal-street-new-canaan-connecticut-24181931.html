About this home

Move right into this beautifully renovated in-town Colonial, ideally located on one of New Canaan&apos;s most desirable neighborhood streets. Combining timeless charm with modern updates, this exceptional home offers the perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and lifestyle. The heart of the home is the stunning open-concept kitchen and family room, thoughtfully designed for both everyday living and entertaining. Just moments from South School, Saxe Middle School, Waveny Park, the YMCA, and downtown New Canaan, the location offers sidewalks to everything that makes in-town living so special. The flexible floor plan features four bedrooms, including a first-floor bedroom currently used as a den/playroom, two full baths, an upper-level laundry room, and a finished walk-up third floor ideal as a playroom, home office or guest space. The full basement provides abundant storage. Outside, enjoy a fully fenced private yard, expansive deck for outdoor entertaining, storage shed, attached garage, and an expanded driveway with ample parking. Rarely does a home offer this combination of character, thoughtful updates, versatile living space, and an unbeatable walk-to-town location. A true turnkey opportunity in the heart of New Canaan.
