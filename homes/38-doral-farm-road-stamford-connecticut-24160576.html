About this home

Nestled in the coveted Westover neighborhood of Stamford, this beautifully appointed single-family residence in the prestigious Doral Farm association offers an exceptional blend of privacy and low-maintenance living. Enjoy the lifestyle of a standalone home with the added convenience of maintenance-free grounds and outstanding amenities.Spanning approximately 3,132 square feet, this 3-bedroom, 2.5-bath home features thoughtfully designed living spaces ideal for both entertaining and everyday comfort. A gracious living room with fireplace, an expansive family room with built-ins, and a generously scaled dining room create a seamless flow. The sun-filled eat-in kitchen with an inviting sitting area serves as the heart of the home, with sliders opening to a deck overlooking beautifully landscaped grounds.The spacious primary suite is complete with a large walk-in closet and an additional closet, offering abundant storage. The primary bath features dual sinks, a soaking tub, and a walk-in shower. Two additional well-proportioned bedrooms are located on the second floor, along with exceptional storage throughout, ensuring both comfort and functionality. Doral Farm is a vibrant, amenity-rich community offering a heated pool, clubhouse, tennis courts, and pickleball-creating a resort-like setting for an active and social lifestyle.A rare opportunity to experience turnkey, low-maintenance living without compromise in one of Stamford&apos;s most desirable communities.
